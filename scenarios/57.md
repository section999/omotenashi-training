## Turn 1

**Guest:** Excuse me, can I exchange US dollars to yen here at the hotel?
**Guest JP:** すみません、ホテルでドルを円に両替できますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、当ホテルのフロントにて外貨両替を承っております。ただいまご対応いたします。
  - EN: Yes, we offer foreign currency exchange at the front desk. I will assist you right away.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms the service clearly and offers immediate help.
- [1] はい、両替できます。
  - EN: Yes, we can exchange currency.
  - Feedback: Correct but brief. Offer to assist immediately rather than leaving the guest to ask what to do next.
- [0] 両替は銀行でやってください。
  - EN: Please exchange currency at a bank.
  - Feedback: Dismissive if the hotel does offer the service. Always confirm availability before redirecting.
- [0] 近くにATMがありますよ。
  - EN: There's an ATM nearby.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful if the guest specifically asked about hotel exchange. Address the question asked.

## Turn 2

**System:** The guest is pleased the service is available and asks about the rate.

**Guest:** What's the exchange rate today?
**Guest JP:** 本日のレートはどのくらいですか？

### Choices

- [2] 本日のレートは1ドルにつき148円でございます。なお、当ホテルの両替レートには手数料が含まれておりますことをご了承くださいませ。
  - EN: Today's rate is 148 yen per dollar. Please note that our hotel's exchange rate includes a service fee.
  - Feedback: Excellent. Provides the exact rate and proactively discloses the fee, which builds trust.
- [1] 1ドル148円です。
  - EN: It's 148 yen per dollar.
  - Feedback: Correct but should mention the included fee so the guest has full information before deciding.
- [0] レートは毎日変わりますので、なんとも言えません。
  - EN: The rate changes daily so I can't really say.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful. The current rate should always be available to quote.
- [0] 良いレートですよ。
  - EN: It's a good rate.
  - Feedback: Vague and potentially misleading. State the actual rate rather than making a judgment.

## Turn 3

**System:** The guest is satisfied with the rate and wants to proceed with the exchange.

**Guest:** OK, I'd like to exchange $200 please.
**Guest JP:** では、200ドルをお願いします。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。200ドルを本日のレート、1ドル148円で両替いたしますと、29,600円になります。お札でのお渡しでよろしいでしょうか。
  - EN: Certainly. Exchanging $200 at today's rate of 148 yen per dollar comes to 29,600 yen. Would you like that in banknotes?
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms the amount, shows the calculation transparently, and checks the guest's preference — full professional service.
- [1] はい、200ドルですね。29,600円になります。
  - EN: Yes, $200. That comes to 29,600 yen.
  - Feedback: Correct amount and calculation, but does not ask about denomination preference or confirm the rate applied.
- [0] パスポートを持ってきてください。
  - EN: Please bring your passport.
  - Feedback: While ID may be required, this response ignores confirming the transaction first and sounds abrupt.
- [0] 少し時間がかかります。
  - EN: It will take a little time.
  - Feedback: Vague and incomplete. Confirm the transaction details before mentioning any wait.
