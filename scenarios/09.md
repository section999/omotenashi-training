## Turn 1

**Guest:** I think I left my wallet in the restaurant last night.
**Guest JP:** 昨夜レストランに財布を忘れてきたと思います。

### Choices

- [2] ご不便をおかけして申し訳ございません。すぐにレストランへ確認いたします。
  - EN: We are sorry for the inconvenience. I will check with the restaurant immediately.
  - Feedback: Empathetic response and immediate action.
- [1] 確認します。
  - EN: I will check.
  - Feedback: Responsive but lacks empathy.
- [0] 自分で探してみてください。
  - EN: Please try looking for it yourself.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful. Staff should assist fully.
- [0] 昨日のことは分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know about what happened yesterday.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Always investigate.

## Turn 2

**System:** You express concern and tell the guest you will check with the restaurant right away.

**Guest:** It's a brown leather wallet.
**Guest JP:** 茶色の革財布です。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。茶色の革財布でございますね。お名前とお部屋番号をお聞かせいただけますか？
  - EN: Thank you. A brown leather wallet, correct? May I have your name and room number?
  - Feedback: Notes the description and gathers contact info.
- [1] 分かりました。
  - EN: Understood.
  - Feedback: Acknowledged but doesn't collect details.
- [0] たくさんあります。
  - EN: There are many of those.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful and potentially alarming.
- [0] 見つかるか分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know if it will be found.
  - Feedback: Pessimistic. Always give hope and effort.

## Turn 3

**System:** You note the description and collect the guest's contact details to follow up.

**Guest:** I found it myself, actually. Thank you anyway.
**Guest JP:** 実は自分で見つけました。ありがとうございました。

### Choices

- [2] お見つかりになってよかったでございます。ご不便をおかけいたしまして申し訳ございませんでした。何かお困りのことがございましたら、いつでもお声がけくださいませ。
  - EN: I'm so glad you found it. We are sorry for any inconvenience caused. Please feel free to ask us anytime if you need anything.
  - Feedback: Warm and gracious close.
- [1] 良かったです。
  - EN: That's great.
  - Feedback: Simple but acceptable.
- [0] そうですか。
  - EN: Is that so?
  - Feedback: Indifferent. Show genuine relief.
- [0] 最初から自分で探せばよかったです。
  - EN: You should have looked for it yourself from the start.
  - Feedback: Judgmental. Never say this.
