title: Language Barrier
description: Guest does not speak Japanese or English.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** (Speaks only in French, pointing to a map.)
**Guest JP:** （フランス語だけで話し、地図を指さす。）

### Choices

- [2] 少々お待ちください。フランス語を話せるスタッフをお呼びいたします。
  - EN: Please wait a moment. I will call a staff member who speaks French.
  - Feedback: Immediately seeks appropriate help.
- [1] 英語話せますか？
  - EN: Do you speak English?
  - Feedback: Tries to find a common language, acceptable.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't understand.
  - Feedback: Needs to take some action, not just say you don't know.
- [0] 日本語で話してください。
  - EN: Please speak in Japanese.
  - Feedback: Demanding the guest speak your language is unacceptable.

## Turn 2

**System:** You go to find a French-speaking colleague to assist, and return ready to help with the map.

**Guest:** (Shows phone with a Google Maps pin.)
**Guest JP:** （Googleマップのピンを表示したスマートフォンを見せる。）

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。こちらの地図で場所を確認いたします。(Points to the map and nods clearly.)
  - EN: Certainly. I will confirm the location on this map. (Points to the map and nods clearly.)
  - Feedback: Uses non-verbal communication effectively.
- [1] あなたの携帯を見ます。
  - EN: I will look at your phone.
  - Feedback: Takes action but phrasing is informal.
- [0] マップは使えません。
  - EN: I cannot use the map.
  - Feedback: Refusing to help with a simple map is wrong.
- [0] フロントに地図があります。
  - EN: There is a map at the front desk.
  - Feedback: Gives info but doesn't help them understand it.

## Turn 3

**System:** You look at the map pin and use gestures and the hotel map to guide the guest to the destination.

**Guest:** (Smiles and gives a thumbs up after being helped.)
**Guest JP:** （助けてもらった後、笑顔で親指を立てる。）

### Choices

- [2] (Smiles warmly, bows slightly.) どうぞよいご滞在を。
  - EN: (Smiles warmly, bows slightly.) Please enjoy your stay.
  - Feedback: Universal warmth transcends language barriers.
- [1] (Nods and returns to work.)
  - EN: (Nods and returns to work.)
  - Feedback: Acceptable but a smile goes further.
- [0] (Walks away without acknowledgment.)
  - EN: (Walks away without acknowledgment.)
  - Feedback: Ignoring a guest's gratitude is rude in any language.
- [0] 次からは通訳を連れてきてください。
  - EN: Next time, please bring an interpreter.
  - Feedback: Completely inappropriate instruction to a guest.
