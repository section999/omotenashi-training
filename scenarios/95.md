## Turn 1

**System:** A guest stops by the front desk to ask about the hotel fitness center before heading there.

**Guest:** Can I use the gym? What time does it open?
**Guest JP:** ジムは使えますか？何時から利用できますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、フィットネスセンターは6時から23時までご利用いただけます。宿泊のお客様は無料でございます。バスタオルもご用意しておりますので、手ぶらでお越しください。
  - EN: Yes, the fitness centre is open from 6 AM to 11 PM and is complimentary for hotel guests. Towels are provided, so please feel free to come empty-handed.
  - Feedback: Covers hours, cost, and a practical detail about towels — thorough and welcoming.
- [1] はい、ご利用いただけます。営業時間は6時から23時でございます。
  - EN: Yes, you may use it. Hours are 6 AM to 11 PM.
  - Feedback: Correct and direct, though mentioning the complimentary towel service would add value.
- [0] ご利用の際はフロントにてお申し付けください。
  - EN: Please inform the front desk when you wish to use it.
  - Feedback: Vague — give the hours first, then explain any check-in requirements if they exist.
- [0] ジムは混んでいる時間帯がございますのでご注意ください。
  - EN: Please be aware that the gym can get crowded at certain times.
  - Feedback: Unsolicited and off-putting — answer the question asked before raising any caveats.

## Turn 2

**System:** Guest is heading to the fitness centre and asks about the equipment available.

**Guest:** Does it have treadmills and free weights?
**Guest JP:** ランニングマシンとフリーウェイトはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、トレッドミルが4台、フリーウェイトエリアにはダンベルとバーベルをご用意しております。ストレッチ用マットや各種マシンもございます。
  - EN: Yes, we have four treadmills, and the free weights area includes dumbbells and barbells. Stretching mats and various machines are also available.
  - Feedback: Specific and comprehensive — the guest knows exactly what to expect before they arrive.
- [1] はい、ランニングマシンとフリーウェイトをご用意しております。
  - EN: Yes, we have treadmills and free weights.
  - Feedback: Accurate, though specifics like the number of treadmills give a more helpful picture.
- [0] フィットネスセンターの詳細はパンフレットをご覧ください。
  - EN: Please refer to the brochure for details on the fitness centre.
  - Feedback: A brochure is no substitute for a personal answer — describe the equipment directly.
- [0] ランニングマシンは早い時間のほうが空いております。
  - EN: The treadmills are less busy in the early morning.
  - Feedback: Possibly useful, but answer whether the equipment exists first before advising when to go.

## Turn 3

**System:** Guest returns from the gym and mentions that one treadmill is out of order.

**Guest:** One of the treadmills has an out-of-order sign on it — just thought you should know.
**Guest JP:** トレッドミルの1台に故障中のサインが貼ってありましたよ。一応お知らせしておきます。

### Choices

- [2] ご報告いただきありがとうございます。早速メンテナンス担当に連絡いたします。ご不便をおかけして大変申し訳ございませんでした。
  - EN: Thank you very much for letting us know. I will contact our maintenance team right away. We are very sorry for the inconvenience.
  - Feedback: Grateful, action-oriented, and appropriately apologetic — a model response to unsolicited guest feedback.
- [1] はい、承りました。確認いたします。ありがとうございます。
  - EN: Understood. I will look into it. Thank you.
  - Feedback: Polite and fine — expressing a genuine apology for the inconvenience would add warmth.
- [0] そちらのご使用はお控えください。
  - EN: Please refrain from using that machine.
  - Feedback: Redundant — the machine is already marked out of order. Acknowledge the information and act on it.
- [0] 修理の手配は担当部署が行います。
  - EN: The repair will be handled by the relevant department.
  - Feedback: Impersonal and deflecting — own the response and commit to passing the information on yourself.
