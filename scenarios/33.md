## Turn 1

**Guest:** Is the pool open today?

### Choices

- [2] はい、本日もプールは営業しております。営業時間は8時から20時まででございます。
  - EN: Yes, the pool is open today as well. Hours are from 8 AM to 8 PM.
  - Feedback: Confirms open and gives hours proactively.
- [1] はい、開いています。
  - EN: Yes, it's open.
  - Feedback: Confirms but doesn't give hours.
- [0] 確認してみます。
  - EN: I'll try to check.
  - Feedback: Should know this basic facility information.
- [0] 今日は月曜日なので、閉まっています。
  - EN: Today is Monday, so it's closed.
  - Feedback: If incorrect, this causes a bad experience. Verify.
