---
id: 84
title: Extension Request During Full Occupancy
description: Guest wants to extend their stay by two nights but the hotel is fully booked.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Good morning. I'm due to check out today but I'd love to stay for two more nights if possible.
**Guest JP:** おはようございます。本日チェックアウトの予定ですが、もし可能であれば、あと2泊延長したいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] おはようございます。引き続きご滞在をご希望いただきありがとうございます。空室状況をただいま確認いたします。少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Good morning. Thank you so much for wanting to continue your stay with us — it is a pleasure to hear. Let me check our availability right now. Please give me just one moment.
  - Feedback: Responds warmly to the extension request, expresses genuine pleasure that the guest wants to stay longer, and checks immediately — the right start to this conversation.
- [1] ありがとうございます。ただいま確認いたします。
  - EN: Thank you. I will check right away.
  - Feedback: Efficient and polite, but could be warmer — the guest has expressed a desire to stay, which is a compliment worth acknowledging.
- [0] チェックアウトは11時までとなっております。
  - EN: Checkout is at 11 AM.
  - Feedback: Responding to an extension request by citing the checkout time is unhelpful and ignores the guest's question entirely.
- [0] 延長については予約サイトからお手続きください。
  - EN: Please handle extension requests through our booking website.
  - Feedback: Sending a guest currently at the hotel to a booking website for a simple extension request is impersonal and an avoidable inconvenience.

## Turn 2

**System:** You check and find the hotel is fully booked for the next two nights; the guest asks if any cancellations are expected.

**Guest:** Oh, that's a shame. Are you expecting any cancellations that might free up a room?
**Guest JP:** それは残念ですね。キャンセルが出てお部屋が空く可能性はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] 大変申し訳ございませんが、今後2日間はキャンセル待ちのお客様もいらっしゃる状況でございます。ただし、キャンセルが入る可能性はございますので、ウェイティングリストにお名前を登録させていただくことはいかがでしょうか。空きが出次第、すぐにご連絡いたします。
  - EN: I am very sorry — for the next two days we are at full capacity and have guests already on a waiting list. However, cancellations do sometimes occur, and I would be very happy to add your name to our waiting list. If anything becomes available, I will contact you immediately.
  - Feedback: Is honest about the situation including the waitlist, but immediately offers the waitlist as a positive option and commits to prompt notification — manages expectations well while keeping hope alive.
- [1] 申し訳ございませんが、満室でございます。キャンセル待ちに登録することは可能です。
  - EN: I am sorry, but we are fully booked. It is possible to register on the waiting list.
  - Feedback: States the situation and offers the waitlist, but is rather flat — more warmth and commitment to follow-up would improve this.
- [0] キャンセルはほとんど出ないと思います。
  - EN: I do not think there will be many cancellations.
  - Feedback: Discouraging the guest with a pessimistic prediction when you cannot actually know this removes hope needlessly.
- [0] 他のホテルをお探しになることをお勧めします。
  - EN: I suggest you look for another hotel.
  - Feedback: Immediately redirecting a loyal in-house guest to a competitor without first offering the waitlist and alternative assistance is poor service.

## Turn 3

**System:** You offer to add the guest to a waitlist and suggest a nearby sister hotel; the guest asks you to keep them informed and provides their mobile number.

**Guest:** Please put me on the list. And yes, I'd like to know about the sister hotel too. Here's my mobile number.
**Guest JP:** リストに入れてください。そして、姉妹ホテルについても知りたいです。これが携帯番号です。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。ウェイティングリストにご登録いたします。姉妹ホテルについてのご案内資料もお渡しいたします。空きが出次第、こちらの番号にすぐご連絡いたします。引き続き当グループのご施設でご滞在いただけますよう、できる限りのご対応をいたします。本日はご滞在いただきありがとうございました。
  - EN: Thank you very much. I am registering you on the waiting list now. I will also give you information about our sister hotel. As soon as anything becomes available, I will call this number straight away. We will do our best to ensure you can continue to stay within our group. Thank you so much for staying with us today.
  - Feedback: Confirms both actions, commits to personal follow-up, frames the sister hotel as a within-group option to maintain the relationship, and closes with a genuine thank-you — a complete and warm resolution.
- [1] ウェイティングリストに追加いたしました。姉妹ホテルの情報もお渡しします。ご連絡いたします。
  - EN: I have added you to the waiting list. I will give you the sister hotel information. We will be in touch.
  - Feedback: Covers all the practical steps but lacks the personal warmth and specific commitment to call the moment something becomes available.
- [0] 連絡はメールでよろしいですか？
  - EN: Would email contact be all right?
  - Feedback: The guest has just provided their mobile number — asking if email is acceptable instead immediately contradicts the information given and feels dismissive.
- [0] キャンセルが出るかどうかはわかりません。
  - EN: I am not sure whether any cancellations will come up.
  - Feedback: Repeating uncertainty about cancellations when the guest has already accepted the situation and is simply asking to be kept informed is unnecessarily discouraging.
