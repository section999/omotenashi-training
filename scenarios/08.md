## Turn 1

**Guest:** I'd like to book a massage for this afternoon.
**Guest JP:** 今日の午後にマッサージを予約したいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。本日の午後のご予約を確認いたします。ご希望のお時間はございますか？
  - EN: Certainly. I will check availability for this afternoon. Do you have a preferred time?
  - Feedback: Smooth booking start with follow-up question.
- [1] はい、時間は何時ですか？
  - EN: Yes, what time would you like?
  - Feedback: Asks the right question but less polished.
- [0] 今日は予約が取れないかもしれません。
  - EN: We may not be able to take a booking for today.
  - Feedback: Check first before assuming no availability.
- [0] スパは3階です。
  - EN: The spa is on the third floor.
  - Feedback: Irrelevant to the booking request.

## Turn 2

**System:** You begin checking availability for spa appointments this afternoon.

**Guest:** Around 3 PM. What treatments do you have?
**Guest JP:** 15時頃です。どんなトリートメントがありますか？

### Choices

- [2] 3時でございますね。アロマオイルマッサージや温石療法など各種ご用意しております。パンフレットもございます。
  - EN: That would be 3 o'clock. We offer various treatments including aromatherapy oil massage and hot stone therapy. We also have a brochure.
  - Feedback: Confirmed time and gave a helpful overview.
- [1] マッサージがあります。
  - EN: We have massages.
  - Feedback: Too minimal given the direct question.
- [0] ウェブサイトを見てください。
  - EN: Please check our website.
  - Feedback: Always explain in person.
- [0] 全部同じです。
  - EN: They're all the same.
  - Feedback: Inaccurate and dismissive.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm the 3 PM slot is available and describe the spa's treatment menu.

**Guest:** I'll go with the aromatherapy. Can I pay at the spa?
**Guest JP:** アロマテラピーにします。スパで支払えますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。スパにて、またはチェックアウト時にお部屋付けも可能でございます。
  - EN: Of course. You may pay at the spa, or we can add it to your room bill at check-out.
  - Feedback: Provides clear payment options.
- [1] はい、払えます。
  - EN: Yes, you can pay.
  - Feedback: Correct but doesn't explain options.
- [0] 先払いです。
  - EN: Payment is required in advance.
  - Feedback: Abrupt and possibly incorrect. Check policy.
- [0] フロントで払ってください。
  - EN: Please pay at the front desk.
  - Feedback: Doesn't match what the guest asked.
