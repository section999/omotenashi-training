## Turn 1

**Guest:** We want to avoid tourist traps. Any hidden gems nearby?
**Guest JP:** 観光客向けの場所は避けたいです。近くに穴場はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] 地元の方に人気の小さな居酒屋や、朝市がございます。観光客があまり来ない穴場をいくつかご紹介できますよ。
  - EN: There are small izakayas popular with locals and a morning market. I can introduce you to a few hidden spots that tourists rarely visit.
  - Feedback: Authentic, insider-knowledge response.
- [1] 人気のスポットをご案内します。
  - EN: I will show you some popular spots.
  - Feedback: Misses the 'avoid tourist traps' request.
- [0] 観光地はたくさんあります。
  - EN: There are many tourist spots.
  - Feedback: Completely misses the point of the question.
- [0] ガイドブックが詳しいです。
  - EN: A guidebook would have more details.
  - Feedback: Concierge's value is local knowledge beyond guidebooks.
