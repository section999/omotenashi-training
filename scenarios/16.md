id: 16
title: Housekeeping Request
description: Guest calls to request room cleaning.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Could you send someone to clean my room?
**Guest JP:** お部屋の掃除に来ていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。ただいま係の者を手配いたします。お部屋に入る前にノックいたします。
  - EN: Certainly. I will send someone right away. They will knock before entering your room.
  - Feedback: Prompt service with reassurance about procedure.
- [1] はい、掃除の人を送ります。
  - EN: Yes, I will send a housekeeper.
  - Feedback: Correct but informal.
- [0] チェックアウト後に掃除します。
  - EN: We will clean it after check-out.
  - Feedback: Incorrect. Mid-stay cleaning is standard.
- [0] 今は無理です。
  - EN: That's not possible right now.
  - Feedback: Refuses without checking. Unacceptable.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm a housekeeper will be sent and assure the guest they will knock first.

**Guest:** Could they also bring extra towels?
**Guest JP:** タオルも追加で持ってきていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。タオルも追加でお持ちします。他にご入り用なものはございますか？
  - EN: Certainly. I will also bring extra towels. Is there anything else you need?
  - Feedback: Confirms and proactively asks for more needs.
- [1] はい、タオルも持ってきます。
  - EN: Yes, I will bring towels as well.
  - Feedback: Correct but doesn't probe for more requests.
- [0] タオルは部屋にあります。
  - EN: There are towels in the room.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Guest wants extras.
- [0] タオルだけですか？
  - EN: Just towels?
  - Feedback: Correct intent but phrasing sounds reluctant.

## Turn 3

**System:** You add the extra towels to the request and ask if there is anything else needed.

**Guest:** That's all. How long will it take?
**Guest JP:** 以上です。どのくらいかかりますか？

### Choices

- [2] 15分から20分以内にお伺いいたします。
  - EN: We will be there within 15 to 20 minutes.
  - Feedback: Specific and reassuring estimate.
- [1] すぐ行きます。
  - EN: We'll be right there.
  - Feedback: Positive but vague.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Give an estimate, even if approximate.
- [0] 忙しいので時間がかかります。
  - EN: We're busy so it will take some time.
  - Feedback: Don't burden guests with staffing issues.
