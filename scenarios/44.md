## Turn 1

**Guest:** We heard there's a typhoon coming. Is it serious?
**Guest JP:** 台風が来ると聞きました。深刻ですか？

### Choices

- [2] ご心配のことと存じます。台風情報を随時確認しております。現在の状況をご説明いたします。
  - EN: I understand you are concerned. We are continuously monitoring typhoon updates. Allow me to explain the current situation.
  - Feedback: Reassures and commits to keeping guests informed.
- [0] 大丈夫だと思います。
  - EN: I think it'll be fine.
  - Feedback: Don't minimize typhoon warnings.
- [0] ニュースを見てください。
  - EN: Please watch the news.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Hotel should brief guests directly.
- [1] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Honest but not reassuring. Provide what you know.

## Turn 2

**System:** You reassure the guests and explain the current typhoon situation as monitored by the hotel.

**Guest:** Should we cancel our plans for tomorrow?
**Guest JP:** 明日の予定をキャンセルすべきでしょうか？

### Choices

- [2] 安全のため、明日の外出は控えることをお勧めいたします。最新情報が入り次第、ご案内いたします。
  - EN: For your safety, we recommend refraining from going out tomorrow. We will keep you updated as soon as we receive new information.
  - Feedback: Safety-first recommendation with update promise.
- [0] たぶん大丈夫です。
  - EN: It will probably be fine.
  - Feedback: Never underestimate typhoon danger.
- [0] 自己判断でお願いします。
  - EN: Please use your own judgment.
  - Feedback: Staff should guide guests in emergency situations.
- [1] 予定はキャンセルしてください。
  - EN: Please cancel your plans.
  - Feedback: Advises correctly but no explanation or update.

## Turn 3

**System:** You advise the guests to avoid going out tomorrow for safety and promise to keep them updated.

**Guest:** What if we need to extend our stay?
**Guest JP:** 滞在を延長する必要が出た場合はどうなりますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。ご安全が最優先です。滞在延長は可能な限り対応いたします。
  - EN: Of course. Your safety is our top priority. We will accommodate an extended stay as much as possible.
  - Feedback: Puts safety first and shows flexibility.
- [1] 空きがあれば大丈夫です。
  - EN: It should be fine if there's availability.
  - Feedback: Conditional answer is acceptable but could be warmer.
- [0] それは難しいです。
  - EN: That is difficult.
  - Feedback: During a typhoon, hotels should prioritize guest safety.
- [0] 早めに帰ってください。
  - EN: Please go home as soon as possible.
  - Feedback: Dangerous advice if typhoon is already approaching.
