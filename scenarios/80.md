title: Wheelchair-Accessible Room Request
description: Guest calls to reserve a wheelchair-accessible room and asks detailed questions about the facilities.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, I need to book a wheelchair-accessible room. Can you tell me if the accessible room has a roll-in shower and grab bars?
**Guest JP:** もしもし、車椅子対応の部屋を予約したいのですが。バリアフリーの部屋にロールインシャワーと手すりはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] ご連絡いただきありがとうございます。バリアフリーのお部屋についてご案内いたします。当ホテルのアクセシブルルームにはロールインシャワー、シャワーチェア、洗面台と浴室に手すりを完備しております。ご不明な点がございましたら、何でもお聞かせください。
  - EN: Thank you for calling. I am happy to tell you about our accessible room. Our wheelchair-accessible room is equipped with a roll-in shower, shower chair, and grab bars at both the sink and bathroom walls. Please feel free to ask me anything else you would like to know.
  - Feedback: Provides a specific and thorough answer to the exact questions asked, and warmly invites further questions — the right level of detail for a guest who needs to plan carefully.
- [1] はい、アクセシブルルームにはロールインシャワーと手すりがございます。
  - EN: Yes, the accessible room has a roll-in shower and grab bars.
  - Feedback: Answers the question correctly but provides no additional detail that might be helpful and does not invite further questions.
- [0] バリアフリーのお部屋については直接ご来館いただいてご確認ください。
  - EN: For details about accessible rooms, please come to the hotel to check in person.
  - Feedback: Asking a guest who uses a wheelchair to visit the hotel in person to check accessibility is wholly impractical and dismissive.
- [0] ウェブサイトにアクセシビリティのページがございます。
  - EN: We have an accessibility page on our website.
  - Feedback: Redirecting a guest to the website rather than answering their question directly is unhelpful — the guest has specifically chosen to call for personal assistance.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the room has full accessible facilities; the guest now asks about wheelchair access from the main entrance to the restaurant.

**Guest:** Great. And is there a smooth wheelchair route from the main entrance all the way to the restaurant?
**Guest JP:** よかったです。メインエントランスからレストランまでの車椅子でのルートはスムーズですか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。メインエントランスには段差がなく、自動ドアを完備しております。エントランスから直接アクセスできる広幅エレベーターがあり、レストランのあるフロアまでご案内できます。レストラン内は全てフラットな床で、車椅子対応テーブルもご用意しております。ご不安な点があればスタッフが随時ご案内いたします。
  - EN: Certainly. The main entrance has no steps and features automatic doors. There is a wide-access elevator directly from the entrance that goes to the restaurant floor. The restaurant itself is entirely flat with no steps, and we have wheelchair-accessible tables. Our staff are always happy to assist you at any point along the way.
  - Feedback: Describes the full route with specific details at each stage, mentions the table arrangements inside the restaurant, and offers staff assistance — thorough and reassuring for a guest who needs to plan their movements carefully.
- [1] はい、メインエントランスからレストランまで段差なくお越しいただけます。エレベーターもございます。
  - EN: Yes, you can get from the main entrance to the restaurant without any steps. There is also an elevator.
  - Feedback: Confirms the route is accessible, but lacks the specific detail about door types, elevator width, and restaurant interior that help the guest plan confidently.
- [0] 基本的にバリアフリーですが、一部段差がある場合もあります。
  - EN: It is basically barrier-free, but there may be some steps in places.
  - Feedback: Vague and potentially alarming — a guest who uses a wheelchair cannot plan around "may be some steps." Specific information is essential.
- [0] スタッフに当日ご案内いただけますのでご心配なく。
  - EN: A staff member will be able to assist you on the day, so please do not worry.
  - Feedback: Deferring all details to the day of arrival does not give the guest the advance information they need to feel confident about their stay.

## Turn 3

**System:** You describe the accessible route in detail; the guest now asks if a wheelchair is available to borrow during the stay.

**Guest:** That's reassuring, thank you. One more thing — do you have a wheelchair I could borrow during my stay?
**Guest JP:** それは安心しました、ありがとうございます。もう一つ伺いたいのですが、滞在中に借りられる車椅子はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、貸し出し用の車椅子を無料でご用意しております。フロントにてお申し付けいただければ、いつでもお部屋までお持ちいたします。ご滞在中はいつでもご利用いただけますので、ご遠慮なくお申し付けくださいませ。ご予約の際にご希望をお伝えいただければ、チェックイン時にご用意いたします。
  - EN: Yes, we have complimentary wheelchairs available to borrow. Just let us know at the front desk and we will bring one to your room at any time. You are welcome to use it throughout your stay — please do not hesitate to ask. If you let us know when you make your reservation, we will have one ready for you at check-in.
  - Feedback: Confirms availability, explains how to access it, offers proactive preparation at check-in, and uses a warm and inclusive tone throughout — exemplary service for a guest with specific mobility needs.
- [1] はい、無料でご貸し出しできます。フロントにてお申し出ください。
  - EN: Yes, we can lend one free of charge. Please ask at the front desk.
  - Feedback: Confirms availability and the process, but does not offer to have it ready on arrival or provide the warmth and personal attention the guest deserves.
- [0] 数に限りがございますので、当日確認が必要です。
  - EN: We have a limited number, so you would need to check on the day.
  - Feedback: Leaving a wheelchair user uncertain about whether they will have access to a wheelchair on arrival is poor planning — confirm and reserve it in advance.
- [0] レンタルについては近くのショップもご利用いただけます。
  - EN: There is a nearby rental shop you could also use.
  - Feedback: Suggesting the guest source their own wheelchair from an external shop when the hotel has its own is unhelpful and implies the hotel is unwilling to assist.
