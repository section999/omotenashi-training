title: Room Safe
description: Guest has forgotten their room safe code.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** I've forgotten the code to my room safe and I have my passport inside.
**Guest JP:** 部屋の金庫のコードを忘れてしまい、中にパスポートが入っています。

### Choices

- [2] ご不便をおかけして申し訳ございません。セキュリティ担当がすぐにお部屋に伺います。
  - EN: We apologize for the inconvenience. A security staff member will come to your room right away.
  - Feedback: Apologizes and sends the right person immediately.
- [1] 担当者を呼びます。
  - EN: I will call the person in charge.
  - Feedback: Correct but no apology.
- [0] コードを何か試してみてください。
  - EN: Please try some codes.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful and potentially risky advice.
- [0] 金庫は私たちの管轄外です。
  - EN: The safe is outside our jurisdiction.
  - Feedback: False. In-room safes are hotel responsibility.

## Turn 2

**System:** You apologize and arrange for a security staff member to go to the guest's room.

**Guest:** How long will it take?
**Guest JP:** どのくらいかかりますか？

### Choices

- [2] 10分以内にお伺いいたします。お部屋でお待ちいただけますか？
  - EN: We will be there within 10 minutes. Could you please wait in your room?
  - Feedback: Specific time estimate with a polite request.
- [1] すぐ行きます。
  - EN: We'll be right there.
  - Feedback: Positive but vague.
- [0] 混んでいるので時間がかかります。
  - EN: It will take some time because we are busy.
  - Feedback: Staffing issues are not the guest's problem.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Always provide an estimated time.

## Turn 3

**System:** You give a 10-minute estimate and ask the guest to wait in the room.

**Guest:** That's a relief. My flight is in 3 hours.
**Guest JP:** 安心しました。フライトまで3時間しかないので。

### Choices

- [2] ご安心ください。お時間に間に合うよう最優先で対応いたします。
  - EN: Please don't worry. We will handle this as our top priority to make sure you're ready in time.
  - Feedback: Acknowledges urgency and commits to priority service.
- [1] 大丈夫です。
  - EN: It's fine.
  - Feedback: Reassuring but lacks urgency acknowledgment.
- [0] 急いでいるんですね。
  - EN: You're in a hurry, aren't you.
  - Feedback: States the obvious without committing to action.
- [0] フライトは変更できますか？
  - EN: Can you change your flight?
  - Feedback: Off-topic. Focus on solving the immediate issue.
