## Turn 1

**Guest:** What should I see in the city today?
**Guest JP:** 今日は市内でどこを見たらいいですか？

### Choices

- [2] 本日でしたら、旧市街の散策や伝統工芸の体験などをお勧めいたします。地図もご用意できますよ。
  - EN: For today, I recommend exploring the old town or experiencing traditional crafts. I can also prepare a map for you.
  - Feedback: Enthusiastic, specific recommendation with a proactive offer.
- [1] 観光地がいくつかあります。
  - EN: There are several sightseeing spots.
  - Feedback: True but too vague to be helpful.
- [0] ガイドブックをどうぞ。
  - EN: Please have a guidebook.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Guests expect personal advice.
- [0] 特にないです。
  - EN: There's nothing in particular.
  - Feedback: Unacceptable from a concierge.

## Turn 2

**System:** You offer specific sightseeing recommendations and the guest is interested in visiting.

**Guest:** How do I get there?
**Guest JP:** そこへはどうやって行けばいいですか？

### Choices

- [2] 地下鉄で2駅でございます。地図とチケットの購入方法をご案内いたします。
  - EN: It's two stops by subway. I will show you the map and how to purchase tickets.
  - Feedback: Complete, proactive assistance.
- [1] 電車で行けます。
  - EN: You can get there by train.
  - Feedback: Minimal but accurate.
- [0] タクシーで行ってください。
  - EN: Please take a taxi.
  - Feedback: Doesn't answer the actual question.
- [0] 自分で調べてください。
  - EN: Please look it up yourself.
  - Feedback: Completely inappropriate for a concierge.

## Turn 3

**System:** You explain the subway route and show the guest the map with ticket information.

**Guest:** Can you book tickets for me?
**Guest JP:** チケットを予約していただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。ご希望の日時をお聞かせいただければ、すぐに手配いたします。
  - EN: Of course. If you let me know your preferred date and time, I will arrange it right away.
  - Feedback: Excellent. Proactively offers to handle everything.
- [1] はい、予約できます。
  - EN: Yes, I can make a booking.
  - Feedback: Willing but doesn't ask for details.
- [0] 自分で予約してください。
  - EN: Please make the reservation yourself.
  - Feedback: Concierge's job is to assist with exactly this.
- [0] 難しいかもしれません。
  - EN: It might be difficult.
  - Feedback: Negative phrasing without a reason.
