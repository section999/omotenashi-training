## Turn 1

**Guest:** I'd like to order room service.
**Guest JP:** ルームサービスをお願いしたいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。ルームサービスでございますね。ご注文をお伺いいたします。
  - EN: Certainly. Room service, of course. I will take your order now.
  - Feedback: Natural and polished — confirms the request with でございますね and smoothly invites the order.
- [1] はい、ルームサービスですね。何をご注文なさいますか？
  - EN: Yes, room service. What would you like to order?
  - Feedback: Clear and polite, but lacks the elegance of かしこまりました.
- [0] メニューはお部屋にございます。
  - EN: The menu is in your room.
  - Feedback: Redirecting the guest without taking the order is unhelpful.
- [0] 少々お待ちください。
  - EN: Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Putting the guest on hold immediately without acknowledgment feels dismissive.

## Turn 2

**System:** You acknowledge the request and invite the guest to place their order.

**Guest:** Can I get a club sandwich and some green tea?
**Guest JP:** クラブサンドイッチと緑茶をいただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] クラブサンドイッチと緑茶でございますね。ただいまご用意いたします。30分ほどでお届けいたします。
  - EN: A club sandwich and green tea — is that correct? We will prepare it now and deliver in about 30 minutes.
  - Feedback: Ideal — repeats the order to confirm, then gives a clear delivery time.
- [1] はい、承りました。少々お時間をいただきます。
  - EN: Yes, I have noted your order. It will take a little while.
  - Feedback: Polite acceptance, but no delivery estimate and no order confirmation.
- [0] 緑茶はご用意が難しい場合がございます。
  - EN: Green tea may be difficult to prepare.
  - Feedback: Vague and uncertain — check first, then give a clear answer or offer an alternative.
- [0] お時間がかかりますが、よろしいですか？
  - EN: It will take some time — is that all right?
  - Feedback: Asking permission for the wait sounds apologetic before anything has gone wrong.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm the order and give the delivery estimate.

**Guest:** How long will it take?
**Guest JP:** どのくらいかかりますか？

### Choices

- [2] おおよそ30分でお届けできる予定でございます。お急ぎでしたら、お申し付けくださいませ。
  - EN: We expect to deliver in approximately 30 minutes. Please do let us know if you are in a hurry.
  - Feedback: Precise, warm, and proactive — くださいませ adds an extra layer of hospitality.
- [1] 30分ほどかと存じます。
  - EN: I believe it will be around 30 minutes.
  - Feedback: Appropriately humble with 存じます, but lacks the offer to expedite.
- [0] はっきりとは申し上げられません。
  - EN: I cannot say for certain.
  - Feedback: Never leave a guest without a time estimate — check and give your best answer.
- [0] なるべく早くお届けします。
  - EN: We will deliver as soon as possible.
  - Feedback: Sounds rushed and vague. A specific time is far more reassuring.
