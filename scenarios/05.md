## Turn 1

**Guest:** How do I get to the temple from here?
**Guest JP:** ここからお寺へはどうやって行けばいいですか？

### Choices

- [2] こちらを出て右に曲がり、まっすぐ進むと5分ほどでございます。地図をお持ちしましょうか？
  - EN: Turn right when you exit here, then go straight and it's about a 5-minute walk. Shall I bring you a map?
  - Feedback: Clear directions with an offer to provide a map.
- [1] 右に行って、まっすぐです。
  - EN: Turn right and go straight.
  - Feedback: Correct but lacks politeness and detail.
- [0] 遠いです。
  - EN: It's far.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful. Always give directions or call a taxi.
- [0] スマホで調べてください。
  - EN: Please look it up on your smartphone.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Staff should assist personally.

## Turn 2

**System:** You give walking directions and offer a map to assist the guest.

**Guest:** Is it walkable?
**Guest JP:** 歩いて行けますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、徒歩で約5分でございます。天気も良いので、お散歩日和でございますね。
  - EN: Yes, it's about a 5-minute walk. The weather is nice, so it's a perfect day for a stroll.
  - Feedback: Practical answer with a friendly touch.
- [1] はい、歩いて行けます。
  - EN: Yes, you can walk there.
  - Feedback: Correct but brief.
- [0] 遠すぎます。
  - EN: It's too far.
  - Feedback: Incorrect if it's a short walk. Know your area.
- [0] タクシーがいいです。
  - EN: A taxi would be better.
  - Feedback: Not asked for and possibly unnecessary.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm the walk is short and the guest seems pleased with the route.

**Guest:** Thank you! And is there a good place to eat nearby?
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます！近くに美味しいお店はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、境内の近くに老舗のそば屋がございます。地元でも人気でございます。
  - EN: Yes, there is a long-established soba restaurant near the temple grounds. It is popular with locals.
  - Feedback: Helpful local recommendation.
- [1] 近くにレストランがあります。
  - EN: There are restaurants nearby.
  - Feedback: Vague but directionally correct.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Never say you don't know without offering to find out.
- [0] ホテルのレストランが一番です。
  - EN: The hotel restaurant is the best.
  - Feedback: Too self-promotional. Put the guest's needs first.
