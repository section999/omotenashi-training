id: 52
title: Lost Key Card
description: A guest reports that they have lost their room key card.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Excuse me, I think I've lost my room key card.
**Guest JP:** すみません、ルームキーをなくしてしまったようです。

### Choices

- [2] これは大変でございましたね。すぐに対応いたします。お部屋番号とお名前をお伺いできますでしょうか。
  - EN: That must have been worrying for you. We will assist you right away. May I ask your room number and name?
  - Feedback: Perfect. Empathizes with the guest, acts immediately, and requests verification politely.
- [1] わかりました。お名前と部屋番号を教えてください。
  - EN: Understood. Please tell me your name and room number.
  - Feedback: Functional but lacks empathy and is slightly too direct without polite framing.
- [0] それは困りましたね。ご自分でよく探してみてください。
  - EN: That's a problem. Please try looking for it yourself carefully.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful and dismissive. Take action immediately for security reasons.
- [0] またなくすんですか？
  - EN: You lost it again?
  - Feedback: Rude and makes assumptions. Never reproach a guest.

## Turn 2

**System:** The guest approaches the front desk and you need to verify their identity before issuing a new card.

**Guest:** My name is Tanaka Kenji, room 814.
**Guest JP:** 田中健二と申します。部屋は814号室です。

### Choices

- [2] 田中様、814号室でございますね。ご本人確認のため、パスポートまたはご身分証明書をご提示いただけますでしょうか。
  - EN: Mr. Tanaka, room 814, correct? For identity verification purposes, may I ask you to show your passport or identification?
  - Feedback: Excellent. Confirms the information, explains why ID is needed, and asks politely.
- [1] はい、確認します。身分証明書を見せてもらえますか？
  - EN: Yes, I'll check. Could you show me your ID?
  - Feedback: Reasonable but the confirmation of the name and room number is missing, which is part of the security protocol.
- [0] 大丈夫です、すぐ新しいカードを作ります。
  - EN: That's fine, I'll make a new card right away.
  - Feedback: Skipping identity verification is a serious security risk.
- [0] 本当に田中さんですか？
  - EN: Are you really Mr. Tanaka?
  - Feedback: Sounds accusatory. Frame the ID request as a security procedure, not a challenge.

## Turn 3

**System:** You verify the guest's identity and issue a replacement key card.

**Guest:** Thank you. Is there anything I should be careful about?
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。何か気をつけることはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] こちらが新しいカードキーでございます。万が一、古いカードが見つかりましても、セキュリティのため使用できないようにしております。お気をつけてお持ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Here is your new key card. In the unlikely event you find the old card, please note that it has been deactivated for security purposes. Please take good care of this one.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Delivers the card, explains the deactivation of the old one, and advises care — all key security points delivered warmly.
- [1] 新しいカードです。古いカードはもう使えません。
  - EN: Here is the new card. The old card no longer works.
  - Feedback: Correct information but lacks warmth and doesn't advise the guest to be careful going forward.
- [0] なくさないように気をつけてください。
  - EN: Please be careful not to lose it.
  - Feedback: Comes across as scolding the guest. Deliver the same message in a warmer way.
- [0] 次からは気をつけてくださいね。
  - EN: Please be more careful next time.
  - Feedback: Condescending tone. Focus on helping, not lecturing.
