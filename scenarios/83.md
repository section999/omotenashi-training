---
id: 83
title: Renovation Noise Complaint
description: Guest complains at the front desk that construction noise from a hotel renovation woke them up at 7:30 AM.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** (Arrives at front desk, looking tired and irritated) Excuse me. I was woken up at 7:30 this morning by loud construction noise. This is not acceptable.
**Guest JP:** （フロントに来て、疲れた様子でいらいらしながら）すみません。今朝7時半に大きな工事の音で目が覚めました。これは受け入れられません。

### Choices

- [2] わざわざお越しいただきありがとうございます。ご不便をおかけし、誠に申し訳ございませんでした。ゆっくりお休みになれなかったこと、大変おつらかったと存じます。ただいま状況を確認し、できる限りの対応をいたします。お部屋番号をお聞かせいただけますでしょうか。
  - EN: Thank you for coming to speak to us. I sincerely apologise for the disturbance this morning. I can only imagine how unpleasant it must have been to be woken up like that. I will look into the situation right away and do everything I can. May I ask your room number?
  - Feedback: Apologises sincerely and with genuine empathy for the impact on the guest's rest, then moves to gather information — this shows the guest they are heard before jumping to solutions.
- [1] 大変申し訳ございません。工事の件でご迷惑をおかけしました。お部屋番号をお聞かせください。
  - EN: I am very sorry. We caused you trouble with the construction. Could I have your room number?
  - Feedback: Apologises and gathers information, but the acknowledgment of the guest's disrupted sleep is too brief for the level of frustration they are showing.
- [0] 工事は事前にお知らせしておりました。
  - EN: We did give prior notice of the construction.
  - Feedback: Citing the notice as a response to a tired and frustrated guest without first apologising will escalate the complaint immediately.
- [0] 工事は朝7時から許可されております。
  - EN: Construction is permitted from 7 AM.
  - Feedback: Defending the timing on regulatory grounds completely ignores the guest's experience — this approach is dismissive and shows no hospitality.

## Turn 2

**System:** You apologise sincerely and realise the renovation notice was sent but the guest may not have seen it; the guest says they were not informed when they booked.

**Guest:** I was never told about any construction when I made my booking. If I had known, I would have stayed somewhere else.
**Guest JP:** 予約した時に工事のことは一切知らされていませんでした。もし知っていたら、他のところに泊まっていたと思います。

### Choices

- [2] おっしゃる通りでございます。ご予約時に十分な情報をお伝えできなかったとすれば、深くお詫び申し上げます。工事のご案内をお送りしておりましたが、お客様に届いていなかった可能性があり、その点について誠に申し訳ございません。今後のご滞在でこのようなことが繰り返されないよう、早急に対策を取りたいと存じます。お部屋を静かな棟に移動させていただくことはいかがでしょうか。
  - EN: You are absolutely right, and I sincerely apologise if we did not communicate this adequately at the time of your booking. We did send a notice about the renovation, but if it did not reach you clearly, that is our responsibility and I am deeply sorry. I would like to offer you a room move to a quieter part of the hotel — would that be acceptable?
  - Feedback: Accepts responsibility for the communication failure rather than deflecting to the notification that was sent, apologises for the outcome not just the process, and immediately offers a concrete remedy — strong service recovery.
- [1] ご案内が届かなかったことをお詫びします。静かな部屋へのご移動をご提案できます。
  - EN: I apologise that the notification did not reach you. We can offer a move to a quieter room.
  - Feedback: Apologises and offers the key remedy, but does not fully accept responsibility for the booking information gap.
- [0] ご案内はメールでお送りしておりますので、ご確認いただけなかったかと思います。
  - EN: We sent the notice by email, so I believe you may not have checked it.
  - Feedback: Implying the guest failed to check their email shifts responsibility onto them — this will inflame an already frustrated guest.
- [0] 工事は今月末で終わる予定です。
  - EN: The construction is expected to finish at the end of the month.
  - Feedback: Mentioning the end date does nothing to resolve today's complaint and does not address what the hotel will do for the guest right now.

## Turn 3

**System:** You offer a room move to a quieter wing and a complimentary breakfast; the guest appreciates the offer and accepts.

**Guest:** All right. A quieter room and breakfast would help. Thank you for sorting this out.
**Guest JP:** わかりました。静かな部屋と朝食があれば助かります。解決してくれてありがとうございます。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。ただいま静かな棟のお部屋のご用意をいたします。ベルスタッフがお荷物のお運びをお手伝いいたします。また、明日以降のご朝食を無料でお楽しみいただけます。ご不便をおかけしたことを改めて深くお詫び申し上げます。どうか残りのご滞在が快適なものになりますよう、精一杯お手伝いいたします。
  - EN: Thank you so much for your understanding. I will arrange your new room in the quieter wing right now. Our bellhop will assist with moving your belongings. You will also enjoy complimentary breakfast from tomorrow morning. Once again, I sincerely apologise for the disruption. We will do everything we can to ensure the rest of your stay is a comfortable one.
  - Feedback: Confirms all arranged compensations, handles logistics, issues a final sincere apology, and commits to the guest's ongoing comfort — a gracious and complete resolution.
- [1] かしこまりました。お部屋を変更し、朝食を無料にいたします。お荷物はベルスタッフがお運びします。
  - EN: Certainly. I will change your room and arrange complimentary breakfast. The bellhop will move your luggage.
  - Feedback: All three elements are confirmed clearly, but lacks a final apology and warm commitment to the rest of the stay.
- [0] では、フロントで新しい鍵をお受け取りください。朝食券もお渡しします。
  - EN: Please collect your new key at the front desk. I will also give you a breakfast voucher.
  - Feedback: Handling this efficiently but transactionally without any warmth or apology leaves the guest feeling processed rather than cared for.
- [0] 朝食は一日分のみのご提供となります。
  - EN: The complimentary breakfast is for one day only.
  - Feedback: Leading with a restriction on the offered gesture immediately after the guest has agreed sours the resolution unnecessarily.
