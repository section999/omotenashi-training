---
id: 2
title: Complaint Handling
description: A guest is unhappy with their room.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** The room is too noisy. I can hear everything from the street.
**Guest JP:** 部屋がとても騒がしくて、通りの音が全部聞こえます。

### Choices

- [2] 大変ご不便をおかけして、申し訳ございません。すぐに対応いたします。
  - EN: We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. We will address this immediately.
  - Feedback: Sincere apology and immediate action promised.
- [1] すみません、うるさかったですか。
  - EN: I'm sorry, was it noisy?
  - Feedback: Acceptable but lacks urgency and solution.
- [0] それは仕方がないです。
  - EN: There's nothing we can do about that.
  - Feedback: Never dismiss a guest's complaint.
- [0] 窓を閉めてください。
  - EN: Please close the window.
  - Feedback: Blaming the guest is inappropriate.

## Turn 2

**System:** You apologize and offer to look into solutions for the noise issue.

**Guest:** Can you move me to a quieter room?
**Guest JP:** もっと静かなお部屋に移っていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。空き状況を確認いたしますので、少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Certainly. I will check availability, so please wait just a moment.
  - Feedback: Perfect. かしこまりました shows full acceptance.
- [1] 確認してみます。
  - EN: I'll try to check.
  - Feedback: Acceptable but add more politeness.
- [0] 今は難しいです。
  - EN: It's difficult right now.
  - Feedback: Don't refuse without checking.
- [0] それは無理かもしれません。
  - EN: That may not be possible.
  - Feedback: Negative language discourages the guest.

## Turn 3

**System:** You check availability and confirm a quieter room is ready for the guest.

**Guest:** Thank you. I hope the new room is better.
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。新しいお部屋が良いといいですね。

### Choices

- [2] ご不便をおかけして、誠に申し訳ございませんでした。新しいお部屋でゆっくりとお休みいただければ幸いです。
  - EN: We are truly sorry for the inconvenience. We hope you enjoy a restful stay in your new room.
  - Feedback: Excellent follow-up apology and warm wishes.
- [1] 良いお部屋になると思います。
  - EN: I think the new room will be better.
  - Feedback: Polite but should include an apology.
- [0] 大丈夫です。
  - EN: It's fine.
  - Feedback: Too casual and doesn't acknowledge inconvenience.
- [0] 次は予約の時に言ってください。
  - EN: Please mention it at the time of booking next time.
  - Feedback: Inappropriate. Don't shift blame to the guest.
