## Turn 1

**Guest:** Can you recommend a good sushi restaurant nearby?
**Guest JP:** 近くにいいお寿司屋さんを紹介していただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、徒歩5分の「鮨 匠」をお勧めいたします。地元でも人気の名店でございます。ご予約いたしましょうか？
  - EN: Yes, I recommend 'Sushi Takumi,' just 5 minutes on foot. It is a renowned establishment popular with locals. Shall I make a reservation?
  - Feedback: Specific, confident recommendation with booking offer.
- [1] 寿司屋はいくつかあります。
  - EN: There are several sushi restaurants.
  - Feedback: Vague. Name a specific place.
- [0] 寿司はどこも同じです。
  - EN: Sushi is the same everywhere.
  - Feedback: Unprofessional and unhelpful opinion.
- [0] ガイドブックを見てください。
  - EN: Please look in the guidebook.
  - Feedback: Concierge should give personal recommendations.

## Turn 2

**System:** You recommend a well-known local sushi restaurant and offer to make a reservation.

**Guest:** Yes, please book for two at 7 PM tonight.
**Guest JP:** はい、今夜19時で2名の予約をお願いします。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。本日19時に2名様でご予約いたします。アレルギーなどはございますか？
  - EN: Certainly. I will book a table for two at 7 PM tonight. Do you have any allergies?
  - Feedback: Books and proactively asks about allergies.
- [1] はい、予約します。
  - EN: Yes, I will make the reservation.
  - Feedback: Will book but misses allergy question.
- [0] 電話番号を教えますので、自分でどうぞ。
  - EN: I'll give you the phone number, so please call yourself.
  - Feedback: Concierge should make the call on the guest's behalf.
- [0] 今日は無理かもしれません。
  - EN: It may not be possible today.
  - Feedback: Try first. Same-day bookings are often possible.

## Turn 3

**System:** You call the restaurant to book the table and ask about any dietary requirements.

**Guest:** No allergies. Thank you so much!
**Guest JP:** アレルギーはありません。ありがとうございます！

### Choices

- [2] こちらこそ、ありがとうございます。ご予約の確認書をお部屋にお持ちいたします。どうぞ素敵なお食事をお楽しみくださいませ。
  - EN: Thank you as well. I will bring the reservation confirmation to your room. Please enjoy your meal.
  - Feedback: Follows up with written confirmation and warm wishes in proper keigo.
- [1] どういたしまして。
  - EN: You're welcome.
  - Feedback: Polite but minimal.
- [0] 大丈夫です。
  - EN: No problem.
  - Feedback: Too casual.
- [0] 気をつけて行ってきてください。
  - EN: Please take care on your way.
  - Feedback: Slightly casual and lacks warmth.
