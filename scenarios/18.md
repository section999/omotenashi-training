---
id: 18
title: Noise Complaint
description: Guest complains about noise from a neighboring room.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** The people next door are very loud. It's midnight.
**Guest JP:** 隣の部屋がとても騒がしいんです。真夜中なのに。

### Choices

- [2] ご不便をおかけして、誠に申し訳ございません。すぐに対応いたします。
  - EN: We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. We will deal with it immediately.
  - Feedback: Sincere apology and immediate action.
- [1] 分かりました。注意します。
  - EN: Understood. I will address it.
  - Feedback: Will act but lacks empathy in wording.
- [0] 他のお客様に何も言えません。
  - EN: We cannot say anything to other guests.
  - Feedback: False. Hotels can and must address disturbances.
- [0] 耳栓はありますか？
  - EN: Do you have earplugs?
  - Feedback: Offensive suggestion. Take action instead.

## Turn 2

**System:** You sincerely apologize for the late-night disturbance and commit to addressing it immediately.

**Guest:** Can you please do something about it?
**Guest JP:** 何とかしていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。ただちに係の者が静粛にご協力いただくよう伝えます。
  - EN: Certainly. I will immediately send someone to ask the other guests to keep it down.
  - Feedback: Uses ただちに for urgency. Excellent.
- [1] はい、言いに行きます。
  - EN: Yes, I will go tell them.
  - Feedback: Will act but phrasing is informal.
- [0] 難しいです。
  - EN: That's difficult.
  - Feedback: Don't deflect. This is a solvable problem.
- [0] 他の部屋に移りますか？
  - EN: Would you like to move to another room?
  - Feedback: Jumping ahead. Resolve the issue first.

## Turn 3

**System:** You send staff to address the neighboring room and assure the guest it will be handled.

**Guest:** Thank you. If it doesn't stop, I'll call again.
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。止まらなければまた電話します。

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。何かございましたら、いつでもフロントまでご連絡ください。安心してお休みください。
  - EN: Of course. Please contact the front desk anytime if anything comes up. Please rest easy.
  - Feedback: Welcomes follow-up and soothes the guest.
- [1] はい、また電話してください。
  - EN: Yes, please call again.
  - Feedback: Invites call but lacks warmth.
- [0] 大丈夫なはずです。
  - EN: It should be fine.
  - Feedback: Don't make promises you can't guarantee.
- [0] もう静かになりますよ。
  - EN: It will be quiet now.
  - Feedback: Overconfident. Can't guarantee others' behavior.
