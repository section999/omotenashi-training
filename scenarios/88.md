---
id: 88
title: Lost-and-Found Inquiry for Previous Stay
description: A guest calls a week after their stay to enquire whether a watch was found in their room.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hello, I stayed at your hotel last week — from the 12th to the 14th — and I think I may have left my watch in the room. It's a silver men's watch with a brown leather strap.
**Guest JP:** もしもし、先週12日から14日まで御社のホテルに宿泊しました。部屋に時計を置いてきたかもしれません。茶色い革のベルトのついた銀色のメンズ時計です。

### Choices

- [2] ご連絡いただきありがとうございます。その節はご利用いただきまして誠にありがとうございました。銀色で茶色い革ベルトの時計でございますね。ただいま遺失物管理台帳を確認いたします。お名前とご宿泊のお部屋番号をお聞かせいただけますでしょうか。
  - EN: Thank you for calling, and thank you for staying with us last week. A silver watch with a brown leather strap — I will check our lost-and-found register right now. May I ask your name and the room number you were staying in?
  - Feedback: Thanks the guest for their previous stay, confirms the description clearly, and moves immediately to the check while gathering the key information — attentive and efficient.
- [1] 確認いたします。お名前とお部屋番号を教えてください。
  - EN: I will check. Please give me your name and room number.
  - Feedback: Efficient and correct but misses a warm acknowledgment of the guest's previous stay.
- [0] 1週間も経っていますので、見つかっていない可能性が高いです。
  - EN: As it has been a week, there is a good chance it has not been found.
  - Feedback: Pre-emptively dampening expectations without even checking is unhelpful and discouraging — always check before making assumptions.
- [0] 遺失物についてはウェブフォームからお問い合わせください。
  - EN: Please submit a lost-item enquiry through our website form.
  - Feedback: Directing a guest who has called for help to fill in an online form is impersonal and fails to provide the immediate assistance available by phone.

## Turn 2

**System:** You check the lost-and-found register and find that the watch is there; the guest is relieved and asks how to get it back.

**Guest:** Oh wonderful, it's there! How can I get it back? I'm not in Japan at the moment — I've already returned home.
**Guest JP:** ああ、よかった、ありました！どうすれば返してもらえますか？今は日本にいなくて、すでに帰国しています。

### Choices

- [2] 見つかりましてよかったございました。海外へのご返送も承っております。保険付きの国際速達クーリエでお届けすることが可能でございます。送料についてはクーリエの種類によって異なりますが、詳しくご案内いたします。ご住所をお聞かせいただけますでしょうか。
  - EN: I am so glad it has been found. We can absolutely arrange to send it back to you internationally. We offer insured international express courier delivery. The shipping cost will vary depending on the courier option, and I will explain the details clearly. May I take your address?
  - Feedback: Confirms the international shipping option immediately, mentions insurance coverage which is important for a valuable item, flags costs transparently, and asks for the address — complete and reassuring.
- [1] 国際郵便でお送りできます。ご住所を教えていただけますか。
  - EN: We can send it by international post. Could you give me your address?
  - Feedback: Handles the return correctly but does not mention insurance, which is important for a watch, and does not distinguish between delivery options.
- [0] 直接取りにいらしていただくことは可能でしょうか。
  - EN: Would it be possible for you to come and collect it in person?
  - Feedback: Asking a guest who has already returned to their home country to come back to collect an item shows no awareness of the situation and is completely impractical.
- [0] 発送はお客様のご負担となります。
  - EN: The shipping cost will be at your own expense.
  - Feedback: Leading with the cost before even discussing the option or asking for the address feels unwelcoming — explain the service first, then discuss costs when relevant.

## Turn 3

**System:** You offer to ship the watch internationally by insured express courier; the guest asks about the shipping cost and provides their address.

**Guest:** That sounds perfect. How much will the shipping cost? And here's my address: 42 Maple Street, Edinburgh, Scotland, EH1 2AB.
**Guest JP:** それは完璧です。送料はいくらですか？住所はこちらです：スコットランド、エジンバラ、メープルストリート42番、EH1 2AB。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。エジンバラへの保険付き国際速達クーリエの場合、送料はおよそ3,000円から5,000円の見込みです。お支払いは電話またはオンラインでご対応可能です。お送りの前に、追跡番号をメールにてお知らせいたします。お時計は厳重に梱包してお届けします。住所を復唱させていただきます。スコットランド、エジンバラ、メープルストリート42番、EH1 2AB、でございますね。
  - EN: Thank you. For insured express courier to Edinburgh, the estimated shipping cost is approximately 3,000 to 5,000 yen. Payment can be arranged by phone or online. Before we send it, I will email you the tracking number. The watch will be carefully packaged. Let me confirm your address — 42 Maple Street, Edinburgh, Scotland, EH1 2AB — is that correct?
  - Feedback: Gives a clear cost estimate, explains payment options, promises a tracking number, mentions careful packaging, and confirms the address back — a thorough and reassuring end to the interaction.
- [1] 送料は3,000円から5,000円ほどです。住所を確認させていただきます。エジンバラ、メープルストリート42番ですね。
  - EN: Shipping will cost around 3,000 to 5,000 yen. Let me confirm the address — 42 Maple Street, Edinburgh.
  - Feedback: Gives the cost and confirms the address, but does not mention tracking, packaging, or payment options.
- [0] 送料は確認後にご連絡します。
  - EN: I will contact you with the shipping cost after checking.
  - Feedback: Deferring the cost information when an estimate is readily available leaves the guest unable to make a decision — provide an estimate now and confirm later if needed.
- [0] 高価なものの場合、万一の際は責任を負いかねます。
  - EN: For valuable items, we may not be able to take responsibility if something goes wrong.
  - Feedback: Warning the guest about liability for a service the hotel is offering before even completing the arrangement is alarming and inappropriate.
