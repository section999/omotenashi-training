## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hello, do you offer same-day laundry service?
**Guest JP:** すみません、当日中にランドリーサービスはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、当ホテルでは当日仕上げのランドリーサービスをご用意しております。午前10時までにお出しいただければ、同日中にお戻しいたします。
  - EN: Yes, our hotel offers a same-day laundry service. If items are handed in by 10 AM, they will be returned the same day.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms the service and immediately provides the key deadline, giving the guest all the information they need.
- [1] はい、同日サービスがあります。
  - EN: Yes, we have same-day service.
  - Feedback: Correct but incomplete. The cut-off time is essential information the guest needs.
- [0] ランドリーサービスはありますが、同日は難しいかもしれません。
  - EN: We have a laundry service, but same-day might be difficult.
  - Feedback: Vague and discouraging. Give a clear answer with specific conditions.
- [0] フロントに来て聞いてください。
  - EN: Please come to the front desk and ask.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful for a phone inquiry. Answer the question directly.

## Turn 2

**System:** The guest is interested in the service and asks a follow-up question.

**Guest:** What time will my items be returned?
**Guest JP:** 何時ごろに戻ってきますか？

### Choices

- [2] 午前10時までにお出しいただいたお品物は、当日の午後6時から7時ごろにお部屋へお届けいたします。
  - EN: Items submitted by 10 AM will be delivered to your room between 6 and 7 PM the same day.
  - Feedback: Excellent. Gives a specific return window and confirms delivery to the room, which is exactly what the guest needs to plan their day.
- [1] 夕方6時ごろです。
  - EN: Around 6 PM in the evening.
  - Feedback: Correct time but does not mention room delivery, which may leave the guest wondering where to collect items.
- [0] だいたい夕方ですが、状況によります。
  - EN: Roughly in the evening, but it depends on the situation.
  - Feedback: Vague. Guests need a reliable time to plan around.
- [0] 早めに出してください。
  - EN: Please submit early.
  - Feedback: Does not answer the question asked. Always address the guest's actual question first.

## Turn 3

**System:** The guest is satisfied with the timing and wants to know about pricing.

**Guest:** How much does it cost? I have a shirt and a pair of trousers.
**Guest JP:** お値段はどのくらいですか？シャツとズボンがあります。

### Choices

- [2] シャツは800円、ズボンは1,200円でございます。合計2,000円になります。ランドリー袋はお部屋のクローゼットにございますので、ご利用くださいませ。
  - EN: A shirt is 800 yen and trousers are 1,200 yen, coming to a total of 2,000 yen. The laundry bag is in your room's closet — please make use of it.
  - Feedback: Excellent. States individual and total prices, and proactively guides the guest on how to submit their items.
- [1] シャツが800円でズボンが1,200円です。合計2,000円になります。
  - EN: A shirt is 800 yen and trousers are 1,200 yen. The total is 2,000 yen.
  - Feedback: Correct pricing, but doesn't mention the laundry bag or how to submit items, leaving the process incomplete.
- [0] 料金表はお部屋にあります。
  - EN: The price list is in your room.
  - Feedback: Redirects instead of answering. The guest asked directly — give the price.
- [0] クリーニング代は安くないですよ。
  - EN: Dry cleaning isn't cheap, you know.
  - Feedback: Inappropriate comment. Never imply criticism about a guest's spending choices.
