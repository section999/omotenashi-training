## Turn 1

**Guest:** The alarm is going off! What do we do?
**Guest JP:** 警報が鳴っています！どうすればいいですか？

### Choices

- [2] 非常口はこちらでございます。エレベーターは使わず、階段でご避難ください。落ち着いてご移動ください。
  - EN: The emergency exit is this way. Please evacuate using the stairs, not the elevator. Please move calmly.
  - Feedback: Clear, calm, and complete evacuation guidance.
- [1] 外に出てください。
  - EN: Please go outside.
  - Feedback: Correct direction but vague on how.
- [0] 多分誤報です。
  - EN: It's probably a false alarm.
  - Feedback: Never assume false alarm. Evacuate first.
- [0] パニックにならないで。
  - EN: Don't panic.
  - Feedback: Does not give actionable guidance.

## Turn 2

**System:** You direct the guest toward the evacuation route and guide them to use the stairs.

**Guest:** Where is the emergency exit?
**Guest JP:** 非常口はどこですか？

### Choices

- [2] 廊下の突き当たりを右に曲がり、非常口の緑のサインに従ってください。
  - EN: Turn right at the end of the corridor and follow the green emergency exit signs.
  - Feedback: Specific and actionable directions.
- [1] 右です。
  - EN: It's to the right.
  - Feedback: Directional but lacks enough detail.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Unacceptable in an emergency. Know exit locations.
- [0] 地図があります。
  - EN: There is a map.
  - Feedback: During alarm, verbal guidance is needed urgently.

## Turn 3

**System:** You point out the emergency exit signs and ensure the guest reaches the evacuation area safely.

**Guest:** Is it safe to go back inside now?
**Guest JP:** もう中に戻っても大丈夫ですか？

### Choices

- [2] 消防署の確認が取れ次第、ご案内いたします。ご安全のため、もう少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: I will let you know as soon as the fire department gives the all-clear. For your safety, please wait a little longer.
  - Feedback: Defers to fire authority. Correct and safe.
- [1] まだ確認中でございます。しばらくこちらでお待ちください。
  - EN: We are still confirming the situation. Please wait here for a moment.
  - Feedback: Appropriate holding response while awaiting official clearance.
- [0] 大丈夫だと思います。
  - EN: I think it's fine.
  - Feedback: Never guess on fire safety. Wait for officials.
- [0] はい、もう入ってください。
  - EN: Yes, you may go back in.
  - Feedback: Only fire officials can clear re-entry.
