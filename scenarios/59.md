## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, I need to reserve a meeting room for four people. Do you have something available?
**Guest JP:** すみません、4名用の会議室を予約したいのですが、空いていますか？

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。4名様用の会議室をお探しでございますね。空き状況を確認いたしますので、ご希望のご利用日時をお伺いできますでしょうか。
  - EN: Thank you. You are looking for a meeting room for four people. I will check availability — may I ask your preferred date and time?
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms the request, commits to checking, and gathers the necessary information to proceed.
- [1] はい、確認します。いつ使いたいですか？
  - EN: Yes, I'll check. When do you want to use it?
  - Feedback: Functional but lacks warmth and does not confirm the number of guests in the response.
- [0] 会議室は今使われています。
  - EN: The meeting room is currently occupied.
  - Feedback: Assumes unavailability without checking. Always verify before responding.
- [0] 何名ですか？
  - EN: How many people?
  - Feedback: The guest already stated four people. Listen carefully and avoid asking for information already given.

## Turn 2

**System:** You check and confirm a room is available. The guest now asks about equipment.

**Guest:** Great. What kind of AV equipment is available in the room?
**Guest JP:** よかったです。部屋にはどんな設備がありますか？

### Choices

- [2] 会議室にはプロジェクター、スクリーン、ホワイトボード、そしてHDMIケーブルをご用意しております。Wi-Fiも無料でご利用いただけます。他にご必要な機材がございましたら、お申し付けくださいませ。
  - EN: The meeting room is equipped with a projector, screen, whiteboard, and HDMI cable. Wi-Fi is also available free of charge. If you need any additional equipment, please do not hesitate to ask.
  - Feedback: Excellent. Lists all available equipment clearly and proactively invites further requests — thorough and helpful.
- [1] プロジェクターとホワイトボードがあります。Wi-Fiも使えます。
  - EN: There is a projector and whiteboard. Wi-Fi is also available.
  - Feedback: Correct but incomplete — does not mention the screen or HDMI, and misses the invitation to request more.
- [0] 基本的なものはあると思います。
  - EN: I think the basics are there.
  - Feedback: Vague and unhelpful. Guests planning a meeting need specific information.
- [0] 自分の機材を持ってきてください。
  - EN: Please bring your own equipment.
  - Feedback: Dismissive and incorrect if equipment is available. Always share what the hotel provides.

## Turn 3

**System:** The guest is satisfied with the facilities and wants to finalize the reservation.

**Guest:** Perfect. Can I confirm the booking for tomorrow at 2 PM for two hours?
**Guest JP:** では、明日の午後2時から2時間でお願いします。確認できますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。明日午後2時から午後4時まで、4名様の会議室を承りました。お名前とご連絡先をお伺いしてよろしいでしょうか。確認書をお送りいたします。
  - EN: Certainly. I have reserved the meeting room for four people tomorrow from 2 PM to 4 PM. May I take your name and contact details? We will send you a confirmation.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms date, time, duration, and number of guests, then completes the booking professionally by requesting contact information for a confirmation.
- [1] はい、明日2時から4時ですね。承りました。
  - EN: Yes, tomorrow from 2 to 4. Noted.
  - Feedback: Correct confirmation of time, but does not request contact details or offer a written confirmation.
- [0] たぶん大丈夫です。
  - EN: It should probably be fine.
  - Feedback: たぶん (probably) is not acceptable for a booking confirmation. Be definitive.
- [0] 当日フロントに来て確認してください。
  - EN: Please come to the front desk on the day to confirm.
  - Feedback: The guest deserves a confirmed booking now, not an instruction to check again later.
