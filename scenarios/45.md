title: Overbooking
description: The hotel is overbooked and a guest must be relocated.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** I have a confirmed reservation. Why isn't my room ready?
**Guest JP:** 予約を確認しています。なぜ部屋の準備ができていないのですか？

### Choices

- [2] 誠に申し訳ございません。ご予約を承っておりましたが、手配に問題が生じました。できる限りの対応をいたします。
  - EN: We are truly sorry. We did have your reservation, but a problem arose with the arrangements. We will do everything we can to resolve this.
  - Feedback: Honest, sincere apology with commitment to resolve.
- [1] 少し待ってください。
  - EN: Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Buys time but doesn't explain or apologize.
- [0] そういうことが起きます。
  - EN: These things happen.
  - Feedback: Dismissive and unacceptable.
- [0] 次回は早めにご連絡ください。
  - EN: Next time, please contact us earlier.
  - Feedback: Shifts blame to guest. Completely wrong.

## Turn 2

**System:** You sincerely apologize and explain there was an overbooking issue with the reservation.

**Guest:** This is unacceptable. I'm very upset.
**Guest JP:** これは受け入れられません。とても不満です。

### Choices

- [2] おっしゃる通りでございます。大変ご迷惑をおかけいたしました。近隣の同等ホテルへのご案内と、交通費をご負担いたします。
  - EN: You are absolutely right. We have caused you a great deal of inconvenience. We will arrange a room at a comparable nearby hotel and cover your transportation costs.
  - Feedback: Validates feelings, offers solution and compensation.
- [1] 申し訳ありません。
  - EN: We are sorry.
  - Feedback: Apology only, no resolution offered.
- [0] ご理解ください。
  - EN: Please understand.
  - Feedback: Asking for understanding without offering a solution.
- [0] それは私のせいではありません。
  - EN: That is not my fault.
  - Feedback: Deflecting blame is deeply inappropriate.

## Turn 3

**System:** You validate the guest's frustration and offer to relocate them to a comparable nearby hotel with transport covered.

**Guest:** Fine. But I want a complimentary upgrade at the next hotel.
**Guest JP:** わかりました。ただ、何らかの補償をお願いしたいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。近隣ホテルにアップグレードのお部屋をご確保いたします。今後のご滞在でも優待をご用意いたします。
  - EN: Certainly. We will secure an upgraded room at a nearby hotel. We will also arrange special benefits for your future stays.
  - Feedback: Agrees and extends goodwill beyond this stay.
- [1] 確認してみます。
  - EN: I will check.
  - Feedback: Will try but non-committal for a serious complaint.
- [0] それはできません。
  - EN: That is not possible.
  - Feedback: In overbooking situations, compensation is standard.
- [0] アップグレードは高いです。
  - EN: An upgrade is expensive.
  - Feedback: Cost should not be mentioned to an upset guest.
