## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, we have a small dog. Is that OK?
**Guest JP:** 小型犬がいるのですが、大丈夫ですか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、当ホテルはペット可でございます。ペット同伴のお部屋をご用意しております。
  - EN: Yes, our hotel is pet-friendly. We have rooms available for guests with pets.
  - Feedback: Warm welcome that confirms the policy.
- [1] ペットはいいですよ。
  - EN: Pets are welcome.
  - Feedback: Friendly but casual.
- [0] 犬はちょっと困ります。
  - EN: Dogs are a bit problematic.
  - Feedback: Check the policy before turning away guests.
- [0] 他のお客様に迷惑ですか？
  - EN: Will it bother other guests?
  - Feedback: Inappropriate question about their pet.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the hotel is pet-friendly and note that a pet-suitable room is available.

**Guest:** Are there any rules about where the dog can go?
**Guest JP:** 犬が入れる場所についてのルールはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、ロビーやレストランへのご入場はお控えいただいております。専用のペットルームと庭園エリアをお使いいただけます。
  - EN: Yes, we ask that pets refrain from entering the lobby and restaurant. You are welcome to use the dedicated pet room and garden area.
  - Feedback: Clear rules with positive alternatives.
- [1] ロビーはダメです。
  - EN: The lobby is not allowed.
  - Feedback: States restriction but doesn't offer alternatives.
- [0] どこでも大丈夫です。
  - EN: Anywhere is fine.
  - Feedback: Incorrect. Every hotel has pet area restrictions.
- [0] 部屋の外はダメです。
  - EN: Outside the room is not allowed.
  - Feedback: Too restrictive if untrue. State actual policy.

## Turn 3

**System:** You explain the pet areas and restrictions, pointing out the dedicated garden space.

**Guest:** Is there anywhere nearby to walk the dog?
**Guest JP:** 近くに犬の散歩ができる場所はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、ホテルの隣に公園がございます。散歩コースもございますので、ぜひご利用くださいませ。
  - EN: Yes, there is a park right next to the hotel. There is also a walking trail, so please feel free to use it.
  - Feedback: Helpful local knowledge for a pet-friendly guest.
- [1] 公園があります。
  - EN: There is a park.
  - Feedback: Gives info but no details on location.
- [0] 外に出てください。
  - EN: Please go outside.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful and rude.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Know local amenities relevant to your guests.
