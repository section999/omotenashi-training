title: Late Check-out Request
description: A guest asks if they can check out later than the standard 11 AM time.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, I was wondering if it's possible to check out at 1 PM instead of 11?
**Guest JP:** すみません、チェックアウトを11時ではなく、午後1時にできますか？

### Choices

- [2] ご要望を承りました。空き状況を確認いたしますので、少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Understood. I will check availability, so please wait just a moment.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Accepts the request formally and promises to check without saying no outright.
- [1] 確認してみます。少し待ってください。
  - EN: I'll try to check. Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Acceptable but 少し待ってください is less polite than 少々お待ちくださいませ.
- [0] チェックアウトは11時です。
  - EN: Check-out is at 11 AM.
  - Feedback: Refuses without checking. Always explore options before declining.
- [0] 遅いチェックアウトはできません。
  - EN: Late check-out is not possible.
  - Feedback: Flat refusal without investigation is poor service. Always verify first.

## Turn 2

**System:** You check with housekeeping and confirm a late check-out until 1 PM is available for an additional fee.

**Guest:** Oh, is there an extra charge for that?
**Guest JP:** それは追加料金がかかりますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、午後1時までのレイトチェックアウトは、追加料金として3,000円いただいております。よろしければ手続きいたします。
  - EN: Yes, for a late check-out until 1 PM, there is an additional fee of 3,000 yen. We would be happy to arrange this for you if you wish.
  - Feedback: Excellent. States the fee clearly, uses polite language, and offers to proceed without pressure.
- [1] 3,000円かかります。どうしますか？
  - EN: It costs 3,000 yen. What would you like to do?
  - Feedback: Correct but lacks the warm framing and polite offering of assistance.
- [0] 追加料金が発生します。
  - EN: An additional fee will be charged.
  - Feedback: Vague — does not state the amount. Guests need clear information.
- [0] 無料ではありません。
  - EN: It is not free.
  - Feedback: Blunt and unhelpful. State the amount and offer to proceed politely.

## Turn 3

**System:** The guest agrees to the fee and wants to confirm the arrangement.

**Guest:** That's fine, I'll take the late check-out. Can you confirm it's all set?
**Guest JP:** わかりました、レイトチェックアウトにします。手続きは完了していますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。チェックアウトを午後1時に変更いたしました。追加料金の3,000円はチェックアウト時にご精算いただきます。ごゆっくりおくつろぎくださいませ。
  - EN: Certainly. Your check-out has been changed to 1 PM. The additional fee of 3,000 yen will be settled at check-out. Please enjoy your extended stay.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms the change, restates the fee and when it's due, and closes with a warm send-off.
- [1] はい、午後1時に変更しました。追加料金はチェックアウト時にお支払いください。
  - EN: Yes, I've changed it to 1 PM. Please pay the additional fee at check-out.
  - Feedback: Correct but the closing is missing warmth and could feel abrupt.
- [0] わかりました。
  - EN: Understood.
  - Feedback: Far too brief. Does not confirm details or reassure the guest.
- [0] 料金を先に払ってもらえますか？
  - EN: Could you pay the fee first?
  - Feedback: Demanding prepayment without prior policy explanation is inappropriate.
