title: Guest Left Item in Safe After Checkout
description: Guest calls 30 minutes after checkout to say they left their passport in the in-room safe and are already heading to the airport.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hello, I checked out about 30 minutes ago and I've just realised my passport is still in the room safe. I'm on my way to the airport.
**Guest JP:** もしもし、30分ほど前にチェックアウトしたんですが、部屋のセーフにパスポートを置いてきてしまいました。今、空港に向かっています。

### Choices

- [2] ご連絡いただきありがとうございます。ご不便をおかけし、誠に申し訳ございません。ただいまお部屋を確認いたします。もしパスポートが見つかりましたら、すぐにご連絡差し上げます。お名前とご宿泊されていたお部屋番号をお聞かせいただけますでしょうか。
  - EN: Thank you for calling. I am so sorry for this stressful situation. I will check the room right now. If the passport is found, I will contact you immediately. May I ask your name and your room number?
  - Feedback: Responds with urgency and empathy, clearly prioritises the check, and gathers the necessary information efficiently — the correct first response to a time-sensitive lost item.
- [1] 確認いたします。お部屋番号を教えていただけますか？
  - EN: I will check. Could you tell me your room number?
  - Feedback: Takes the right first step but lacks any expression of urgency or acknowledgment of how stressful this situation must be for the guest.
- [0] チェックアウト後はお部屋への立入りができません。
  - EN: We are unable to access rooms after checkout.
  - Feedback: Refusing to help on procedural grounds when a guest faces a passport emergency is unacceptable and completely contrary to good service.
- [0] 空港でなくされた可能性もございますが…。
  - EN: It is also possible you may have lost it at the airport…
  - Feedback: Speculating on where the item was lost rather than immediately acting to check the room wastes precious time and implies doubt about the guest.

## Turn 2

**System:** You locate the passport in the safe — it is still there; the guest asks if the hotel can send it by courier to the airport.

**Guest:** Oh thank goodness. Can you send it to me at the airport? I'm at Terminal 2.
**Guest JP:** よかったです。空港に届けてもらえますか？ターミナル2にいます。

### Choices

- [2] 見つかりまして本当によかったございました。ターミナル2への緊急クーリエ手配をただいま確認いたします。お客様のフライト時刻をお聞かせいただけますでしょうか。できる限り速やかにお届けできるよう、最善を尽くします。
  - EN: I am so relieved it has been found. I am now checking the arrangements for an emergency courier to Terminal 2. May I ask your flight time? We will do everything we can to get it to you as quickly as possible.
  - Feedback: Shares the guest's relief, immediately moves to arrange the courier, asks the key question about flight time, and commits to urgency — a strong and empathetic response.
- [1] かしこまりました。クーリエの手配を確認いたします。フライト時刻を教えてください。
  - EN: Certainly. I will check courier arrangements. Please tell me your flight time.
  - Feedback: Practical and clear, but could acknowledge the guest's relief more warmly before moving into logistics.
- [0] クーリエサービスはこちらでは手配しておりません。
  - EN: We do not arrange courier services here.
  - Feedback: Telling the guest the hotel cannot help when they urgently need their passport at the airport is a serious failure of service duty.
- [0] タクシーで取りに戻られることはできますか？
  - EN: Would it be possible for you to take a taxi back to collect it?
  - Feedback: Asking the guest to return to the hotel when they are already heading to the airport for a flight is wholly impractical and unhelpful.

## Turn 3

**System:** You coordinate an emergency courier to the airport departure level and must confirm details and reassure the guest.

**Guest:** Will it actually get here in time? My flight is in two and a half hours.
**Guest JP:** 本当に間に合いますか？フライトまであと2時間半しかありません。

### Choices

- [2] ご安心ください。ただいまクーリエに手配済みでございます。2時間半でございますと、時間的に十分間に合う見込みです。クーリエのご連絡先とトラッキング番号を今すぐお送りいたします。またご不明な点がございましたら、私の直通番号にいつでもご連絡くださいませ。どうかご安心ください。
  - EN: Please be assured — the courier has been arranged right now. With two and a half hours remaining, it should arrive well within time. I will send you the courier contact and tracking number immediately. Please feel free to call my direct number at any time if you have any concerns. You have my word we are on this.
  - Feedback: Gives concrete reassurance with a time assessment, provides tracking information, and offers a direct contact — this is the full package of reassurance the guest needs in an anxious moment.
- [1] ご安心ください。クーリエを手配しましたので、間に合うと思います。
  - EN: Please do not worry. We have arranged a courier and I believe it will arrive in time.
  - Feedback: Reassuring and relevant, but does not offer a tracking number or direct contact — leaving the guest with no way to follow up.
- [0] 交通状況によっては遅れる可能性もございます。
  - EN: There is a possibility of delay depending on traffic conditions.
  - Feedback: Leading with a caveat about potential delay when the guest is already anxious will cause unnecessary distress — commit to the best effort and monitor.
- [0] 到着したらターミナルのインフォメーションカウンターにお聞きください。
  - EN: When it arrives, please ask at the terminal information counter.
  - Feedback: Passing responsibility to the airport staff rather than ensuring the handoff is managed directly by the hotel is an abdication of duty.
