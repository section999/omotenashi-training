---
id: 71
title: Overbooking Situation
description: Guest arrives with a confirmed reservation but no room is available due to a system error.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, I have a reservation under the name Johnson. Here's my confirmation email.
**Guest JP:** すみません、ジョンソンという名前で予約しています。確認メールがここにあります。

### Choices

- [2] ジョンソン様、ご到着いただきありがとうございます。ご予約を確認させていただきます。少々お待ちくださいませ。……誠に申し訳ございません。システムのエラーにより、本日ご用意できるお部屋がございません。心よりお詫び申し上げます。
  - EN: Welcome, Mr Johnson. Thank you for arriving. Let me check your reservation — please bear with me one moment. I am deeply sorry — due to a system error, we are unable to accommodate you in our hotel this evening. I offer you my sincerest apologies.
  - Feedback: Acknowledges the guest properly, checks immediately, and delivers the difficult news with a genuine and formal apology — the correct approach to a very serious failure.
- [1] ジョンソン様、確認いたします。……申し訳ございませんが、本日お部屋のご用意ができない状況です。
  - EN: Mr Johnson, let me check. I am sorry, but unfortunately we are unable to prepare a room for you today.
  - Feedback: Delivers the key message and apologises, but lacks the depth of formal acknowledgment and explanation of the cause that the situation demands.
- [0] システムのエラーですので、私どもにはどうしようもございません。
  - EN: This is a system error, so there is nothing we can do.
  - Feedback: Blaming the system and implying helplessness is completely unacceptable — the guest has a confirmed booking and is owed a full solution.
- [0] 本日は満室となっております。
  - EN: We are fully booked today.
  - Feedback: Stating the hotel is full without acknowledging the guest's confirmed reservation dismisses their legitimate complaint entirely.

## Turn 2

**System:** You have located a superior room at a partner hotel nearby and must offer this to the very upset guest.

**Guest:** This is unacceptable. I booked this hotel specifically. I'm not going anywhere else.
**Guest JP:** これは受け入れられません。このホテルを指定して予約したんです。他には行きません。

### Choices

- [2] おっしゃる通りでございます。ご不便をおかけし、誠に申し訳ございません。お気持ちはよく存じます。近くの提携ホテルに、お客様のご予約よりも上のグレードのお部屋をご用意できました。タクシーのご手配と夕食のバウチャーも、もちろんホテル側でご負担させていただきます。できる限りのご対応をいたします。
  - EN: You are absolutely right, and I am truly sorry for this serious inconvenience. I completely understand how you feel. We have secured a superior room at our partner hotel nearby — it is a higher grade than what you originally booked. We will of course arrange your taxi and provide a dinner voucher at our expense. We will do everything we can for you.
  - Feedback: Validates the guest's anger, apologises formally, presents a concrete superior alternative, and lists specific compensation — this is the ideal response under very difficult circumstances.
- [1] 大変申し訳ございません。近くの提携ホテルにグレードアップのお部屋をご用意できますので、いかがでしょうか。
  - EN: I am very sorry. We can arrange an upgraded room at a nearby partner hotel — would that be acceptable?
  - Feedback: Apologises and offers a solution, but does not sufficiently validate the guest's frustration or detail the compensation being offered.
- [0] 他にご選択肢がございませんので、ご理解いただくしかございません。
  - EN: There are no other options, so I'm afraid you will have to understand.
  - Feedback: Telling an upset guest they have no choice and must accept the situation is dismissive and will severely escalate the complaint.
- [0] このようなことは滅多にございません。
  - EN: This kind of thing rarely happens.
  - Feedback: Minimising the incident does nothing to resolve it and implies the guest should feel less entitled to be upset.

## Turn 3

**System:** You offer a free taxi, dinner voucher, and room upgrade at the partner hotel, and the guest reluctantly agrees.

**Guest:** Fine. But I'm not happy about this. Will someone sort everything out for me?
**Guest JP:** わかりました。でも、納得はしていません。全部手配してもらえますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。すべてこちらでご手配いたします。ただいまタクシーをご用意いたします。夕食のバウチャーとホテルへのご案内もお渡しいたします。明日、改めてご不便をおかけしたことへのお詫びのご連絡を差し上げたく存じます。本当に申し訳ございませんでした。
  - EN: Certainly — we will take care of absolutely everything. I am arranging your taxi right now. I will provide your dinner voucher and a letter of introduction to the hotel. I would also like to follow up with you tomorrow to express our apologies once more for this serious inconvenience. We are truly sorry.
  - Feedback: Affirms total ownership of the situation, lists every step being taken, and promises a follow-up — this level of care is precisely what an overbooking situation demands.
- [1] はい、タクシーとバウチャーをご用意いたします。先方のホテルにもご連絡しておきます。
  - EN: Yes, I will arrange the taxi and voucher. I will also contact the partner hotel.
  - Feedback: Covers the practical steps but offers no further apology, no personal touch, and no follow-up — missing the warmth and accountability the situation requires.
- [0] 提携ホテルに直接ご連絡いただければ、スムーズかと思います。
  - EN: It would probably be smoother if you contact the partner hotel directly.
  - Feedback: Asking the guest to handle their own arrangements after this failure is unacceptable — every step must be managed by the hotel.
- [0] タクシーは外でお待ちのものをご利用ください。
  - EN: Please use one of the taxis waiting outside.
  - Feedback: Sending the guest to find their own transport after an overbooking is a serious lapse in service recovery.
