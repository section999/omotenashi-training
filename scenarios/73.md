---
id: 73
title: Room Change Request Mid-Stay
description: Guest calls at 11 PM complaining of loud plumbing noises in the wall that are preventing sleep.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, sorry to call so late. There's a really loud banging noise coming from the pipes in my wall and I can't sleep.
**Guest JP:** すみません、こんな遅い時間に電話して申し訳ないのですが、壁の中のパイプからすごく大きな音がして眠れないんです。

### Choices

- [2] お電話いただきありがとうございます。夜分にご不便をおかけし、誠に申し訳ございません。配管の音が気になってお休みになれないとのこと、すぐに対応いたします。ただいま空き室の状況を確認いたしますので、少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Thank you for calling. I am truly sorry for the disturbance so late in the evening. I understand you cannot sleep due to the noise from the pipes — I will address this right away. Please give me just a moment while I check room availability.
  - Feedback: Apologises sincerely for the late-night disturbance, confirms understanding of the problem, and immediately takes action — the ideal response to a sleeping comfort issue.
- [1] ご不便をおかけして申し訳ございません。お部屋の変更が可能か確認いたします。
  - EN: I am sorry for the inconvenience. I will check whether a room change is possible.
  - Feedback: Polite and practical, but lacks a warmer acknowledgment of how disruptive the situation is at this time of night.
- [0] 配管の音は建物の構造上、止めることが難しい場合がございます。
  - EN: Pipe noises can be difficult to stop due to the building structure.
  - Feedback: Explaining why the problem cannot be fixed instead of trying to solve it is unhelpful — the guest needs a solution, not a technical explanation.
- [0] 耳栓のご用意もできますが、いかがでしょうか。
  - EN: We can provide earplugs if that would help.
  - Feedback: Offering earplugs as a first response to a serious noise complaint is dismissive and suggests the hotel will not attempt a real resolution.

## Turn 2

**System:** You check and find that an equivalent room on a higher floor is available; the guest asks if they need to move all their belongings tonight.

**Guest:** A different room sounds good. But do I have to move all my stuff at 11 PM?
**Guest JP:** 別のお部屋はいいですね。でも、夜11時に荷物を全部移動しないといけないんですか？

### Choices

- [2] ご安心ください。ベルスタッフがすぐにお伺いし、お荷物のお運びをお手伝いいたします。お客様にはお手を煩わせません。新しいお部屋へのご案内も私どもがいたします。どうかご負担なくお任せくださいませ。
  - EN: Please do not worry at all. Our bellhop will come to you immediately and assist with all of your belongings. You do not need to lift a thing. We will personally escort you to your new room. Please leave everything to us.
  - Feedback: Directly and warmly addresses the guest's concern, promises full physical assistance, and removes every burden from the guest — exactly the right reassurance.
- [1] ベルボーイがお荷物をお運びします。ご安心ください。
  - EN: A bellhop will carry your belongings for you. Please do not worry.
  - Feedback: Reassures the guest and offers help, but lacks the fuller detail and warmth of a more complete response.
- [0] お荷物はご自身でお持ちいただき、新しいお部屋の鍵を受け取りにいらしてください。
  - EN: Please bring your belongings yourself and come to collect the key for the new room.
  - Feedback: Expecting the guest to move their own things at 11 PM after a noise complaint is a significant service failure.
- [0] なるべく早く移動していただけますと助かります。
  - EN: It would help if you could move as soon as possible.
  - Feedback: Framing the urgency as a benefit to the hotel rather than focusing on the guest's comfort shows the wrong priorities.

## Turn 3

**System:** You offer to have a bellhop assist with the move and send a small amenity to the new room as an apology; the guest is grateful and agrees.

**Guest:** That's very kind of you. Yes, please send someone to help. Thank you so much.
**Guest JP:** ご親切にありがとうございます。ぜひお願いします。本当にありがとうございます。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。ただいまベルスタッフをお部屋にお向かいさせます。また、ご迷惑をおかけしたお詫びとして、新しいお部屋に少々のアメニティをご用意させていただきます。どうかゆっくりとお休みいただけますよう、心よりお祈り申し上げます。
  - EN: Certainly. I am sending the bellhop to your room right now. As a small apology for the inconvenience you have experienced, we will also place a complimentary amenity in your new room. We sincerely hope you are able to enjoy a restful night's sleep.
  - Feedback: Confirms the action, mentions the amenity naturally as an apology, and closes with a genuine and warm wish — a complete and gracious resolution.
- [1] ありがとうございます。ベルスタッフをすぐに向かわせます。新しいお部屋では快適にお過ごしいただけると思います。
  - EN: Thank you. I will send the bellhop right away. I hope you find the new room comfortable.
  - Feedback: Efficient and friendly, but does not mention the amenity and lacks a final apology for the disruption.
- [0] では、ベルボーイが参りましたらドアをお開けください。
  - EN: Please open your door when the bellhop arrives.
  - Feedback: Purely transactional — gives an instruction without warmth or any acknowledgment of the inconvenience caused.
- [0] 次回のご滞在にはご注意いただければと思います。
  - EN: Please bear this in mind for your next stay.
  - Feedback: Implying the guest should have been more careful is inappropriate and shifts blame onto them for the hotel's infrastructure issue.
