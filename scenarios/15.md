---
id: 15
title: Declining a Guest Request
description: Guest makes a request the hotel cannot accommodate — staff must decline politely.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Can I bring my dog into the restaurant?
**Guest JP:** レストランに犬を連れて行ってもいいですか？

### Choices

- [2] 大変申し訳ございませんが、レストランへのペットのご入場はお断りしております。お部屋でのペットのご滞在は喜んで承っております。
  - EN: I'm very sorry, but we are unable to allow pets in the restaurant. We are happy to accommodate your pet in your room.
  - Feedback: Clear refusal with genuine apology and a helpful alternative.
- [1] すみません、ペットはレストランに入れません。
  - EN: Sorry, pets cannot enter the restaurant.
  - Feedback: Correct but abrupt. Soften with an apology and an alternative.
- [0] ちょっと…難しいですね。
  - EN: That might be a little difficult.
  - Feedback: Vague and unclear. A guest deserves a direct, polite answer.
- [0] ダメです。
  - EN: No.
  - Feedback: Far too blunt. Never refuse without an apology and reason.

## Turn 2

**Guest:** That's disappointing. Can I at least have room service for my dog — some plain chicken?
**Guest JP:** 残念です。せめて犬のためにルームサービスで茹でた鶏肉をもらえますか？

### Choices

- [2] 誠に恐れ入りますが、ペット用のお食事のご提供は行っておりません。近くにペット用品店がございますので、よろしければご案内いたします。
  - EN: I'm truly sorry, but we do not offer meal service for pets. There is a pet supply store nearby — I would be happy to show you the way.
  - Feedback: Polite refusal with a proactive alternative that genuinely helps.
- [1] ペット用の食事はご提供できません。申し訳ございません。
  - EN: We are unable to provide food for pets. I apologize.
  - Feedback: Correct and polite, but no alternative offered.
- [0] キッチンに聞いてみます。（できないのに）
  - EN: I will ask the kitchen. (When you know it is not possible.)
  - Feedback: Do not raise false hope. Declining honestly is better than a broken promise.
- [0] 自分でコンビニに行ってください。
  - EN: Please go to the convenience store yourself.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Always offer to assist, not redirect the burden to the guest.

## Turn 3

**Guest:** I understand. Is there a quiet outdoor area where I can walk my dog?
**Guest JP:** 分かりました。犬を散歩できる静かな屋外スペースはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、ホテル裏手に専用のペット散歩エリアがございます。フロントにてご案内マップをお渡しできます。
  - EN: Yes, there is a dedicated pet walking area at the rear of the hotel. We can provide a map at the front desk.
  - Feedback: Ends on a positive note — a concrete, helpful offer after two refusals.
- [1] 外に出れば散歩できます。
  - EN: You can walk outside.
  - Feedback: Correct but unhelpful. Provide specific guidance.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Always find out for the guest rather than leaving them without an answer.
- [0] ロビーでは散歩できません。
  - EN: You cannot walk in the lobby.
  - Feedback: Answers the wrong question and offers nothing useful.
