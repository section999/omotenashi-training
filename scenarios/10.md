## Turn 1

**Guest:** Can I leave my bags here after I check out?
**Guest JP:** チェックアウト後に荷物を預けることはできますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。チェックアウト後もお荷物をお預かりいたします。
  - EN: Of course. We are happy to store your luggage even after check-out.
  - Feedback: Immediate, accommodating response.
- [1] はい、大丈夫です。
  - EN: Yes, that's fine.
  - Feedback: Correct but informal.
- [0] それはできません。
  - EN: We cannot do that.
  - Feedback: Most hotels offer this. Check before refusing.
- [0] ロビーに置いてください。
  - EN: Please leave it in the lobby.
  - Feedback: Insecure and unacceptable.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the hotel offers luggage storage and ask for more details.

**Guest:** Until about 6 PM?
**Guest JP:** 18時頃までですが。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。18時までお預かりいたします。お荷物の数をお聞かせください。
  - EN: Certainly. We will store your luggage until 6 PM. How many bags do you have?
  - Feedback: Confirms time and asks for count.
- [1] はい、6時まで預かります。
  - EN: Yes, we'll keep them until 6.
  - Feedback: Correct but doesn't confirm count.
- [0] 荷物が多いと困ります。
  - EN: We'll have trouble if there's too much luggage.
  - Feedback: Discouraging. Offer assistance instead.
- [0] なぜそんなに遅いですか？
  - EN: Why so late?
  - Feedback: Inappropriate question. Respect guest plans.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm the 6 PM pickup time and ask how many bags need to be stored.

**Guest:** Great. Can I get a tag for my bags?
**Guest JP:** では、荷物にタグをつけていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。タグをお付けします。こちらが引換証でございます。お帰りの際にご提示ください。

  - Feedback: Proper procedure with clear instructions.
- [1] はい、タグを渡します。
  - EN: Yes, I will give you the tags.
  - Feedback: Correct but incomplete procedure.
- [0] タグはありません。
  - EN: We don't have tags.
  - Feedback: Luggage tags are standard. Be prepared.
- [0] 自分で書いてください。
  - EN: Please fill it out yourself.
  - Feedback: Staff should handle this service properly.
