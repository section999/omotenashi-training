title: Wedding Anniversary Private Dining Request
description: A guest requests a private dining room with flowers and a cake to celebrate their wedding anniversary.
---

## Turn 1

**System:** A guest calls the dining reservation line to arrange a special anniversary dinner. Your first priority is to warmly acknowledge the occasion before handling logistics.

**Guest:** Good evening. My wife and I are celebrating our wedding anniversary tonight, and we'd love a private dining room if possible — with some flowers and maybe a cake?
**Guest JP:** あの、今夜は妻と結婚記念日のお祝いをしたいのですが、個室をご用意いただけますか？お花とケーキもあるといいなと思って。

### Choices

- [2] 「この度は結婚記念日、誠におめでとうございます。素晴らしいお時間をホテルでお過ごしいただけますよう、精一杯お手伝いいたします。個室につきましては、空き状況を確認させていただきますので、少々お待ちいただけますでしょうか？」
  - EN: Congratulations on your wedding anniversary — how wonderful. We will do everything we can to make your time with us truly special. Regarding a private room, may I ask you to hold for just a moment while I check availability?
  - Feedback: Perfect response. It opens with a sincere congratulation that acknowledges the emotional significance of the occasion, makes a genuine commitment to making it special, and then transitions smoothly into the practical step of checking availability.
- [1] 「おめでとうございます。個室の空き状況を確認いたします。少々お待ちください。」
  - EN: Congratulations. I will check private room availability. Please hold a moment.
  - Feedback: Acceptable — the congratulations are offered and the process begins. However, the response is brief and perfunctory. For a wedding anniversary, extra warmth and a fuller acknowledgment of the occasion is expected at a 5-star property.
- [0] 「個室は事前にご予約いただく必要がございます。本日のご予約はお受けできない場合もございます。」
  - EN: Private rooms require advance reservations. We may not be able to accommodate a booking for today.
  - Feedback: Leading with restrictions before even congratulating the guest or trying to help is deeply unwelcoming. Even if same-day availability is uncertain, expressing doubt before checking is a failure of hospitality spirit.
- [0] 「ケーキとお花は別途料金がかかりますが、ご承知おきください。」
  - EN: Please be aware that the cake and flowers will incur an additional charge.
  - Feedback: Immediately discussing additional charges before acknowledging the anniversary or confirming what can be arranged is tone-deaf and transactional. Pricing can be discussed after the emotional moment is properly acknowledged.

## Turn 2

**System:** A private dining room is available for the evening. Staff now needs to confirm the reservation time, take decoration preferences for the flowers, and ask for the cake message.

**Guest:** Oh wonderful! We were thinking around 7:30. And for the flowers, we love roses — red roses especially. Could the cake say "Happy 10th Anniversary"?
**Guest JP:** よかった！時間は7時半ごろを考えています。お花はバラが好きで、赤いバラがいいです。ケーキには「ハッピー10周年アニバーサリー」と書いていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] 「かしこまりました。本日7時半に個室でのご夕食、2名様でお承りいたします。赤いバラのアレンジメントとケーキのご用意をいたします。ケーキのメッセージは『ハッピー10周年アニバーサリー』でよろしいでしょうか？念のため確認させていただきました。10周年、改めておめでとうございます。」
  - EN: Certainly. I have you reserved for this evening at 7:30 PM in the private dining room for two. We will prepare a red rose arrangement and your celebration cake. Just to confirm, the message on the cake is "Happy 10th Anniversary" — is that right? And again, congratulations on your 10th anniversary.
  - Feedback: Ideal response. All key details are confirmed back to the guest (time, party size, flowers, cake message), and the milestone anniversary is acknowledged with renewed warmth — transforming a routine confirmation into a genuinely memorable interaction.
- [1] 「7時半に個室でお承りします。赤いバラとケーキのメッセージも承りました。」
  - EN: Noted for a private room at 7:30. I have noted the red roses and cake message.
  - Feedback: The practical details are captured, but the language is flat and clinical. For a 10th anniversary dinner, the emotional energy of the interaction matters enormously. This response fails to reflect the significance of the occasion.
- [0] 「バラのアレンジメントとケーキは3日前にご注文いただくのが通常の手順です。」
  - EN: The standard procedure is to order flower arrangements and cake at least 3 days in advance.
  - Feedback: Even if this is a general policy, leading with a procedural constraint to a guest planning an anniversary dinner is inappropriate. Staff should check with the relevant departments first and present solutions, not obstacles.
- [0] 「ケーキのメッセージは英語でよいですか？日本語の方がよければ変更できます。」
  - EN: Is it okay if the cake message is in English? We can change it to Japanese if preferred.
  - Feedback: The guest already specified the message in English. Questioning this choice without reason implies the staff wasn't listening carefully, and may cause unnecessary confusion about a detail the guest has already decided on.

## Turn 3

**System:** All details are confirmed. As a finishing touch, staff should proactively offer an additional surprise element (e.g., sparkling wine on arrival, rose petal turndown) and close the interaction perfectly.

**Guest:** That's everything I hoped for. You've been so helpful — thank you very much.
**Guest JP:** 完璧です。本当にありがとうございます。よくしていただいて、嬉しいです。

### Choices

- [2] 「こちらこそ、素晴らしい記念日をお手伝いできることを光栄に存じます。もしよろしければ、お席にご着席の際にシャンパンのウェルカムサービスもご用意できますが、いかがでしょうか？素敵な記念日をお過ごしいただけますよう、スタッフ一同、心よりお祝い申し上げております。」
  - EN: The honor is entirely ours. If you would like, we can also arrange a champagne welcome when you are seated — may I add that for you? All of our staff sincerely wish you a truly wonderful anniversary.
  - Feedback: This is the ideal close. The staff accepts the guest's gratitude graciously, goes one step further by offering a champagne welcome (an elegant upsell that feels like a gift), and closes with a sincere collective wish from the entire team.
- [1] 「ありがとうございます。素敵な記念日をお過ごしください。7時半にお待ちしております。」
  - EN: Thank you. Please have a wonderful anniversary. We look forward to seeing you at 7:30.
  - Feedback: A pleasant close that confirms the time, but it's a missed opportunity. The guest is emotionally open and delighted — proactively adding one more special touch would elevate the experience to truly memorable.
- [0] 「お支払いはレストランでのキャッシュレスがお勧めです。事前にご確認ください。」
  - EN: We recommend cashless payment at the restaurant. Please confirm in advance.
  - Feedback: Ending a heartfelt anniversary booking interaction by talking about payment is jarring and inappropriate. Any payment details can be handled at the venue — introducing them here kills the emotional warmth of the moment.
- [0] 「もし何かご変更がある場合は、必ず3時間前までにご連絡ください。」
  - EN: If there are any changes, please be sure to contact us at least 3 hours in advance.
  - Feedback: Closing with a cancellation/change policy after a guest has expressed warm gratitude is tone-deaf. The guest is excited and happy — responding with logistical conditions is unwelcoming and diminishes the special feeling of the booking.
