## Turn 1

**Guest:** Can you call a taxi to the airport for me?
**Guest JP:** 空港までタクシーを呼んでいただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。何時のご出発でしょうか？空港までのご手配をいたします。
  - EN: Certainly. What time will you be departing? I will arrange transportation to the airport.
  - Feedback: Immediate agreement and asks for departure time.
- [1] はい、タクシーを呼びます。
  - EN: Yes, I will call a taxi.
  - Feedback: Agrees but doesn't ask for key details.
- [0] 自分で呼んでください。
  - EN: Please call one yourself.
  - Feedback: Concierge should handle this.
- [0] バスの方が安いです。
  - EN: The bus is cheaper.
  - Feedback: Unsolicited advice. Fulfill the request first.

## Turn 2

**System:** You agree to arrange a taxi and ask for the guest's departure time.

**Guest:** My flight is at 2 PM.
**Guest JP:** フライトは14時です。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。2時間前の12時頃のご出発をお勧めいたします。11時55分にタクシーをご用意いたします。
  - EN: Certainly. We recommend departing around noon, two hours before your flight. I will have a taxi ready at 11:55.
  - Feedback: Proactive timing advice and firm booking.
- [1] 分かりました。タクシーを頼みます。
  - EN: Understood. I will arrange a taxi.
  - Feedback: Accepts but doesn't advise on timing.
- [0] 遅いかもしれません。
  - EN: You might be late.
  - Feedback: Vague. Offer concrete advice.
- [0] 電車の方が早いです。
  - EN: The train is faster.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful redirect. Guest chose a taxi.

## Turn 3

**System:** You recommend a departure time and confirm the taxi booking with the guest.

**Guest:** How much will it cost approximately?
**Guest JP:** 大体いくらくらいかかりますか？

### Choices

- [2] 空港まで目安として約4,000円から5,000円程度でございます。交通状況により多少異なる場合がございます。
  - EN: To the airport it is approximately 4,000 to 5,000 yen. It may vary slightly depending on traffic conditions.
  - Feedback: Provides estimate with appropriate caveat.
- [1] 4,000円ぐらいです。
  - EN: It's about 4,000 yen.
  - Feedback: Gives estimate but no qualifier.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Always have general fare knowledge.
- [0] 運転手に聞いてください。
  - EN: Please ask the driver.
  - Feedback: Deflection. Provide info proactively.
