## Turn 1

**Guest:** I have a severe peanut allergy. Can you let the kitchen know?
**Guest JP:** ピーナッツに重度のアレルギーがあります。キッチンに伝えていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。厨房に必ずお伝えいたします。食事の際は都度スタッフにもお申し付けください。
  - EN: Certainly. I will definitely inform the kitchen. Please also let our staff know each time you dine.
  - Feedback: Takes it seriously and advises guest to repeat it.
- [1] はい、伝えます。
  - EN: Yes, I will let them know.
  - Feedback: Will act but doesn't give additional safety advice.
- [0] メニューを確認してください。
  - EN: Please check the menu.
  - Feedback: Shifts responsibility to guest. Kitchen must be told.
- [0] たぶん大丈夫です。
  - EN: It's probably fine.
  - Feedback: Never minimize a severe allergy.

## Turn 2

**System:** You take the allergy seriously and confirm you will notify the kitchen immediately.

### Choices

- [2] 確認してまいります。シェフに直接確認し、安全なメニューをご案内いたします。

  - Feedback: Goes to verify rather than guessing.
- [1] 大丈夫だと思います。
  - EN: I think it should be fine.
  - Feedback: Too uncertain for allergy safety.
- [0] 普通の朝食は大丈夫です。
  - EN: The regular breakfast should be fine.
  - Feedback: Can't confirm without checking. Never guess.
- [0] ピーナッツは料理に使いません。
  - EN: We don't use peanuts in our cooking.
  - Feedback: Without verifying, this could be dangerous.
