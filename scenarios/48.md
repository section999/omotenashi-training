## Turn 1

**Guest:** I'd like to extend my stay by another week.
**Guest JP:** 滞在をもう一週間延長したいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] ご延泊ご希望でございますね。空き状況を確認いたします。少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: You would like to extend your stay. I will check availability. Please wait just a moment.
  - Feedback: Positive framing and immediate check.
- [1] 確認します。
  - EN: I will check.
  - Feedback: Will check but lacks warmth.
- [0] それは難しいかもしれません。
  - EN: That might be difficult.
  - Feedback: Check before expressing doubt.
- [0] 料金はどうしますか？
  - EN: What about the rate?
  - Feedback: Confirm availability first, then discuss rates.

## Turn 2

**System:** You check availability for the additional week and confirm the extension is possible.

**Guest:** Will I get a discount for the extended stay?
**Guest JP:** 延長分は割引になりますか？

### Choices

- [2] 1週間以上のご滞在の場合、長期滞在割引のプランがございます。詳細をご案内いたします。
  - EN: For stays of one week or more, we have a long-stay discount plan. Allow me to explain the details.
  - Feedback: Knows the product and offers the relevant deal.
- [1] 調べてみます。
  - EN: I will look into it.
  - Feedback: Will look into it, which is acceptable.
- [0] 割引はありません。
  - EN: There are no discounts.
  - Feedback: Confirm policy before refusing.
- [0] 料金は変わりません。
  - EN: The rate will not change.
  - Feedback: May be incorrect. Always check for long-stay rates.

## Turn 3

**System:** You inform the guest about the long-stay discount plan and explain the details.

**Guest:** Great. I'll also need laundry service twice a week.
**Guest JP:** わかりました。週2回のランドリーサービスも必要です。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。週2回のランドリーサービスを手配いたします。ご希望の曜日をお聞かせください。
  - EN: Certainly. I will arrange laundry service twice a week. Please let us know which days you prefer.
  - Feedback: Locks in the schedule details.
- [1] はい、手配します。
  - EN: Yes, I will arrange it.
  - Feedback: Agrees but doesn't confirm the schedule.
- [0] ランドリーは追加料金です。
  - EN: Laundry has an additional charge.
  - Feedback: True, but should confirm the service first.
- [0] 週2回は多いですね。
  - EN: Twice a week is quite a lot.
  - Feedback: Inappropriate comment on guest's request.
