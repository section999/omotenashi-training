title: Wake-up Call
description: A guest calls the front desk to request a morning wake-up call.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hello, could I get a wake-up call at 6:30 AM tomorrow?
**Guest JP:** すみません、明日の朝6時半にモーニングコールをお願いできますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。明日の午前6時30分にモーニングコールをお入れいたします。
  - EN: Certainly. We will arrange a wake-up call for you at 6:30 AM tomorrow.
  - Feedback: Perfect. かしこまりました accepts the request formally, and the time is confirmed clearly.
- [1] はい、6時半ですね。承りました。
  - EN: Yes, 6:30, understood.
  - Feedback: Acceptable, but lacks the full confirmation of the full time with AM and the warm closing touch.
- [0] 6時半ですか。わかりました。
  - EN: 6:30? Got it.
  - Feedback: Too casual. わかりました is not appropriate for guest interactions; use かしこまりました.
- [0] アラームは自分でセットできますよ。
  - EN: You can set the alarm yourself.
  - Feedback: Dismissive and unhelpful. Always fulfill reasonable guest requests.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the wake-up call and the guest continues with another question.

**Guest:** Also, is breakfast included with my room?
**Guest JP:** あと、朝食はお部屋に含まれていますか？

### Choices

- [2] 少々お待ちくださいませ。ご予約内容を確認いたします。朝食付きのプランでご予約いただいております。
  - EN: One moment please. Let me check your booking. Your reservation includes breakfast.
  - Feedback: Excellent. Checks the booking before answering and delivers a complete, polite response.
- [1] 朝食はプランによって違います。確認してみます。
  - EN: Breakfast depends on the plan. I'll check.
  - Feedback: Correct approach but phrasing is informal and lacks a warm acknowledgment.
- [0] たぶん含まれていると思います。
  - EN: I think it's probably included.
  - Feedback: Vague and unreliable. Always verify before answering.
- [0] フロントに来て確認してください。
  - EN: Please come to the front desk to check.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful when the guest is already on the phone. Check and answer directly.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm breakfast is included and the guest wraps up the call.

**Guest:** Thank you so much. Oh, could I also get a morning newspaper delivered to my room?
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。あと、朝刊をお部屋にお届けいただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。朝刊もお部屋にお届けいたします。ご利用いただきありがとうございます。ごゆっくりおやすみくださいませ。
  - EN: Certainly. We will deliver the morning newspaper to your room as well. Thank you for calling. Please have a restful night.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Acknowledges both requests, thanks the guest, and closes warmly.
- [1] わかりました。新聞をお届けします。
  - EN: Understood. We will deliver the newspaper.
  - Feedback: Correct but missing the warm closing and thanks, which are expected in hotel service.
- [0] 新聞は1階に置いてあります。
  - EN: Newspapers are available on the first floor.
  - Feedback: Ignores the guest's request for room delivery. Always fulfil what was asked.
- [0] 新聞のサービスはないと思います。
  - EN: I don't think we have a newspaper service.
  - Feedback: Presumptuous. Verify before saying a service is unavailable.
