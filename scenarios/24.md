## Turn 1

**Guest:** We need a conference room for a meeting tomorrow.
**Guest JP:** 明日の会議のために会議室が必要です。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。何名様でご利用の予定でしょうか？また、ご希望のお時間はございますか？
  - EN: Certainly. How many people will be using the room? And do you have a preferred time?
  - Feedback: Asks for capacity and time to find best fit.
- [1] 予約しますか？
  - EN: Would you like to make a reservation?
  - Feedback: Too simple. Gather details first.
- [0] 会議室は高いですよ。
  - EN: Conference rooms are quite expensive.
  - Feedback: Leads with cost before understanding needs.
- [0] 今日は遅いので明日相談しましょう。
  - EN: It's late today, so let's discuss it tomorrow.
  - Feedback: The guest needs it tomorrow. Act now.

## Turn 2

**System:** You ask for the number of attendees and preferred time to find the right room.

**Guest:** About 10 people, from 9 AM to noon.
**Guest JP:** 10名程度で、9時から正午までです。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。10名様、9時から12時でございますね。会議室Aをご用意できます。プロジェクターも含まれます。
  - EN: Certainly. Ten people, from 9 AM to noon, correct? We can prepare Conference Room A. A projector is included.
  - Feedback: Confirms all details and mentions included equipment.
- [1] 確認します。
  - EN: I will check.
  - Feedback: Will check but doesn't confirm details back.
- [0] その部屋は大きすぎます。
  - EN: That room is too large.
  - Feedback: Potentially unhelpful critique without an alternative.
- [0] 10人は多いです。
  - EN: Ten people is a lot.
  - Feedback: Inappropriate personal comment.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm Conference Room A is available for 10 people from 9 AM to noon.

**Guest:** Does it include a projector and whiteboard?
**Guest JP:** プロジェクターとホワイトボードは含まれていますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、プロジェクターとホワイトボードは標準装備でございます。ご要望があればWiFiも完備しています。
  - EN: Yes, a projector and whiteboard are standard equipment. WiFi is also available if needed.
  - Feedback: Confirms equipment and proactively adds WiFi.
- [1] はい、あります。
  - EN: Yes, there are.
  - Feedback: Confirms but doesn't volunteer extra info.
- [0] 別途料金がかかります。
  - EN: There will be an additional charge.
  - Feedback: Equipment is often included. Verify before charging.
- [0] ホワイトボードはないかもしれません。
  - EN: There may not be a whiteboard.
  - Feedback: Uncertain. Know your room facilities.
