## Turn 1

**Guest:** Do you have a laundry service?
**Guest JP:** ランドリーサービスはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、ランドリーサービスをご用意しております。ランドリーバッグとご料金表がお部屋にございますのでご確認くださいませ。
  - EN: Yes, we offer a laundry service. A laundry bag and price list are in your room, so please take a look.
  - Feedback: Confirms service and directs to in-room materials politely.
- [1] はい、あります。
  - EN: Yes, we do.
  - Feedback: Confirms but gives no extra information.
- [0] コインランドリーは地下です。
  - EN: The coin laundry is in the basement.
  - Feedback: May be irrelevant if they want full service.
- [0] 値段が高いですよ。
  - EN: It's quite expensive.
  - Feedback: Unsolicited pricing warning. Let guest decide.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the laundry service and mention the bag and price list in the room.

**Guest:** How long does it take?
**Guest JP:** どのくらいかかりますか？

### Choices

- [2] 通常1日でご返却いたします。お急ぎの場合は、特急サービスもございます。
  - EN: We normally return items within one day. If you are in a hurry, we also offer an express service.
  - Feedback: Standard and express options both explained.
- [1] 1日です。
  - EN: It takes one day.
  - Feedback: Gives timeline but misses express option.
- [0] 明日以降になります。
  - EN: It will be tomorrow or later.
  - Feedback: Vague and doesn't mention options.
- [0] 依頼してから考えます。
  - EN: We'll think about it after you request it.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful non-answer.

## Turn 3

**System:** You explain the standard one-day turnaround and mention the express option.

**Guest:** I need my shirts back by tomorrow morning. Is that possible?
**Guest JP:** シャツを明日の朝までに返してほしいのですが。可能ですか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。特急サービスをご利用いただければ、明朝ご返却できます。別途料金が発生いたします。
  - EN: Certainly. If you use the express service, we can return them by tomorrow morning. There will be an additional charge.
  - Feedback: Confirms it's possible with honest disclosure of extra charge.
- [1] できると思います。
  - EN: I think we can.
  - Feedback: Too uncertain. Confirm or check.
- [0] 明日は無理です。
  - EN: Tomorrow is not possible.
  - Feedback: Refuses without exploring express option.
- [0] それは難しいです。
  - EN: That is difficult.
  - Feedback: Negative without offering alternatives.
