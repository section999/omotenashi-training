## Turn 1

**Guest:** I can't seem to connect to the WiFi in my room.
**Guest JP:** お部屋でWiFiに接続できないのですが。

### Choices

- [2] ご不便をおかけして申し訳ございません。接続情報を再度ご確認いたしましょうか？パスワードはお持ちですか？
  - EN: We are sorry for the inconvenience. Shall we verify your connection details once more? Do you have the password?
  - Feedback: Apologizes and starts troubleshooting politely.
- [1] パスワードは分かりますか？
  - EN: Do you know the password?
  - Feedback: Relevant question but skips the apology.
- [0] WiFiは今問題ないです。
  - EN: There are no issues with the WiFi right now.
  - Feedback: Don't contradict the guest without investigating.
- [0] 携帯電話会社に聞いてください。
  - EN: Please contact your mobile carrier.
  - Feedback: Completely wrong direction. This is a hotel issue.

## Turn 2

**System:** You apologize for the inconvenience and begin troubleshooting the connection with the guest.

**Guest:** I have the password, but it still says incorrect network.
**Guest JP:** パスワードはあるのですが、ネットワークが正しくないと表示されます。

### Choices

- [2] ご確認ありがとうございます。技術担当をすぐにお部屋に伺わせます。あるいはロビーのWiFiをご利用いただけます。
  - EN: Thank you for checking. I will send a technician to your room immediately. You may also use the lobby WiFi in the meantime.
  - Feedback: Offers tech support and interim solution.
- [1] 担当者を呼びます。
  - EN: I will call a technician.
  - Feedback: Escalates correctly but no interim solution.
- [0] もう一度試してください。
  - EN: Please try again.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful if they've already tried.
- [0] 古い端末は繋がりにくいです。
  - EN: Older devices can have trouble connecting.
  - Feedback: Blaming the guest's device is inappropriate.

## Turn 3

- [2] はい、ロビーのWiFiはビデオ通話に十分な速度がございます。ご不便をおかけして大変申し訳ございません。

  - Feedback: Uncertain. Know your facilities.
