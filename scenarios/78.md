title: VIP Guest Special Arrangements
description: A VIP guest's personal assistant calls ahead with a detailed list of requirements for their upcoming stay.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hello, I'm calling on behalf of Ms Tanaka. She'll be staying at your hotel next week and has several specific requirements I'd like to discuss.
**Guest JP:** もしもし、田中様の代理でお電話しております。来週ご宿泊の予定ですが、いくつかの特別なご要望についてお話ししたいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] ご連絡いただきありがとうございます。田中様のご来館を心よりお待ち申し上げております。ご要望を詳しくお伺いするとともに、確実にご対応できるよう丁寧に記録いたします。どうぞご遠慮なくお聞かせください。
  - EN: Thank you very much for calling. We very much look forward to welcoming Ms Tanaka. I will listen carefully to all requirements and make sure everything is recorded accurately so we can fulfil each one. Please go ahead.
  - Feedback: Expresses genuine welcome, commits to careful documentation, and invites the assistant to share everything — sets exactly the right tone for handling a VIP's pre-arrival requirements.
- [1] はい、ご要望を伺います。お聞かせください。
  - EN: Yes, I will take note of the requirements. Please go ahead.
  - Feedback: Receptive and polite, but lacks the warmth and forward anticipation expected for a VIP pre-arrival call.
- [0] ご要望によっては対応できない場合もございますので、ご了承ください。
  - EN: Please be aware that some requests may not be able to be accommodated.
  - Feedback: Flagging potential refusals before even hearing the requirements is unwelcoming and creates an unnecessarily negative first impression with a VIP's representative.
- [0] 担当部署にお繋ぎします。
  - EN: I will transfer you to the relevant department.
  - Feedback: Immediately transferring a VIP pre-arrival call without taking any details yourself feels dismissive and may frustrate an assistant trying to communicate efficiently.

## Turn 2

**System:** You note all requirements — hypoallergenic bedding, specific mineral water brand, garden-facing room, no feather pillows — and confirm what can be arranged; the assistant now asks that the guest not be disturbed by housekeeping before 11 AM.

**Guest:** Thank you. Also, Ms Tanaka must not be disturbed by housekeeping before 11 AM under any circumstances.
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。また、田中様は午前11時より前はいかなる場合もハウスキーピングに邪魔されてはなりません。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。ハウスキーピングは午前11時以降にのみ伺うよう、担当チームに厳に申し伝えます。また緊急の場合にのみ例外となる旨、事前にご確認いただけますでしょうか。それ以外はいかなる場合も11時以前にはお部屋にはお伺いいたしません。
  - EN: Certainly. I will give strict instructions to the housekeeping team to service the room only after 11 AM. May I just confirm that exceptions would be made only in the case of a genuine emergency? Other than that, we will ensure the room is not accessed before 11 AM under any circumstances.
  - Feedback: Confirms the instruction precisely, adds the sensible caveat about emergencies to avoid misunderstanding, and provides full assurance — thorough and professional handling.
- [1] 承知いたしました。ハウスキーピングに11時以降にするよう伝えます。
  - EN: Understood. I will instruct housekeeping to come after 11 AM.
  - Feedback: Records the request correctly but does not confirm it back in detail or address the emergency exception, which could cause confusion later.
- [0] ハウスキーピングのスケジュールはこちらで決めておりますので、保証はいたしかねます。
  - EN: Housekeeping scheduling is managed on our side, so we cannot guarantee this.
  - Feedback: Refusing to accommodate a clear and simple instruction for a VIP guest is unacceptable — this request is entirely within the hotel's control.
- [0] 「起こさないでください」のサインをドアにかけてください。
  - EN: Please hang the Do Not Disturb sign on the door.
  - Feedback: Telling the VIP's assistant to use the standard DND sign rather than confirming a guaranteed staff instruction is insufficient for VIP-level service.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm all arrangements are in place; the assistant asks for a direct contact number in case of issues during the stay.

**Guest:** Excellent. Could you give me a direct number we can call if any issues arise during Ms Tanaka's stay?
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。田中様のご滞在中に何か問題があった場合に、直接連絡できる番号を教えていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。私の直通番号と、24時間対応のデューティーマネージャーの番号をお伝えいたします。いずれの番号でも田中様のお名前をお申し付けいただければ、ただちに優先対応いたします。どうかご滞在中、何かございましたらご遠慮なくご連絡くださいませ。
  - EN: Of course. I will give you both my direct number and the 24-hour duty manager's number. If you call either line and mention Ms Tanaka's name, your call will be given immediate priority. Please do not hesitate to contact us at any time during the stay.
  - Feedback: Provides two contact options, explains how to use them for priority handling, and gives a warm open invitation — exactly the level of proactive care a VIP arrangement warrants.
- [1] デューティーマネージャーの番号をお教えします。24時間対応しております。
  - EN: I will give you the duty manager's number. It is available 24 hours.
  - Feedback: Useful and correct, but only provides one number and does not specify how the call will be handled in relation to the VIP booking.
- [0] 通常のフロントデスクの番号におかけください。
  - EN: Please call the main front desk number.
  - Feedback: Directing a VIP's assistant to the general front desk without any priority arrangement is insufficient — VIP guests require dedicated contact handling.
- [0] 問題がございましたら、ご滞在中に直接フロントにお越しください。
  - EN: If there are any issues, please come to the front desk during the stay.
  - Feedback: Asking a VIP guest's team to physically visit the front desk for every issue entirely defeats the purpose of pre-stay coordination.
