id: 37
title: Lost Key Card
description: Guest has lost their room key card.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** I think I lost my key card.
**Guest JP:** キーカードをなくしてしまったと思います。

### Choices

- [2] ご不便をおかけして申し訳ございません。すぐに新しいカードをご用意いたします。お部屋番号をお聞かせください。
  - EN: We apologize for the inconvenience. I will prepare a new card right away. Could you tell me your room number?
  - Feedback: Apologizes and moves straight to resolution.
- [1] どこでなくしましたか？
  - EN: Where did you lose it?
  - Feedback: Relevant question but replacement should come first.
- [0] 自分で探してください。
  - EN: Please look for it yourself.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful. Issue a replacement immediately.
- [0] カードは高いです。
  - EN: Key cards are expensive.
  - Feedback: Inappropriate to mention cost first.

## Turn 2

**System:** You apologize and begin the process of issuing a replacement key card.

**Guest:** Room 308.
**Guest JP:** 308号室です。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。308号室のカードを再発行いたします。身分証明書をご提示いただけますか？
  - EN: Thank you. I will reissue a key card for Room 308. Could you please show me your identification?
  - Feedback: Secure procedure: requests ID before reissue.
- [1] はい、カードを作ります。
  - EN: Yes, I will make a new card.
  - Feedback: Will reissue but doesn't ask for ID.
- [0] なぜなくしましたか？
  - EN: Why did you lose it?
  - Feedback: Blaming the guest is inappropriate.
- [0] 手数料がかかります。
  - EN: There will be a fee.
  - Feedback: Should issue first and explain any fee after.

## Turn 3

**System:** You pull up Room 308 and request the guest's ID before reissuing the card.

**Guest:** Here's my passport.
**Guest JP:** こちらがパスポートです。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。こちらが新しいカードでございます。お気をつけください。
  - EN: Thank you. Here is your new key card. Please take care.
  - Feedback: Polite handover with a gentle reminder.
- [1] はい、どうぞ。
  - EN: Yes, here you are.
  - Feedback: Hands it over but no warmth.
- [0] 次はなくさないように。
  - EN: Please don't lose it next time.
  - Feedback: Scolding a guest is inappropriate.
- [0] 古いカードは使えません。
  - EN: The old card cannot be used.
  - Feedback: Unnecessary information that sounds accusatory.
