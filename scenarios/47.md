description: A large group arrives to check in simultaneously.

## Turn 1

**Guest:** We're a group of 20 from the Yamamoto company. We all need to check in.
**Guest JP:** 山本商事の20名のグループです。全員チェックインしたいのですが。

### Choices

- [2] 山本商事様、ようこそいらっしゃいませ。グループチェックインのご準備をしております。ご担当の方にまずご対応いたします。
  - EN: Welcome, Yamamoto Corporation. We are prepared for your group check-in. We will assist the group representative first.
  - Feedback: Organized and welcoming for large group, correctly addresses the company.
- [1] 20名ですか？少し時間がかかります。
  - EN: Twenty people? It will take a little time.
  - Feedback: Manages expectations but lacks warmth.
- [0] 順番に並んでください。
  - EN: Please line up in order.
  - Feedback: Too casual and inefficient for a pre-arranged group.
- [0] 予約はありますか？
  - EN: Do you have a reservation?
  - Feedback: Group bookings are pre-arranged. Should know this.

## Turn 2

**System:** You greet the group and direct the representative to the desk to begin the check-in process.

**Guest:** Can we get all the room keys at once?
**Guest JP:** 全員分の部屋の鍵を一度にいただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、全員分のルームキーを事前にご準備しております。こちらの封筒にお名前と部屋番号が記入されております。
  - EN: Yes, we have prepared room keys for everyone in advance. Each envelope has the name and room number written on it.
  - Feedback: Pre-prepared keys show excellent group handling.
- [1] 準備します。少し待ってください。
  - EN: I will prepare them. Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Will prepare but missed the opportunity to pre-stage.
- [0] 一人ずつ対応いたします。
  - EN: We will assist one person at a time.
  - Feedback: Inefficient for a 20-person group.
- [0] それは難しいです。
  - EN: That is difficult.
  - Feedback: Don't refuse reasonable group requests.

## Turn 3

**System:** You hand over pre-prepared key envelopes labeled with each member's name and room number.

**Guest:** We also need a meeting room for tomorrow morning.
**Guest JP:** 明日の朝に会議室も必要です。

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。明日の朝、何名様でご利用でしょうか？ご希望のお時間もお聞かせください。
  - EN: Certainly. How many people will be using it tomorrow morning? Please also let us know your preferred time.
  - Feedback: Handles multiple group needs efficiently.
- [1] 確認します。
  - EN: I will check.
  - Feedback: Will handle but lacks engagement.
- [0] それはコンシェルジュに聞いてください。
  - EN: Please ask the concierge about that.
  - Feedback: Don't redirect unnecessarily at check-in.
- [0] 難しいかもしれません。
  - EN: It might be difficult.
  - Feedback: Check first before suggesting difficulty.
