id: 99
title: Lost and Found — Forgotten Umbrella
description: Guest enquires about an umbrella they left in the lobby the previous day.
---

## Turn 1

**System:** A guest approaches the front desk and believes they left their umbrella in the lobby yesterday.

**Guest:** I think I left my umbrella in the lobby yesterday. Has anything been handed in?
**Guest JP:** 昨日、ロビーに傘を忘れてしまったのですが、届いていますか？

### Choices

- [2] お調べいたします。昨日おおよそ何時ごろロビーにいらっしゃいましたか？また、傘のお色や特徴をお教えいただけますか？
  - EN: Let me check for you. Approximately what time were you in the lobby yesterday, and could you describe the colour and any features of the umbrella?
  - Feedback: Gathers the two most useful details — time and description — to narrow the search efficiently.
- [1] はい、お調べいたします。少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Yes, I will check for you. Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Polite and correct, though asking for a description first will make the search faster and more accurate.
- [0] 落とし物は最寄りの交番にお問い合わせください。
  - EN: For lost items, please enquire at the nearest police box.
  - Feedback: The item was left in the hotel — check the hotel's own lost and found before directing the guest elsewhere.
- [0] 昨日のことですと、見つからない可能性が高いです。
  - EN: Since it was yesterday, there is a good chance it may not be found.
  - Feedback: Defeatist before even looking — always conduct a thorough search before suggesting the item is likely gone.

## Turn 2

**System:** Guest provides a description. Staff checks the lost and found and finds a match.

**Guest:** It's a black foldable umbrella with a wooden handle.
**Guest JP:** 黒の折りたたみ傘で、持ち手が木製のものです。

### Choices

- [2] ただいま確認いたしました。黒の折りたたみ傘で木製の持ち手のもの、お預かりしております。お受け取りの際に確認のため、お名前とお部屋番号をお教えいただけますか？
  - EN: I have just checked, and we do have a black foldable umbrella with a wooden handle. To confirm ownership when you collect it, may I have your name and room number?
  - Feedback: Matches the description, confirms the find, and requests verification before handing it over — careful and professional.
- [1] 一致するものがございました。フロントにてお受け取りください。
  - EN: We have a matching umbrella. Please collect it at the front desk.
  - Feedback: Good outcome — requesting verification before handing it over is important procedure that was skipped here.
- [0] 複数の傘をお預かりしておりますので、フロントにてご確認ください。
  - EN: We have several umbrellas in lost and found; please come to the front desk to identify yours.
  - Feedback: Better than nothing, but try to confirm over the phone or call to save the guest an unnecessary trip.
- [0] 申し訳ありませんが、該当するものは見つかりませんでした。
  - EN: We are sorry, but we could not find a matching item.
  - Feedback: If genuinely not found, assure the guest it will be kept on record and check other storage areas before giving up.

## Turn 3

**System:** Ownership is verified. Guest comes to the front desk to collect the umbrella and expresses relief.

**Guest:** Oh thank goodness. This umbrella was a gift — I'm so relieved.
**Guest JP:** よかった。この傘、プレゼントでいただいたものなので、本当に助かりました。

### Choices

- [2] それはよかったございました。大切なものがお手元に戻り、私どもも安心いたしました。またのお越しを心よりお待ち申し上げております。
  - EN: I am so glad we could help. We are delighted such a precious item has been returned to you. We sincerely look forward to welcoming you again.
  - Feedback: Warm, sincere, and ends on a genuinely human note — exactly the right tone for this happy resolution.
- [1] お役に立てて光栄です。またのご利用をお待ちしております。
  - EN: We are glad to have been of service. We look forward to your next visit.
  - Feedback: Polite and appropriate — a little more warmth acknowledging the personal significance of the item would be perfect.
- [0] 保管期間は1週間でございますので、次回はお早めにお越しください。
  - EN: Items are kept for one week, so please come sooner next time.
  - Feedback: The guest is collecting it right now — this remark is unnecessary and breaks the warm atmosphere of the moment.
- [0] 次回はお忘れにならないようお気をつけください。
  - EN: Please be careful not to forget your belongings next time.
  - Feedback: Scolding a guest who has just thanked you warmly leaves a poor final impression — end on a positive note.
