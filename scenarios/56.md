title: Dietary Restrictions
description: A guest calls ahead of dinner to report a shellfish allergy.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, I have a shellfish allergy. I wanted to let you know before dinner tonight.
**Guest JP:** すみません、甲殻類アレルギーがあります。今夜の夕食前にお伝えしたくてご連絡しました。

### Choices

- [2] ご連絡いただきありがとうございます。大切なことを事前にお知らせくださいまして、誠にありがとうございます。レストランにしっかり申し伝えます。
  - EN: Thank you very much for letting us know. We truly appreciate you informing us in advance. I will pass this information to the restaurant without fail.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Thanks the guest sincerely, acknowledges the importance of the allergy, and commits to informing the kitchen.
- [1] わかりました。レストランに伝えておきます。
  - EN: Understood. I'll let the restaurant know.
  - Feedback: Correct action but lacks appreciation for the advance notice, which is an important safety and hospitality detail.
- [0] 食事の時にレストランで直接言ってください。
  - EN: Please tell the restaurant directly when you eat.
  - Feedback: Passes the responsibility to the guest instead of proactively coordinating with the kitchen.
- [0] アレルギーですか。大変ですね。
  - EN: An allergy? That must be tough.
  - Feedback: Sympathetic but takes no action. Always commit to a specific follow-up.

## Turn 2

**System:** You note the allergy and the guest asks whether the kitchen can accommodate them.

**Guest:** Can the chef actually prepare something safe for me?
**Guest JP:** シェフは私のために安全なお料理を作っていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。ただいまレストランの担当者に確認いたします。対応可能なメニューについてご案内できますよう、折り返しご連絡いたします。
  - EN: Certainly. I will check with the restaurant team right away. We will contact you back to advise on which menu options will be suitable for you.
  - Feedback: Excellent. Takes immediate action, sets clear expectations with a callback, and puts the guest at ease.
- [1] たぶん大丈夫です。シェフに伝えます。
  - EN: It should probably be fine. I'll tell the chef.
  - Feedback: たぶん (probably) is too uncertain for an allergy situation. Always confirm properly before reassuring.
- [0] メニューを見れば何が入っているかわかりますよ。
  - EN: If you look at the menu you can see what's in the dishes.
  - Feedback: Puts the burden of managing an allergy on the guest. The hotel should actively assist.
- [0] 甲殻類が入っていないものを選んでください。
  - EN: Please choose something that doesn't contain shellfish.
  - Feedback: Again places the responsibility on the guest. The staff should guide and coordinate.

## Turn 3

**System:** You have confirmed with the kitchen, and now the guest also mentions they are vegetarian.

**Guest:** Actually, I should also mention — I'm vegetarian.
**Guest JP:** あと、実はベジタリアンでもあります。

### Choices

- [2] 承りました。甲殻類アレルギーとベジタリアン対応、両方しっかりレストランに申し伝えます。ご安心してお越しくださいませ。
  - EN: Noted. I will make sure to pass both the shellfish allergy and your vegetarian requirements to the restaurant. Please come to dinner with complete peace of mind.
  - Feedback: Perfect. Confirms both requirements, uses 承りました to acknowledge formally, and closes with a reassuring invitation.
- [1] わかりました。ベジタリアンですね。レストランに追加で伝えます。
  - EN: Understood. Vegetarian. I will pass that along to the restaurant as well.
  - Feedback: Correct but the response lacks warmth. The guest should be reassured that both needs will be fully met.
- [0] ベジタリアンは難しいかもしれません。
  - EN: Vegetarian might be difficult.
  - Feedback: Vague and discouraging without verifying with the kitchen first.
- [0] 二つも制限があるんですか。
  - EN: You have two restrictions?
  - Feedback: Sounds exasperated. Never make a guest feel their needs are an inconvenience.
