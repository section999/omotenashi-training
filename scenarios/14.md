## Turn 1

**Guest:** We arrived early. Can we check in now? It's only 10 AM.
**Guest JP:** 早めに到着しました。今すぐチェックインできますか？まだ10時なのですが。

### Choices

- [2] お早いお越しでございます。お部屋の準備状況を確認いたします。少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: You have arrived early. I will check on the status of your room. Please wait just a moment.
  - Feedback: Acknowledges early arrival and offers to check.
- [1] チェックインは14時からです。
  - EN: Check-in starts at 2 PM.
  - Feedback: States policy but doesn't offer any help.
- [0] 無理です。
  - EN: That's not possible.
  - Feedback: Too blunt. Always check room availability first.
- [0] ロビーで待ってください。
  - EN: Please wait in the lobby.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful if said without checking first.

## Turn 2

**System:** You acknowledge the early arrival and check the room preparation status.

**Guest:** We've been traveling all night. We're really tired.
**Guest JP:** 一晩中移動していました。とても疲れています。

### Choices

- [2] 長旅でお疲れのことと存じます。できる限り早くお部屋のご準備をいたします。しばらくラウンジでおくつろぎください。
  - EN: You must be tired from your long journey. We will prepare your room as quickly as possible. Please relax in the lounge for a while.
  - Feedback: Shows empathy and offers lounge as comfort.
- [1] 分かりました。少し待ってください。
  - EN: Understood. Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Empathetic intent but no concrete offer.
- [0] 14時まで待ってください。
  - EN: Please wait until 2 PM.
  - Feedback: Rigid and unsympathetic given the situation.
- [0] それは大変でしたね。
  - EN: That must have been tough.
  - Feedback: Sympathetic but no action follows.

## Turn 3

**System:** You show empathy and offer the lounge as a comfortable place to rest while the room is prepared.

**Guest:** Thank you. We'll wait in the lounge.
**Guest JP:** ありがとうございます。ラウンジで待ちます。

  - EN: Yes, I will call you when it's ready.
  - Feedback: Will notify but misses chance to offer something.

  - EN: Understood.
  - Feedback: Passive. Add a comforting gesture.
