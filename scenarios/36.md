## Turn 1

**Guest:** Has any mail or packages arrived for me?
**Guest JP:** 私宛の郵便物や荷物は届いていますか？

### Choices

- [2] お名前をお聞かせいただけますか？確認いたします。
  - EN: May I have your name? I will check.
  - Feedback: Asks for name to check properly.
- [1] 確認します。
  - EN: I will check.
  - Feedback: Will check but doesn't ask for name.
- [0] 何も来ていないと思います。
  - EN: I don't think anything has arrived.
  - Feedback: Don't assume without checking.
- [0] 郵便物はここでは扱いません。
  - EN: We don't handle mail here.
  - Feedback: Hotels typically do receive guest packages.

## Turn 2

**System:** You ask for the guest's name to look up any packages or mail at the front desk.

**Guest:** It's under Johnson, room 512.
**Guest JP:** ジョンソン、512号室です。

### Choices

- [2] ジョンソン様、512号室でございますね。はい、本日午前中に小包が届いております。

  - Feedback: Confirms the package has arrived with polite repetition.
- [1] 確認します。少し待ってください。
  - EN: I will check. Please wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Will check but could do it faster with name given.
- [0] ないかもしれません。
  - EN: There may not be anything.
  - Feedback: Check before guessing.
- [0] 部屋に持っていきましょうか？
  - EN: Shall I bring it to your room?
  - Feedback: Gets ahead before confirming it exists.

## Turn 3
