## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, I have a reservation under Smith.
**Guest JP:** スミスと申します。予約しております。

### Choices

- [2] スミス様、いらっしゃいませ。ご予約を確認いたします。少々お待ちくださいませ。
  - EN: Welcome, Mr. Smith. I will confirm your reservation. One moment please.
  - Feedback: Perfect! Warm welcome with polite confirmation.
- [1] はい、確認します。
  - EN: Yes, I will check.
  - Feedback: Correct but too brief. Add a greeting.
- [0] 名前は何ですか？
  - EN: What is your name?
  - Feedback: Too direct. Use お名前 and polite form.
- [0] ちょっと待って。
  - EN: Wait a moment.
  - Feedback: Very rude. Never use casual speech with guests.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the reservation and begin processing check-in.

**Guest:** Could I get a room on a higher floor with a view?
**Guest JP:** 眺めの良い高層階のお部屋をいただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。高層階のお部屋をご用意いたします。
  - EN: Of course. I will prepare a room on a higher floor for you.
  - Feedback: Perfect use of ございます and いたします.
- [1] 高い階の部屋にします。
  - EN: I will make it a room on a high floor.
  - Feedback: Understandable but lacks polite keigo.
- [0] 空いてるか分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know if there's availability.
  - Feedback: Unhelpful. Always offer to check.
- [0] それは無理です。
  - EN: That is not possible.
  - Feedback: Never refuse without checking first.

## Turn 3

**System:** You assign a higher-floor room with a view and hand over the key card.

**Guest:** Great, what time is breakfast?
**Guest JP:** 朝食は何時からですか？

### Choices

- [2] 朝食は7時から10時まででございます。レストランは2階にございます。
  - EN: Breakfast is served from 7 to 10. The restaurant is on the second floor.
  - Feedback: Complete, polite answer with location.
- [1] 7時から10時です。
  - EN: It's from 7 to 10.
  - Feedback: Correct time but missing location and politeness.
- [0] メニューを見てください。
  - EN: Please look at the menu.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Answer directly.
- [0] 知りません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Unacceptable. Always know basic hotel information.
