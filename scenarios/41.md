title: Baby Crib Request
description: Guests with a baby need a crib in their room.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** We have a 6-month-old. Can we get a baby crib?
**Guest JP:** 生後6ヶ月の赤ちゃんがいます。ベビーベッドをお借りできますか？

### Choices

- [2] もちろんでございます。ベビーベッドをお部屋にご用意いたします。お部屋番号をお聞かせください。
  - EN: Of course. We will set up a baby crib in your room. May I have your room number?
  - Feedback: Warmly accommodates family needs.
- [1] あります。持ってきます。
  - EN: We have one. I'll bring it.
  - Feedback: Correct but lacks warmth for a family.
- [0] 数が少ないので確認します。
  - EN: We have limited stock so I'll check.
  - Feedback: Should check without raising uncertainty first.
- [0] 追加料金がかかりますよ。
  - EN: There will be an additional charge.
  - Feedback: Leads with cost instead of service.

## Turn 2

**System:** You warmly arrange for a baby crib to be set up in the guests' room.

**Guest:** Is there anywhere to warm baby food in the hotel?
**Guest JP:** ホテル内で離乳食を温める場所はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、ルームサービスにてお湯をご用意することも可能ですし、キッチン付きのお部屋への変更もご相談いただけます。
  - EN: Yes, we can provide hot water through room service, and we can also discuss switching to a room with a kitchenette.
  - Feedback: Offers two practical solutions.
- [1] 電子レンジがあります。
  - EN: There is a microwave.
  - Feedback: Gives one solution but not very specific.
- [0] 外で買ってください。
  - EN: Please buy it outside.
  - Feedback: Dismissive to a parent with a baby.
- [0] 食事はレストランで。
  - EN: Meals should be at the restaurant.
  - Feedback: Doesn't address the baby food concern.

## Turn 3

**System:** You offer practical options for warming baby food and the guests are relieved.

**Guest:** Thank you so much. Traveling with a baby is tough.
**Guest JP:** 本当にありがとうございます。赤ちゃん連れの旅は大変です。

### Choices

- [2] 本当に大変でいらっしゃいますね。ご滞在中、何かお手伝いできることがあれば、いつでもお申し付けください。
  - EN: That truly is a lot of work. Please let us know anytime during your stay if there is anything we can help with.
  - Feedback: Genuine empathy and open offer of support.
- [1] 大変ですね。
  - EN: That must be tough.
  - Feedback: Empathizes but doesn't offer further help.
- [0] 赤ちゃんはかわいいですね。
  - EN: What a cute baby.
  - Feedback: Nice but not relevant to the hospitality context.
- [0] 頑張ってください。
  - EN: Good luck.
  - Feedback: Too casual and doesn't offer hotel assistance.
