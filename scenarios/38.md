title: Valet Parking
description: Guest uses the hotel valet parking service.
---

## Turn 1

**Guest:** Hi, can you park my car?
**Guest JP:** 車を駐車していただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。バレーパーキングでご利用でございますね。こちらが引換券でございます。
  - EN: Certainly. You'll be using the valet parking service. Here is your claim ticket.
  - Feedback: Smooth, professional valet greeting with ticket.
- [1] はい、停めます。
  - EN: Yes, I will park it.
  - Feedback: Accepts but skips the ticket.
- [0] 駐車場はあちらです。
  - EN: The parking lot is over there.
  - Feedback: Misdirects guest to self-park when they asked for valet.
- [0] 高いですけど、大丈夫ですか？
  - EN: It's expensive, is that alright?
  - Feedback: Don't question the guest's choice.

## Turn 2

**System:** You take the vehicle and hand the guest a claim ticket for retrieval.

**Guest:** Is there anything I should know about my car?
**Guest JP:** 車について何か注意事項はありますか？

### Choices

- [2] お車は安全にお預かりいたします。何か特記事項がございましたら、お申し付けください。
  - EN: We will take good care of your vehicle. Please let us know if there is anything special we should be aware of.
  - Feedback: Reassuring and invites any relevant info.
- [1] 大丈夫です。
  - EN: It's fine.
  - Feedback: Reassuring but generic.
- [0] 別に何もないです。
  - EN: There's nothing in particular.
  - Feedback: Dismissive. Let the guest share any concerns.
- [0] ガソリンを入れていいですか？
  - EN: Would it be alright if we fill up the gas?
  - Feedback: Odd question unless offered as a service.

## Turn 3

**System:** You reassure the guest their car will be well cared for and invite any special instructions.

**Guest:** When I need the car, how do I retrieve it?
**Guest JP:** 車が必要なとき、どうやって取り出せばいいですか？

### Choices

- [2] こちらの引換券をフロントにお持ちいただければ、15分以内にご用意いたします。
  - EN: If you bring this claim ticket to the front desk, we will have your car ready within 15 minutes.
  - Feedback: Clear retrieval procedure with timing.
- [1] 引換券を見せてください。
  - EN: Please show me the claim ticket.
  - Feedback: Correct process but lacks detail.
- [0] 呼んだら来ます。
  - EN: We'll come when you call.
  - Feedback: Too casual and vague.
- [0] フロントで聞いてください。
  - EN: Please ask at the front desk.
  - Feedback: You should explain the process directly.
