## Turn 1

**Guest:** I'll be checking out today. It's been a wonderful two weeks.
**Guest JP:** 本日チェックアウトします。素晴らしい2週間でした。

### Choices

- [2] ありがとうございます。2週間ご滞在いただき、誠にありがとうございました。またのお越しを心よりお待ち申し上げております。
  - EN: Thank you. We are sincerely grateful for your two-week stay. We look forward to welcoming you again.
  - Feedback: Heartfelt, sincere farewell for a long-staying guest.
- [1] ありがとうございました。
  - EN: Thank you very much.
  - Feedback: Polite but doesn't acknowledge the long stay.
- [0] チェックアウトの手続きをします。
  - EN: I will proceed with check-out.
  - Feedback: Skips the human moment entirely.
- [0] 早いですね。
  - EN: That was quick.
  - Feedback: Odd comment for someone staying two weeks.

## Turn 2

**System:** You express heartfelt gratitude for the guest's two-week stay and begin the check-out process.

**Guest:** Is there anything I need to do before leaving?
**Guest JP:** 出発前に何かすることはありますか？

### Choices

- [2] ルームキーのご返却と精算のみでございます。お荷物のお運びもご用意できます。お部屋にお忘れ物はございませんか？
  - EN: Just return your room key and settle the bill. We can also help carry your luggage. Have you left anything in the room?
  - Feedback: Smooth, complete check-out guidance with an extra offer and a standard farewell courtesy.
- [1] カードを返してください。
  - EN: Please return the key card.
  - Feedback: Correct but misses other check-out steps.
- [0] 何もしなくて大丈夫です。
  - EN: There's nothing you need to do.
  - Feedback: Skips key return and billing — essential check-out steps.
- [0] 精算書を見てください。
  - EN: Please look at the statement.
  - Feedback: Abrupt. Walk through it together with the guest.

## Turn 3

**System:** You walk the guest through key return and billing, and offer luggage assistance.

**Guest:** I'll definitely be back. Thank you for everything.
**Guest JP:** また必ず来ます。本当にありがとうございました。

### Choices

- [2] こちらこそ、ありがとうございました。またのご来館を、スタッフ一同心よりお待ちしております。お気をつけてお帰りくださいませ。
  - EN: Thank you as well. All of our staff look forward to welcoming you again. Please take care on your way home.
  - Feedback: Perfect farewell from the entire team.
- [1] またお越しください。
  - EN: Please come again.
  - Feedback: Polite invitation but less heartfelt.
- [0] どういたしまして。
  - EN: You're welcome.
  - Feedback: Too casual for a two-week guest's farewell.
- [0] 気をつけて。
  - EN: Take care.
  - Feedback: Far too casual. Use full polite form.
