## Turn 1

**Guest:** Can I get two extra bath towels and some shampoo?
**Guest JP:** バスタオルを2枚とシャンプーを追加でいただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] かしこまりました。バスタオル2枚とシャンプーをお持ちいたします。
  - EN: Certainly. I will bring two bath towels and shampoo.
  - Feedback: Clean confirmation of request.
- [1] はい、持ってきます。
  - EN: Yes, I will bring them.
  - Feedback: Correct but doesn't repeat the specifics.
- [0] フロントに取りに来てください。
  - EN: Please come to the front desk to pick them up.
  - Feedback: Room service should be delivered to the room.
- [0] シャンプーはどの種類ですか？
  - EN: What type of shampoo would you like?
  - Feedback: Fine if asking for preference, but odd phrasing here.

## Turn 2

**System:** You confirm the towels and shampoo will be delivered and ask if anything else is needed.

**Guest:** Also, do you have a razor I could borrow?
**Guest JP:** それと、カミソリはお借りできますか？

### Choices

- [2] はい、使い捨てカミソリをご用意しております。一緒にお持ちいたします。
  - EN: Yes, we have disposable razors available. I will bring one along with the other items.
  - Feedback: Accommodating and adds it to the delivery.
- [1] フロントにあります。
  - EN: We have one at the front desk.
  - Feedback: True but should offer to deliver it.
- [0] ありません。
  - EN: We don't have any.
  - Feedback: Most hotels have disposable razors. Check first.
- [0] コンビニで買えます。
  - EN: You can buy one at a convenience store.
  - Feedback: Should offer hotel amenities first.

## Turn 3

**System:** You confirm a disposable razor is available and add it to the delivery.

**Guest:** Great, how soon can you bring them?
**Guest JP:** いつ頃持ってきていただけますか？

### Choices

- [2] 10分以内にお届けいたします。他にご入り用なものはございますか？
  - EN: We will deliver within 10 minutes. Is there anything else you need?
  - Feedback: Gives ETA and offers more help.
- [1] すぐです。
  - EN: Right away.
  - Feedback: Positive but vague.
- [0] 忙しいので少し待ってください。
  - EN: We're busy so please wait a bit.
  - Feedback: Don't burden guests with staff workload.
- [0] 分かりません。
  - EN: I don't know.
  - Feedback: Always give a timeframe.
