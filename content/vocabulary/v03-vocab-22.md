**金庫** *(kinko)*

"金庫" means safe, used to store valuables securely. Staff must explain its operation, location, and any restrictions. Proper guidance reassures guests about safety. Miscommunication may cause frustration or security issues.

---

## Quiz

**お貴重品はお部屋の____にお入れください。
(Please put your valuables in the room safe.)**

- A. 金庫 (safe)
- B. 冷蔵庫 (refrigerator)
- C. 引き出し (drawer)
- D. クローゼット (closet)

**Correct Answer:** 金庫 (safe)

**Explanation:** 金庫 is the safe — always guide guests to store valuables in it at check-in to prevent security concerns.
