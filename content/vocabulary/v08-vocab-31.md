**中** *(naka)*

"中" refers to inside a space, such as rooms or buildings. Staff use it to indicate where guests should enter or go. Clear instructions prevent guests from waiting outside or lost in corridors. It enhances navigation.

*Example: 会議室は建物の中にございます。* (The conference room is inside the building.)

---

## Quiz

**受付はロビーの____にございます。
(The reception is inside the lobby.)**

- A. 中 (inside / within)
- B. 外 (outside)
- C. 裏 (behind / back)
- D. 正面 (front / facing)

**Correct Answer:** 中 (inside / within)

**Explanation:** 中 (inside) — telling guests something is 中にございます prevents them from circling a building looking for an outdoor entrance; pairing 中 with 入口はこちら (entrance is here) completes the guidance.
