**こんにちは** *(konnichiwa)*

This is a standard greeting meaning "hello" or "good afternoon." It is polite and can be used with guests at any time during the daytime. In hotel settings, it sounds friendly yet professional. It is one of the most commonly used greetings in Japanese.

---

## Quiz

**____。チェックインでございますか？
(Hello. Are you checking in?)**

- A. こんばんは (good evening)
- B. こんにちは (hello / good afternoon)
- C. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- D. 失礼いたします (excuse me)

**Correct Answer:** こんにちは (hello / good afternoon)

**Explanation:** こんにちは is the standard daytime greeting meaning "hello" or "good afternoon."
