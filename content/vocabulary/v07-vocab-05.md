**地図** *(chizu)*

"地図" means a map, which helps guests navigate the surrounding area or find destinations. Hotels often provide maps for sightseeing, restaurants, or transportation.

Staff should explain directions clearly while using the map to support understanding. Offering a map proactively shows attentiveness and improves the guest experience, especially for foreign visitors.

---

## Quiz

**Guest looks confused about directions. What should you say?**

- A. 地図でご案内いたします (I will guide you with a map)
- B. 知らない (don't know)
- C. 自分で行ってください (please go yourself)
- D. 無理です (impossible / that's not possible)

**Correct Answer:** 地図でご案内いたします (I will guide you with a map)

**Explanation:** 地図でご案内いたします — で marks the map as the tool being used; ご案内いたします (I will guide you) is the humble form, turning a simple direction into a full act of hospitality.
