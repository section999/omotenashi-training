## ガイド (がいど · gaido)

**Meaning:** guide

ガイド is a person or tool providing information about a place, event, or culture. Guides help tourists understand local history, customs, and attractions. Professional guides ensure safety, timing, and an engaging experience. Audio or written guides supplement personal guidance.

**Example:**

観光ガイドが名所を案内してくれました。

*The tour guide showed us the famous places.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** A person who provides information and directions to tourists is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** ガイド

**Explanation:** ガイド (gaido) means guide — a person or tool that provides information, directions, and context for tourists at attractions.
