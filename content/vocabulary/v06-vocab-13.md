**水漏れ** *(mizumore)*

"水漏れ" means water leakage from pipes or equipment. It can cause damage if not handled quickly. Staff must report it immediately. Safety and cleanliness are priorities.

---

## Quiz

**Guest reports water leaking. Best response?**

- A. 水漏れを確認いたします (we will check the water leak)
- B. 知らない (don't know)
- C. 無理です (impossible / that's not possible)
- D. 自分で (by yourself / on your own)

**Correct Answer:** 水漏れを確認いたします (we will check the water leak)

**Explanation:** 水漏れを確認いたします — water leaks can escalate quickly; responding with 確認いたします immediately and notifying maintenance without delay is both the safe and service-focused action.
