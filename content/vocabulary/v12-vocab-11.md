## 危険 (きけん · kiken)

**Meaning:** danger

危険 refers to situations or objects that pose a threat to life, health, or property. Staff must identify hazardous areas and warn guests appropriately. Preventive measures reduce the risk of accidents. Clear signage and verbal warnings are essential for hazard management.

**Example:**

このエリアは危険ですので、立ち入らないでください。

*This area is dangerous, so please do not enter.*

## Quiz

**Which word best describes something that can cause harm or risk?**

- A. 安全 (safety / safe)
- B. 危険 (danger)
- C. 救助 (rescue)
- D. 警報 (alarm)

**Correct Answer:** 危険 (danger)

**Explanation:** 危険 (kiken) means danger — a situation posing risk to life or property. 安全 = safety, 救助 = rescue, 警報 = alarm.
