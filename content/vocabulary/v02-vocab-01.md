**フロント** *(furonto)*

"フロント" refers specifically to the hotel front desk where all main guest interactions occur. This includes check-in, check-out, handling requests, and answering questions. It is both a physical location and a functional role within the hotel. In professional settings, it is often used with polite expressions to guide guests.

---

## Quiz

**A guest looks lost in the lobby. What is the most appropriate sentence?**

- A. フロントはあそこです (the front desk is over there)
- B. フロントはこちらでございます (the front desk is here)
- C. フロント行きます (I will go to the front desk)
- D. フロント見ます (I will check at the front desk)

**Correct Answer:** フロントはこちらでございます (the front desk is here)

**Explanation:** フロントはこちらでございます uses ございます — the most polite form — and こちら to guide the guest respectfully.
