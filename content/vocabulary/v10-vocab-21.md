**超過** *(chouka)*

"超過" refers to exceeding a limit, such as overpayment or extra charges. Staff must explain why an additional fee applies. Guests expect clear communication on excess charges. Proper handling maintains trust and satisfaction.

*Example: 荷物の重量が超過しています。* (The luggage exceeds the weight limit.)

---

## Quiz

**限度を超えた状態は____です。
(The state of exceeding a limit is excess.)**

- A. 超過 (excess / overage)
- B. 不足 (shortage / insufficient)
- C. 精算 (settlement / final billing)
- D. 合計 (total)

**Correct Answer:** 超過 (excess / overage)

**Explanation:** 超過 applies to weight limits, time limits, and charges — when explaining a 超過料金 (excess charge), always state the limit, the actual amount, and the resulting fee clearly (制限〇〇、実際〇〇、追加〇〇円) so the guest understands the calculation.
