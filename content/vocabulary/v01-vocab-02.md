**おはようございます** *(ohayou gozaimasu)*

This phrase means "good morning" and is used in formal or polite situations. It is commonly used between staff members and also toward guests in the morning. Compared to casual "ohayou," this version shows respect and professionalism. It is appropriate in workplaces like hotels.

---

## Quiz

**____。本日もよろしくお願いいたします。
(Good morning. Thank you for your cooperation today.)**

- A. いらっしゃいませ (welcome)
- B. おはようございます (good morning)
- C. こんにちは (hello / good afternoon)
- D. 申し訳ございません (I am very sorry)

**Correct Answer:** おはようございます (good morning)

**Explanation:** おはようございます is the polite formal greeting used in the morning, appropriate between staff and toward guests.
