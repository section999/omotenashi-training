## 人気 (にんき · ninki)

**Meaning:** popular

人気 describes something well-liked or frequently visited by people. Popularity affects tourism trends, reservation demand, and social media attention. Understanding 人気 helps tourists choose attractions wisely. Businesses use popularity for promotions.

**Example:**

この神社は観光客にとても人気があります。

*This shrine is very popular among tourists.*

---

## Quiz

**Something well-liked or in high demand is described as:**

- A. 人気 (popularity)
- B. 名所 (sightseeing spot / landmark)
- C. おすすめ (recommendation)
- D. ツアー (tour)

**Correct Answer:** 人気 (popularity)

**Explanation:** 人気 (ninki) means popular — well-liked or in demand. 名所 = famous place, おすすめ = recommendation, ツアー = tour.
