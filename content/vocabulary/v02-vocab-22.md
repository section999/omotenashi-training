**延泊** *(enpaku)*

"延泊" means extending the stay beyond the original reservation. This is a common request from guests. Staff must check availability before confirming. It is important to respond clearly and politely.

---

## Quiz

**A guest asks to extend their stay. Which is the best response?**

- A. 延泊できる (can extend stay)
- B. 延泊は可能でございます (an extension of stay is possible)
- C. 延泊する？ (do you want to extend your stay?)
- D. 延泊だめ (cannot extend stay)

**Correct Answer:** 延泊は可能でございます (an extension of stay is possible)

**Explanation:** 延泊は可能でございます uses ございます and states availability clearly — polite and direct.
