**ようこそ** *(youkoso)*

This means "welcome" and is slightly less formal than "irasshaimase." It is often used in written messages or announcements. It expresses warmth and friendliness. It is suitable for greeting guests in a broader sense.

---

## Quiz

**当ホテルへ____。
(Welcome to our hotel.)**

- A. いらっしゃいませ (welcome)
- B. ようこそ (welcome)
- C. またお越しくださいませ (please come again)
- D. お越しください (please come)

**Correct Answer:** ようこそ (welcome)

**Explanation:** ようこそ means "welcome" and is commonly used in announcements or written greetings for a warm, broad welcome.
