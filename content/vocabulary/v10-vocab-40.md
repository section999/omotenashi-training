**確認番号** *(kakunin bangou)*

"確認番号" is a unique number verifying a reservation, payment, or booking. Staff use it to confirm details quickly. Guests provide it for check-in or inquiries. Accurate management prevents errors or miscommunication.

*Example: 予約の確認番号を教えてください。* (Please tell me your reservation confirmation number.)

---

## Quiz

**Which number verifies a reservation or payment?**

- A. 確認番号 (confirmation number)
- B. 伝票 (bill slip)
- C. 領収書 (receipt)
- D. 合計 (total)

**Correct Answer:** 確認番号 (confirmation number)

**Explanation:** 確認番号 and 予約番号 serve similar purposes but at different stages — 予約番号 is assigned at booking time, while 確認番号 may refer to a separate verification code sent by email or SMS; always clarify which one you need to avoid confusion when the guest has both.
