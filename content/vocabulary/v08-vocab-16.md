**出口** *(deguchi)*

"出口" indicates an exit, often for leaving buildings or rooms. Staff should point out emergency and main exits. Clear directions prevent congestion or accidents during emergencies. Proper signage is essential for guest safety.

*Example: 非常口の出口は右側です。* (The emergency exit is on the right.)

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "Where is the nearest exit?"**

- A. 出口は右側です (the exit is on the right side)
- B. 入口です (it is the entrance)
- C. 奥です (it is at the back)
- D. 手前です (it is in front)

**Correct Answer:** 出口は右側です (the exit is on the right side)

**Explanation:** 出口 (exit) — always specify which exit (main exit, emergency exit) by combining 出口 with a direction; simply saying 出口 without a direction can send guests in the wrong way in a large building.
