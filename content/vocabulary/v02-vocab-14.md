**税金** *(zeikin)*

"税金" refers to taxes applied to the room charge. It is usually included or added separately. Staff should clearly explain whether tax is included. This helps avoid misunderstandings.

---

## Quiz

**料金には税金が____れております。
(Tax is included in the price.)**

- A. 含ま (included)
- B. 入ら (to enter / be included)
- C. 加えら (to be added)
- D. はいら (yes (informal/incorrect))

**Correct Answer:** 含ま (included)

**Explanation:** 含まれております (fukumarete orimasu) means "is included" — the correct and polite expression for explaining what is covered in the price.
