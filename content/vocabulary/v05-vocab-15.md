**会計** *(kaikei)*

"会計" refers to the bill or checkout process after dining. Staff should handle it accurately and politely.

Explaining payment methods helps avoid confusion.

---

## Quiz

**お____はこちらで承ります。
(We will take care of the bill here.)**

- A. 会計 (billing / accounting)
- B. 注文 (order)
- C. メニュー (menu)
- D. 提供 (provision)

**Correct Answer:** 会計 (billing / accounting)

**Explanation:** お会計はこちらで承ります — 承ります is the humble form of receiving (a request/payment), appropriate at the cashier or tableside when settling the bill.
