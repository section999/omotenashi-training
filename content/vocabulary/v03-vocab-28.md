---

**ルームサービス** *(rūmu sābisu)*

"ルームサービス" is service provided directly to guest rooms, including food, drinks, or amenities. Staff may explain hours, menu, and charges. Clear communication improves guest satisfaction and avoids misunderstandings.

---

## Quiz

**Which is the most appropriate expression when explaining room service availability?**

- A. ルームサービスは24時間対応でございます (room service is available 24 hours)
- B. ルームサービスありません (room service is not available)
- C. ルームサービスどうぞ (please use room service)
- D. ルームサービス今 (room service now)

**Correct Answer:** ルームサービスは24時間対応でございます (room service is available 24 hours)

**Explanation:** ルームサービスは24時間対応でございます clearly states availability using ございます — professional and informative.
