**階段** *(kaidan)*

"階段" means stairs, which serve as an alternative to elevators and are also crucial in emergencies such as fire evacuations. Staff should be able to explain their location clearly and emphasize safety when needed.

In some situations, guests may prefer stairs for health reasons or to avoid waiting. Providing clear directions and indicating whether the stairs are accessible or restricted ensures both safety and convenience.

---

## Quiz

**During a fire drill, what should staff recommend?**

- A. 階段をご利用ください (please use the stairs)
- B. エレベーターをご利用ください (please use the elevator)
- C. 部屋に戻ってください (please return to your room)
- D. 何もしないでください (please do nothing)

**Correct Answer:** 階段をご利用ください (please use the stairs)

**Explanation:** 階段をご利用ください — elevators must never be used during a fire; guiding guests to 階段 (stairs) is the correct safety protocol and a critical piece of hotel emergency knowledge.
