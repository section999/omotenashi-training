**通訳** *(tsuuyaku)*

"通訳" is orally translating between languages. Staff must provide accurate interpretation when assisting foreign guests. Proper interpretation ensures smooth communication and prevents misunderstandings.

*Example: 会議で通訳を依頼しました。* (We requested an interpreter for the meeting.)

---

## Quiz

**Which word means "oral translation between languages"?**

- A. 通訳 (interpretation)
- B. 翻訳 (translation)
- C. 案内 (guidance / information)
- D. 説明 (explanation)

**Correct Answer:** 通訳 (interpretation)

**Explanation:** 通訳 (spoken) vs 翻訳 (written) — this distinction matters when a guest asks for help: a guest who needs a document translated needs 翻訳, while one who needs someone to speak on their behalf in a meeting needs 通訳; offering the wrong type wastes time and erodes confidence.
