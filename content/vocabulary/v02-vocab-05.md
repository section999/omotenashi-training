**予約確認** *(yoyaku kakunin)*

"予約確認" refers to verifying reservation details such as name, date, and room type. This step prevents errors and ensures smooth service. It is often done during check-in using polite expressions. Accuracy is very important.

---

## Quiz

**ご予約の____をさせていただきます。
(I will verify your reservation.)**

- A. 確認 (confirmation)
- B. 変更 (change / modification)
- C. 取消 (cancellation)
- D. 延泊 (extended stay)

**Correct Answer:** 確認 (confirmation)

**Explanation:** 確認 (kakunin) means confirmation or verification — the correct word for checking reservation details.
