## 警告 (けいこく · keikoku)

**Meaning:** warning

警告 is a stronger alert than 注意, signalling serious risk or imminent danger. Staff may use verbal alerts, signs, or alarms. Understanding warnings prevents accidents and ensures compliance with safety rules. Clear communication is critical during emergencies.

**Example:**

この機械は高温のため、警告サインがあります。

*This machine has a warning sign because it is hot.*

## Quiz

**You see a sign indicating immediate danger if you proceed. What is this called?**

- A. 注意 (caution / attention)
- B. 警告 (warning)
- C. 危険 (danger)
- D. 点検中 (under inspection)

**Correct Answer:** 警告 (warning)

**Explanation:** 警告 (keikoku) means warning — a stronger alert than caution, signalling serious or imminent risk. 注意 = caution, 危険 = danger, 点検中 = under inspection.
