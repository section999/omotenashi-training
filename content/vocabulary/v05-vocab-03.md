**昼食** *(chuushoku)*

"昼食" refers to lunch, usually served around midday. Hotels may offer light meals or full-course options depending on the restaurant type.

Staff should guide guests about menu options and peak hours to avoid crowding.

---

## Quiz

**Which phrase correctly tells a guest when lunch is available?**

- A. 昼食は12時からご利用いただけます (lunch is available from 12 o'clock)
- B. 昼食ない (no lunch available)
- C. 昼食自分で (manage lunch yourself)
- D. 昼食できない (cannot have lunch)

**Correct Answer:** 昼食は12時からご利用いただけます (lunch is available from 12 o'clock)

**Explanation:** 昼食は12時からご利用いただけます — ご利用いただけます is the humble form of "you may use / is available", standard for informing guests of service hours.
