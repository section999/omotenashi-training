**ルームキー** *(rūmu kī)*

"ルームキー" refers specifically to the key for a guest's room. It is usually given at check-in. Staff often combine it with polite expressions when handing it over. It is essential for access.

---

## Quiz

**こちらがルームキーでございます。____。
(Here is your room key. Please.)**

- A. どうぞ (please / go ahead)
- B. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- C. かしこまりました (certainly / understood (very formal))
- D. 失礼いたします (excuse me)

**Correct Answer:** どうぞ (please / go ahead)

**Explanation:** どうぞ is the natural completion — it invites the guest to take the key and creates a warm, welcoming handover.
