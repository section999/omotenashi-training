**清掃中** *(seisouchuu)*

"清掃中" means "cleaning in progress." It is often used on signs or door hangers to inform guests that staff are currently cleaning the room. Proper signage prevents interruptions and ensures privacy.

---

## Quiz

**A guest approaches a room being cleaned. Which sign should they see?**

- A. 清掃中 (being cleaned / cleaning in progress)
- B. 清掃済み (cleaned)
- C. 掃除なし (no cleaning)
- D. 入室禁止なし (no room entry restriction)

**Correct Answer:** 清掃中 (being cleaned / cleaning in progress)

**Explanation:** 清掃中 signals that cleaning is actively in progress — it prevents guests from entering and protects staff privacy during work.
