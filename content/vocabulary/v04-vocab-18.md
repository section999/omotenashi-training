**破損** *(hason)*

"破損" means damage. Staff must report or repair any damage to hotel property. Handling with care and prompt response prevents complaints and maintains hotel standards.

---

## Quiz

**客室内の家具に____がございました。
(There was damage to furniture in the room.)**

- A. 破損 (damage)
- B. 汚れ (stain / dirt)
- C. 修理 (repair)
- D. 点検 (inspection)

**Correct Answer:** 破損 (damage)

**Explanation:** 破損 specifically means physical damage — reporting 破損 accurately allows maintenance to respond quickly and prevents further deterioration.
