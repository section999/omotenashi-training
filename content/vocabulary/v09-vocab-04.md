**到着日** *(touchaku bi)*

"到着日" is the day the guest is scheduled to arrive at the hotel. Staff need this to ensure the room is ready, plan early check-ins, and arrange airport pick-ups if needed. Accurate arrival information improves guest satisfaction and operational efficiency. Guests sometimes provide flight details to confirm arrival dates.

*Example: 到着日は午後3時です。* (The arrival date is at 3 PM.)

---

## Quiz

**A guest asks to arrive earlier than their booked date. Which term is relevant?**

- A. 到着日 (arrival date)
- B. 出発日 (departure date)
- C. 宿泊日 (stay date)
- D. 予約日 (reservation date)

**Correct Answer:** 到着日 (arrival date)

**Explanation:** 到着日 is the arrival-specific date — when a guest requests an early arrival, staff must check whether the room is ready from the previous night's check-out and communicate clearly about early check-in fees if applicable.
