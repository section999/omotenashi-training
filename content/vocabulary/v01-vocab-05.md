**ありがとうございます** *(arigatou gozaimasu)*

This is a polite way to say "thank you." It is used in customer service to show appreciation to guests. Compared to casual "arigatou," this form is more respectful and professional. It is essential in hospitality communication.

---

## Quiz

**ご利用いただき、____。
(Thank you for using our service.)**

- A. かしこまりました (certainly / understood (very formal))
- B. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- C. 申し訳ございません (I am very sorry)
- D. 失礼いたします (excuse me)

**Correct Answer:** ありがとうございます (thank you)

**Explanation:** ありがとうございます is the standard polite expression of gratitude used in all professional hospitality contexts.
