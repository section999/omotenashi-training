**清掃係** *(seisō kakari)*

"清掃係" means housekeeping staff. They handle cleaning, replenishing amenities, and maintaining guest room standards. Staff may introduce them or explain their duties politely. Proper introduction ensures guest comfort.

---

## Quiz

**A housekeeping staff member will come to the room. How do you politely inform the guest?**

- A. 清掃係がまいります (the cleaning staff will come)
- B. 清掃係来る (cleaning staff will come)
- C. 清掃係行く (cleaning staff will go)
- D. 清掃係です (I am the cleaning staff)

**Correct Answer:** 清掃係がまいります (the cleaning staff will come)

**Explanation:** 清掃係がまいります uses まいります — the humble form of 来る (to come) — appropriate when talking about hotel staff in front of guests.
