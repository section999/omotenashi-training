**窓** *(mado)*

"窓" means window. Staff may explain how to open or close windows safely or point out the view. Proper instruction ensures comfort and prevents accidents. It can also be mentioned when discussing room features.

---

## Quiz

**Which is the most helpful expression for explaining how to open the window?**

- A. 窓は手前に引いて開けてください (please pull the window toward you to open it)
- B. 窓は開かない (the window does not open)
- C. 窓見てください (please look at the window)
- D. 窓閉める (close the window)

**Correct Answer:** 窓は手前に引いて開けてください (please pull the window toward you to open it)

**Explanation:** 窓は手前に引いて開けてください gives a clear physical instruction — directional guidance is essential for guest safety and comfort.
