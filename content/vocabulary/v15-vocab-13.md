## 宅配便 (たっきゅうびん · takkyuubin)

**Meaning:** courier service

宅配便 is a service that transports packages to designated addresses. Widely used in Japan for personal and business deliveries. Efficient 宅配便 enhances convenience for tourists and residents alike.

**Example:**

お土産を宅配便で家に送りました。

*I sent souvenirs home via courier.*

---

## Quiz

**A door-to-door package delivery service is called:**

- A. 宅配便 (courier / home delivery)
- B. 配達 (delivery)
- C. 発送 (shipping / sending)
- D. 荷物 (luggage / baggage)

**Correct Answer:** 宅配便 (courier / home delivery)

**Explanation:** 宅配便 (takkyuubin) means courier service — a door-to-door package delivery system widely used in Japan. 配達 = delivery, 発送 = shipping, 荷物 = luggage.
