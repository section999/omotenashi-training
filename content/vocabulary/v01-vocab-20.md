**またお越しくださいませ** *(mata okoshi kudasaimase)*

This phrase means "please come again." It is very polite and commonly used when guests leave. It expresses gratitude and encourages repeat visits. It is essential in customer service etiquette.

---

## Quiz

**ご利用ありがとうございました。____。
(Thank you for your visit. Please come again.)**

- A. お気をつけて (please take care)
- B. ようこそ (welcome)
- C. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- D. またお越しくださいませ (please come again)

**Correct Answer:** またお越しくださいませ (please come again)

**Explanation:** またお越しくださいませ is the standard farewell in Japanese hospitality — it warmly invites the guest to return.
