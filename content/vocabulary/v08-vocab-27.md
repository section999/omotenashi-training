**こちら側** *(kochira gawa)*

"こちら側" refers to "this side" near the speaker. Staff use it for room directions, entrances, or seating. It helps guests distinguish between left/right sides. Clear indication ensures smooth navigation.

*Example: エレベーターはこちら側にございます。* (The elevator is on this side.)

---

## Quiz

**受付カウンターは____です。
(The reception counter is on this side.)**

- A. こちら側 (this side)
- B. あちら側 (over there)
- C. 奥 (back / deeper in)
- D. 外 (outside)

**Correct Answer:** こちら側 (this side)

**Explanation:** こちら側 (this side, near the speaker) vs あちら側 (that side, far from speaker) — using 側 (side) rather than just こちら adds spatial clarity, especially in wide corridors or open lobbies.
