**近くに** *(chikaku ni)*

"近くに" means nearby or close to a location. Staff use it to indicate proximity to hotels, attractions, or stations. It helps guests find restaurants, shops, or transportation efficiently. Clear instructions improve satisfaction and convenience.

*Example: コンビニはホテルの近くにございます。* (There is a convenience store near the hotel.)

---

## Quiz

**レストランは駅の____にあります。
(The restaurant is near the station.)**

- A. 近くに (nearby)
- B. 遠くに (far away)
- C. 隣に (next to)
- D. 向かいに (across from)

**Correct Answer:** 近くに (nearby)

**Explanation:** 近くに (nearby) — following up with a walking time (徒歩〇分) after saying 近くに makes the information practical; 近く alone is vague without a distance indicator.
