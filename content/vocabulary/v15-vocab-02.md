## 洗濯 (せんたく · sentaku)

**Meaning:** washing

洗濯 refers to the act of washing clothes or fabrics. It can be done by hand or machine. Travelers often rely on 洗濯 services in hotels or self-service laundries. Proper washing removes dirt, stains, and odors effectively.

**Example:**

毎週日曜日に洗濯をします。

*I do the laundry every Sunday.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** The act of cleaning clothes is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 洗濯

**Explanation:** 洗濯 (sentaku) means washing — the process of cleaning clothes or fabrics by hand or machine.
