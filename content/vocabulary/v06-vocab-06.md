**忘れ物** *(wasuremono)*

"忘れ物" refers to items left behind by guests. Staff should carefully check rooms and report found items. Proper storage and documentation are essential. Returning lost items improves guest satisfaction and trust.

---

## Quiz

**お部屋に____がございました。
(There was a forgotten item in the room.)**

- A. 忘れ物 (lost and found / forgotten item)
- B. クレーム (complaint)
- C. 故障 (breakdown / malfunction)
- D. 問題 (problem)

**Correct Answer:** 忘れ物 (lost and found / forgotten item)

**Explanation:** 忘れ物 means a forgotten item — reporting 忘れ物 promptly with proper documentation is a key omotenashi value, showing care for the guest even after departure.
