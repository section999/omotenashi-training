## 入国 (にゅうこく · nyuukoku)

**Meaning:** entry (into a country)

入国 refers to the act of entering a country. Immigration officers check passports, visas, and customs declarations. Proper documentation is required for legal entry. Travelers should understand entry procedures to avoid delays.

**Example:**

空港で入国手続きを行いました。

*I went through entry procedures at the airport.*

---

## Quiz

**The act of entering a country is called:**

- A. 出国 (departure from the country)
- B. 入国 (entry into the country)
- C. 通貨 (currency)
- D. 観光地 (tourist destination)

**Correct Answer:** 入国 (entry into the country)

**Explanation:** 入国 (nyuukoku) means entry into a country. 出国 = departure, 通貨 = currency, 観光地 = tourist spot.
