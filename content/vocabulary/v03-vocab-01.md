**客室** *(kyakushitsu)*

"客室" means guest room in a hotel and refers to any room designated for guests. It is more formal than "部屋" and is often used in brochures, signage, or official hotel communication. Staff use it when describing room types or locations.

---

## Quiz

**本日のご宿泊は____でございます。
(Your stay today is in the guest room.)**

- A. 客室 (guest room)
- B. 部屋 (room)
- C. フロント (front desk)
- D. 受付 (reception)

**Correct Answer:** 客室 (guest room)

**Explanation:** 客室 is the formal term for guest room — used in all official hotel communication and guest-facing contexts.
