**掃除** *(souji)*

"掃除" means cleaning in general and refers to all activities to maintain room or hotel hygiene. It includes dusting, vacuuming, and sanitizing surfaces. Staff should follow hotel standards to ensure guest satisfaction and a safe environment.

---

## Quiz

**毎朝、客室の____を行っております。
(We perform cleaning of the guest rooms every morning.)**

- A. 掃除 (cleaning)
- B. 点検 (inspection)
- C. 修理 (repair)
- D. 補充 (replenishment / refill)

**Correct Answer:** 掃除 (cleaning)

**Explanation:** 掃除 means general cleaning — used when describing daily room maintenance across all areas of the hotel.
