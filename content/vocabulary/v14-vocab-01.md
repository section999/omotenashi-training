## 外国人 (がいこくじん · gaikokujin)

**Meaning:** foreigner

外国人 refers to a person from another country. In tourism and service industries, interacting with foreigners requires cultural awareness and language skills. Understanding their needs ensures smooth communication and satisfaction. Hospitality for foreigners promotes a welcoming environment.

**Example:**

空港で多くの外国人を見かけました。

*I saw many foreigners at the airport.*

---

## Quiz

**A person from another country is called:**

- A. 観光客 (tourist)
- B. 外国人 (foreigner)
- C. 旅行者 (traveler)
- D. 名所 (sightseeing spot / landmark)

**Correct Answer:** 外国人 (foreigner)

**Explanation:** 外国人 (gaikokujin) means foreigner — a person from another country. 観光客 = tourist, 旅行者 = traveller, 名所 = famous place.
