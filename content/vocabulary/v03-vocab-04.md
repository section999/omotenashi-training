**ダブルルーム** *(daburu rūmu)*

A double room has one bed for two people. Often chosen by couples. Staff must clarify this type during booking or check-in to avoid confusion.

---

## Quiz

**Guest: "We booked a double room." How do you confirm politely?**

- A. ダブルルームでございます (it is a double room)
- B. シングルルームでございます (it is a single room)
- C. ツインルームでございます (it is a twin room)
- D. スイートルームでございます (it is a suite room)

**Correct Answer:** ダブルルームでございます (it is a double room)

**Explanation:** ダブルルームでございます confirms the room type respectfully using ございます — always verify the correct room type with the guest.
