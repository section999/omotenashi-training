**朝食付き** *(choushoku tsuki)*

"朝食付き" shows the plan includes breakfast. Staff provide details about timing, menu, and location. Guests value this convenience for planning their day. Clear explanation prevents misunderstandings.

*Example: このプランは朝食付きです。* (This plan includes breakfast.)

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "Does my plan include breakfast?"**

- A. 朝食付き (breakfast included)
- B. 素泊まり (room only (no meals))
- C. 夕食付き (dinner included)
- D. プラン (plan)

**Correct Answer:** 朝食付き (breakfast included)

**Explanation:** 朝食付き — when confirming, always add the time and location (朝食は7時から1階レストランにてご用意しております) so the guest has everything they need to plan their morning without a follow-up question.
