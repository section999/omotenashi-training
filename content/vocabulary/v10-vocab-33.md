**バウチャー** *(bauchaa)*

"バウチャー" is proof of prepayment or entitlement to a service. Staff must confirm details and validity. Guests use vouchers for booking hotels, meals, or services. Proper verification ensures correct usage.

*Example: バウチャーを受付でご提示ください。* (Please present the voucher at reception.)

---

## Quiz

**サービス利用の証明書は____です。
(The document proving entitlement to a service is a voucher.)**

- A. バウチャー (voucher)
- B. クーポン (coupon)
- C. 伝票 (bill slip)
- D. 領収書 (receipt)

**Correct Answer:** バウチャー (voucher)

**Explanation:** バウチャー vs クーポン — a バウチャー typically represents a prepaid value or specific service entitlement, while a クーポン is a discount token; accepting a クーポン where a バウチャー was required (or vice versa) can result in revenue loss or incorrect billing.
