**雑巾** *(zoukin)*

"雑巾" is a cleaning cloth. Staff use it for wiping surfaces or spills. Proper use maintains hygiene and cleanliness in guest rooms. Clear instruction ensures safe and effective cleaning.

---

## Quiz

**テーブルは____で拭きます。
(We wipe the table with a cleaning cloth.)**

- A. 雑巾 (cleaning cloth / rag)
- B. 掃除機 (vacuum cleaner)
- C. モップ (mop)
- D. ゴミ箱 (trash can)

**Correct Answer:** 雑巾 (cleaning cloth / rag)

**Explanation:** 雑巾 is used for wiping surfaces — 拭きます means "to wipe", and knowing the right tool for each task is part of professional housekeeping.
