**一括払い** *(ikkatsu barai)*

"一括払い" is paying the entire amount in one single payment. Staff must confirm the total accurately before processing. This method simplifies transactions and is preferred for shorter stays or single services. Guests benefit from avoiding installment tracking or extra fees.

*Example: 宿泊料金は一括払いでお願いします。* (Please pay the accommodation fee in a lump sum.)

---

## Quiz

**全額を一度に支払う方法は____です。
(The method of paying the full amount at once is lump-sum payment.)**

- A. 一括払い (lump sum payment)
- B. 分割払い (installment payment)
- C. 前払い (prepayment)
- D. 後払い (deferred payment / pay later)

**Correct Answer:** 一括払い (lump sum payment)

**Explanation:** 一括払い (lump-sum payment) is the default in most hotel transactions — confirming ご一括でよろしいでしょうか (shall I process this as a single payment?) before swiping prevents the need to void and reprocess if the guest preferred installments.
