## 通報 (つうほう · tsuuhou)

**Meaning:** report (to authorities)

通報 refers to notifying the proper authorities about an emergency, crime, or accident. Timely reporting enables quick intervention and resolution. Staff must know how to contact police, fire, or medical services. Clear, concise communication ensures effective response.

**Example:**

火災の場合はすぐに消防署に通報してください。

*Report to the fire department immediately in case of fire.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** Reporting an emergency to authorities is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 通報

**Explanation:** 通報 (tsuuhou) means reporting to authorities — notifying emergency services such as police or fire department.
