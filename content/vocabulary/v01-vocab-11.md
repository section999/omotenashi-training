**どうぞ** *(douzo)*

This word means "please" or "here you go." It is used when offering something to someone. In hotels, it is commonly used when handing keys, documents, or items. It sounds polite and inviting.

---

## Quiz

**こちらがお部屋の鍵でございます。____。
(Here is your room key.)**

- A. こちらへどうぞ (please come this way)
- B. ごゆっくりどうぞ (please take your time)
- C. どうぞ (please / go ahead)
- D. ありがとうございます (thank you)

**Correct Answer:** どうぞ (please / go ahead)

**Explanation:** どうぞ is used when presenting or offering something — it is polite, simple, and universally understood.
