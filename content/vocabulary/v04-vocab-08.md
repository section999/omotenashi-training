**補充** *(hojuu)*

"補充" means replenishment of supplies like toiletries or minibar items. Staff should check inventory and replace items as needed. Proper replenishment improves guest satisfaction and hotel reputation.

---

## Quiz

**アメニティの____は毎日行っております。
(Replenishment of amenities is done daily.)**

- A. 補充 (replenishment / refill)
- B. 掃除 (cleaning)
- C. 交換 (exchange / replacement)
- D. 点検 (inspection)

**Correct Answer:** 補充 (replenishment / refill)

**Explanation:** 補充 means refilling or restocking — it applies to toiletries, minibar, and any consumables that must be topped up during a guest's stay.
