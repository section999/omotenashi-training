**子供** *(kodomo)*

"子供" refers to children who will stay at the hotel. Staff plan beds, meals, and amenities based on age. Children may have discounted rates compared to adults. Proper identification ensures correct billing and service.

*Example: 子供1名を追加しました。* (One child has been added.)

---

## Quiz

**家族予約には大人2名、____1名です。
(The family booking is 2 adults and 1 child.)**

- A. 子供 (child)
- B. 大人 (adult)
- C. 幼児 (infant)
- D. 人数 (number of people)

**Correct Answer:** 子供 (child)

**Explanation:** 子供 (child) often qualifies for reduced rates — when adding a 子供 to a reservation, confirm age to determine whether they fall into the 子供 or 幼児 category, as pricing and amenities (cribs, high chairs) differ.
