---

**早朝チェックイン** *(sōchō chekku-in)*

This refers to checking in earlier than the standard time. It depends on room availability and may include extra charges. Staff should explain this condition clearly. Managing expectations is important.

---

## Quiz

**早朝チェックインは追加料金が____場合がございます。
(Early check-in may incur an additional charge.)**

- A. 発生する (to occur)
- B. ない (there is not / none)
- C. かかる (to take / to cost)
- D. 含まれる (to be included)

**Correct Answer:** 発生する (to occur)

**Explanation:** 発生する (hassei suru) means "to arise/occur" — 料金が発生する is the standard professional expression for additional charges.
