**外貨** *(gaika)*

"外貨" refers to money from a foreign country. Staff must exchange and process accurately. Guests often request currency for travel or payment. Proper handling avoids errors and ensures satisfaction.

*Example: 外貨での支払いも可能です。* (Payment in foreign currency is also possible.)

---

## Quiz

**海外の通貨は____です。
(Money from a foreign country is called foreign currency.)**

- A. 外貨 (foreign currency)
- B. 為替 (exchange rate)
- C. 両替 (currency exchange)
- D. 残高 (balance)

**Correct Answer:** 外貨 (foreign currency)

**Explanation:** 外貨 handling requires knowing the day's 為替レート — hotels that accept 外貨 should display the current rate prominently (本日の為替レートはこちらです) so guests can decide whether to pay in foreign currency or convert first.
