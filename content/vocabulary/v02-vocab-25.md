**鍵** *(kagi)*

"鍵" means key and refers to a physical key. Although many hotels now use cards, the term is still understood. It is a basic but essential word. Staff must hand it over politely.

---

## Quiz

**Which is the most appropriate expression when handing over a key?**

- A. 鍵です (it is the key)
- B. 鍵どうぞ (here is the key)
- C. こちらが鍵でございます (here is your key)
- D. 鍵ある (there is a key)

**Correct Answer:** こちらが鍵でございます (here is your key)

**Explanation:** こちらが鍵でございます presents the key respectfully — こちら draws attention to the item and ございます adds formality.
