**隣** *(tonari)*

"隣" means next to or adjacent. Staff use it to describe rooms, shops, or facilities nearby. Combining it with landmarks improves guest understanding. Accurate use prevents guests from going to the wrong location.

*Example: レストランの隣にカフェがあります。* (There is a cafe next to the restaurant.)

---

## Quiz

**ATMはコンビニの____にございます。
(The ATM is next to the convenience store.)**

- A. 隣 (next to / adjacent)
- B. 向かい (across / opposite)
- C. 奥 (back / deeper in)
- D. 手前 (in front / near side)

**Correct Answer:** 隣 (next to / adjacent)

**Explanation:** 隣 (next to / adjacent) — distinguishing 隣 (side-by-side) from 向かい (across from) is important; sending a guest to the wrong direction because of a word mix-up is a common navigation error.
