**お疲れ様です** *(otsukaresama desu)*

This phrase means "thank you for your hard work." It is commonly used among staff members. It acknowledges effort and builds good relationships in the workplace. It is not typically used for customers.

---

## Quiz

**本日も____。
(Thank you for your hard work today.)**

- A. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- B. お気をつけて (please take care)
- C. お疲れ様です (good work / thank you for your efforts)
- D. ごゆっくりどうぞ (please take your time)

**Correct Answer:** お疲れ様です (good work / thank you for your efforts)

**Explanation:** お疲れ様です acknowledges a colleague's effort — it is used between staff members, not toward guests.
