## アイロン (あいろん · airon)

**Meaning:** iron

アイロン is a device used to remove wrinkles from clothes by applying heat. Proper ironing ensures neat and presentable clothing. In hotels, irons are often available in rooms or laundry services. Using an アイロン requires care to avoid burning fabrics.

**Example:**

シャツにアイロンをかけました。

*I ironed my shirt.*
