**お気をつけて** *(oki o tsukete)*

This means "take care." It is used when someone is leaving. It expresses concern for the person's safety. In hotels, staff say this to guests going out.

---

## Quiz

**いってらっしゃいませ。____。
(Have a nice day. Take care.)**

- A. またお越しくださいませ (please come again)
- B. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- C. お気をつけて (please take care)
- D. ごゆっくりどうぞ (please take your time)

**Correct Answer:** お気をつけて (please take care)

**Explanation:** お気をつけて expresses care for the guest's safety as they depart — it is warm and sincere.
