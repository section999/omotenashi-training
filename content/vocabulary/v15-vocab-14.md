## 保管 (ほかん · hokan)

**Meaning:** storage

保管 refers to safely keeping items for a period of time. Hotels and services offer 保管 for luggage, valuables, or packages. Proper management during storage prevents loss or damage.

**Example:**

荷物を1週間保管してもらいました。

*I had my luggage stored for a week.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** Keeping items safely for a period of time is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 保管

**Explanation:** 保管 (hokan) means storage — safely keeping items such as luggage or valuables for a defined period.
