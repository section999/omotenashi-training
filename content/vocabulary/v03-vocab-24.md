**鍵穴** *(kagiana)*

"鍵穴" is the keyhole of a door or safe. Staff may point it out when explaining how to use a physical key. Clear guidance prevents guests from struggling and enhances the check-in experience.

---

## Quiz

**鍵は____に差し込んで回してください。
(Insert the key into the keyhole and turn.)**

- A. 鍵穴 (keyhole)
- B. 金庫 (safe)
- C. ドア (door)
- D. 窓 (window)

**Correct Answer:** 鍵穴 (keyhole)

**Explanation:** 鍵穴 means keyhole — used when explaining how to use a physical key, an important detail for guests unfamiliar with older-style locks.
