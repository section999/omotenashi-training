## 業務 (ぎょうむ · gyoumu)

**Meaning:** duties

業務 refers to all tasks and responsibilities assigned to an employee. Efficient execution of duties is essential for organisational success. Understanding one's duties reduces errors and increases productivity. Documentation and proper workflow help manage business operations effectively.

**Example:**

毎朝、業務内容を確認します。

*I check my duties every morning.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** All assigned work tasks are called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 業務

**Explanation:** 業務 (gyoumu) means duties — all tasks and responsibilities assigned to an employee for efficient organisational operations.
