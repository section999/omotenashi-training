**宿泊税** *(shukuhaku zei)*

"宿泊税" is a local tax applied to hotel stays. Staff must include this in the total bill. Guests should be informed beforehand. Proper handling ensures compliance and transparency.

*Example: 宿泊税は料金に含まれています。* (The accommodation tax is included in the price.)

---

## Quiz

**ホテル滞在にかかる税金は____です。
(The tax applied to hotel stays is called accommodation tax.)**

- A. 宿泊税 (accommodation tax)
- B. 消費税 (consumption tax)
- C. サービス料 (service charge)
- D. 手数料 (handling fee)

**Correct Answer:** 宿泊税 (accommodation tax)

**Explanation:** 宿泊税 varies by city and room rate — Tokyo and Osaka have different 宿泊税 thresholds; always confirm the applicable local rate at the time of billing, as staff who quote outdated rates create legal and financial compliance risks.
