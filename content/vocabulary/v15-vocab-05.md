## クリーニング (くりーにんぐ · kuriiningu)

**Meaning:** dry cleaning

クリーニング refers to professional cleaning for delicate fabrics using chemical solvents instead of water. It is used for suits, coats, and formal wear. Dry cleaning preserves fabric quality and appearance. Hotels often offer クリーニング for guest convenience.

**Example:**

スーツをクリーニングに出しました。

*I sent my suit to dry cleaning.*

---

## Quiz

**Professional cleaning using solvents instead of water is called:**

- A. 洗濯 (washing / laundry)
- B. クリーニング (dry cleaning)
- C. アイロン (iron)
- D. ランドリー (laundry)

**Correct Answer:** クリーニング (dry cleaning)

**Explanation:** クリーニング (kuriiningu) means dry cleaning — professional fabric cleaning using chemical solvents. 洗濯 = regular washing, アイロン = ironing, ランドリー = laundry service.
