## 配達 (はいたつ · haitatsu)

**Meaning:** delivery

配達 refers to delivering goods, packages, or laundry to a specified location. In hotels, 配達 can include room service, laundry return, or parcel service. Timely 配達 ensures guest satisfaction and convenience.

**Example:**

ランドリーが翌日に配達されました。

*The laundry was delivered the next day.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** Delivering items to a specified location is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 配達

**Explanation:** 配達 (haitatsu) means delivery — bringing goods, laundry, or packages to a designated location on time.
