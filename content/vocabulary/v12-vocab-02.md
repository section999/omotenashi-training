## 火災 (かさい · kasai)

**Meaning:** fire

火災 refers to the outbreak of fire that can cause property damage, injury, or death. Staff must know fire locations, alarms, and evacuation procedures. Quick detection and response can prevent escalation. Regular fire drills ensure that both staff and guests can act appropriately during a fire.

**Example:**

火災が発生したら、消火器を使うか避難してください。

*If a fire occurs, use the fire extinguisher or evacuate.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** Building damage caused by uncontrolled flames is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 火災

**Explanation:** 火災 (kasai) means fire — an outbreak of flames causing property damage or injury requiring immediate response.
