**ごゆっくりどうぞ** *(goyukkuri douzo)*

This phrase means "please take your time" or "please relax." It is used to make guests feel comfortable. It shows hospitality and kindness. It is often used when guests enter their rooms or facilities.

---

## Quiz

**お部屋で____。
(Please relax in your room.)**

- A. どうぞ (please / go ahead)
- B. こちらへどうぞ (please come this way)
- C. お気をつけて (please take care)
- D. ごゆっくりどうぞ (please take your time)

**Correct Answer:** ごゆっくりどうぞ (please take your time)

**Explanation:** ごゆっくりどうぞ invites the guest to relax and take their time — it creates a sense of warmth and unhurried welcome.
