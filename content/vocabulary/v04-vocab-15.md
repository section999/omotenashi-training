**モップ** *(moppu)*

"モップ" is a mop used for wet cleaning of floors. Staff use it to remove dirt and spills. Proper technique and hygiene standards are essential to maintain cleanliness and prevent contamination.

---

## Quiz

**Which phrase correctly describes using a mop?**

- A. モップで床を清掃いたします (we will clean the floor with a mop)
- B. モップありません (there is no mop)
- C. モップ自分で (use the mop yourself)
- D. モップ使う (use the mop)

**Correct Answer:** モップで床を清掃いたします (we will clean the floor with a mop)

**Explanation:** モップで床を清掃いたします — the で particle marks the tool being used; 清掃いたします is the humble form appropriate for hotel housekeeping staff.
