## 指示 (しじ · shiji)

**Meaning:** instruction

指示 refers to guidance or commands given to staff or guests during normal operations or emergencies. Clear, concise instructions ensure tasks are understood and executed safely. Staff must communicate effectively, especially under pressure. Proper instructions prevent confusion and accidents.

**Example:**

避難の際は、スタッフの指示に従ってください。

*During evacuation, follow the instructions of the staff.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** Guidance given to ensure safe or correct action is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 指示

**Explanation:** 指示 (shiji) means instruction — guidance or directives given to ensure safe and correct action during operations or emergencies.
