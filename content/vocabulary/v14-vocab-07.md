## 通貨 (つうか · tsuuka)

**Meaning:** currency

通貨 refers to money used in a country. Tourists must be aware of local currency for payments. Knowledge of exchange rates is important for budgeting. Currency handling impacts travel convenience and financial safety.

**Example:**

日本の通貨は円です。

*The currency of Japan is yen.*

---

## Quiz

**The money used in a country is called:**

- A. 税関 (customs)
- B. 通貨 (currency)
- C. 料金表 (price list)
- D. 両替所 (currency exchange counter)

**Correct Answer:** 通貨 (currency)

**Explanation:** 通貨 (tsuuka) means currency — the money system used in a country. 税関 = customs, 料金表 = price list, 両替所 = exchange office.
