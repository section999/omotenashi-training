**分** *(fun / pun)*

"分" indicates minutes for time estimation. Staff use it with "徒歩" or transport time. Clear use of minutes helps guests plan effectively. It prevents delays and confusion during check-ins or sightseeing.

*Example: 空港まで車で30分です。* (It's 30 minutes by car to the airport.)

---

## Quiz

**駅まで徒歩10____です。
(It's a 10-minute walk to the station.)**

- A. 分 (minutes)
- B. 秒 (seconds)
- C. 時間 (time / hours)
- D. 階 (floor / -th floor)

**Correct Answer:** 分 (minutes)

**Explanation:** 分 (minutes) is the standard time unit for walking distances — note the pronunciation change: 1分 (いっぷん), 3分 (さんぷん), 6分 (ろっぷん); using the wrong pronunciation sounds unnatural to Japanese guests.
