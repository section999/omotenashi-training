## 文化 (ぶんか · bunka)

**Meaning:** culture

文化 refers to a country or region's traditions, art, and social practices. Tourists often experience local culture through food, festivals, and customs. Understanding culture promotes respect and enjoyable interactions. Cultural knowledge enhances travel experiences.

**Example:**

日本の文化に興味があります。

*I am interested in Japanese culture.*

---

## Quiz

**Traditions, arts, and social practices of a country are called:**

- A. 文化 (culture)
- B. 習慣 (custom / habit)
- C. マナー (manners)
- D. 注意事項 (precautions)

**Correct Answer:** 文化 (culture)

**Explanation:** 文化 (bunka) means culture — the traditions, arts, and social practices of a society. 習慣 = customs/habits, マナー = manners, 注意事項 = precautions.
