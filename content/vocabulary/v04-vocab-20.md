**点検** *(tenken)*

"点検" means inspection or checking. Staff perform regular inspections of rooms, equipment, and amenities to maintain safety and service quality. Proper documentation ensures accountability.

---

## Quiz

**安全のため、定期的に設備の____を行っております。
(For safety, we conduct regular inspections of the facilities.)**

- A. 点検 (inspection)
- B. 修理 (repair)
- C. 破損 (damage)
- D. 補充 (replenishment / refill)

**Correct Answer:** 点検 (inspection)

**Explanation:** 点検 means systematic inspection — 定期的に (regularly) and 設備 (facilities/equipment) are key words in safety-related housekeeping communication.
