**身分証明書** *(mibun shomeisho)*

"身分証明書" refers to any identification document. It is used for verifying identity and ensuring security. Hotels may request it depending on policy. It is more general than "passport."

---

## Quiz

**Which is MOST appropriate when requesting an ID?**

- A. 身分証明書見せて (show your ID)
- B. 身分証明書を拝見してもよろしいでしょうか (may I see your ID?)
- C. 身分証明書ある？ (do you have your ID?)
- D. 身分証明書くださいよ (please give me your ID)

**Correct Answer:** 身分証明書を拝見してもよろしいでしょうか (may I see your ID?)

**Explanation:** 身分証明書を拝見してもよろしいでしょうか uses humble language (拝見) and asks permission politely — the correct professional approach.
