**夕食** *(yuushoku)*

"夕食" means dinner, typically the main meal of the day. Hotel dinner service may include multiple courses or special menus.

Staff should explain reservation requirements and dining hours clearly.

---

## Quiz

**本日の____は18時より開始いたします。
(Today's dinner begins at 6 p.m.)**

- A. 夕食 (dinner)
- B. 朝食 (breakfast)
- C. 昼食 (lunch)
- D. バイキング (buffet)

**Correct Answer:** 夕食 (dinner)

**Explanation:** 夕食 means dinner — 開始いたします (we begin) is the humble form appropriate when announcing the start of a hotel meal service.
