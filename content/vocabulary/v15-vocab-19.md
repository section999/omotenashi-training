## 利用時間 (りようじかん · riyou jikan)

**Meaning:** usage hours

利用時間 specifies the time period during which a service can be used. Important for scheduling laundry, lockers, or deliveries. Knowing 利用時間 prevents inconvenience or delays.

**Example:**

ランドリーの利用時間は午前7時から午後10時までです。

*Laundry hours are from 7 AM to 10 PM.*

---

## Quiz

**The period during which a service can be used is called:**

- A. 利用時間 (usage hours)
- B. 延長料金 (extension fee)
- C. 荷物 (luggage / baggage)
- D. 管理番号 (management number)

**Correct Answer:** 利用時間 (usage hours)

**Explanation:** 利用時間 (riyou jikan) means usage hours — the designated time period during which a service such as laundry or lockers is available. 延長料金 = extension fee, 荷物 = luggage, 管理番号 = tracking number.
