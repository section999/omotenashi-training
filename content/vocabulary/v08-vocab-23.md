**徒歩** *(toho)*

"徒歩" refers to traveling by walking. Staff use it to give directions for nearby locations. Providing approximate walking times improves guest planning. Clear communication prevents misunderstandings about distance.

*Example: 駅まで徒歩10分です。* (It's a 10-minute walk to the station.)

---

## Quiz

**スーパーはホテルから____5分です。
(The supermarket is a 5-minute walk from the hotel.)**

- A. 徒歩 (on foot / walking)
- B. 車 (car)
- C. 電車 (train)
- D. バス (bus)

**Correct Answer:** 徒歩 (on foot / walking)

**Explanation:** 徒歩 (on foot / walking) — 徒歩〇分 is the most useful distance format in hotel service; always combine it with a realistic estimate and consider the guest's condition (luggage, age) when advising whether to walk.
