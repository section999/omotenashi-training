**無料** *(muryou)*

"無料" means free of charge. Many hotel services such as Wi-Fi or water may be free. Staff should clearly inform guests. Transparency avoids misunderstandings.

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "Is Wi-Fi free?" Best response?**

- A. 無料でございます (it is free of charge)
- B. お金です (it costs money)
- C. 知らない (don't know)
- D. 無理 (impossible / not feasible)

**Correct Answer:** 無料でございます (it is free of charge)

**Explanation:** 無料でございます — a short, clear answer with ございます is best; guests asking about charges need reassurance quickly, and using でございます keeps it formal without adding unnecessary words.
