**宿泊日** *(shukuhaku bi)*

"宿泊日" refers to the exact dates the guest will stay at the hotel. It helps staff plan room assignments, prepare amenities, and coordinate housekeeping. This information is critical for billing, as well as for scheduling breakfast, dinner, and other services. Misunderstanding the stay date can lead to double bookings or missed services.

*Example: 宿泊日は4月10日から4月12日までです。* (The stay dates are from April 10 to April 12.)

---

## Quiz

**チェックインとチェックアウトの日をまとめて何と呼びますか。
(What do we call the check-in and check-out dates collectively?)**

- A. 宿泊日 (stay date)
- B. 予約日 (reservation date)
- C. 到着日 (arrival date)
- D. 出発日 (departure date)

**Correct Answer:** 宿泊日 (stay date)

**Explanation:** 宿泊日 encompasses the full stay window — knowing the guest's 宿泊日 allows staff to pre-assign the room, schedule housekeeping, and prepare any meal or amenity inclusions before arrival.
