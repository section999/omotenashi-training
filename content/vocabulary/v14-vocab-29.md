## マナー (まなー · manaa)

**Meaning:** manners

マナー is proper or polite behaviour expected in social situations. Tourists should follow local manners to show respect. Good manners prevent conflicts and enhance the experience for all. Observing manners includes etiquette in transport, dining, and public areas.

**Example:**

公共の場ではマナーを守りましょう。

*Let us observe good manners in public places.*

## Quiz

**Proper social behaviour is called:**

- A. 注意事項 (precautions)
- B. マナー (manners)
- C. 習慣 (custom / habit)
- D. 文化 (culture)

**Correct Answer:** マナー (manners)

**Explanation:** マナー (manaa) means manners — the expected polite behaviour in social situations. 注意事項 = precautions, 習慣 = customs, 文化 = culture.
