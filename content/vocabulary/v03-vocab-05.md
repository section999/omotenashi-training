**ツインルーム** *(tsuin rūmu)*

A twin room has two separate beds, ideal for friends or colleagues. Staff confirm this during check-in to match guest preferences. Correct room assignment ensures guest satisfaction.

---

## Quiz

**ご宿泊は____でよろしいでしょうか？
(Your stay will be in a twin room, is that okay?)**

- A. ツインルーム (twin room)
- B. シングルルーム (single room)
- C. ダブルルーム (double room)
- D. スイートルーム (suite room)

**Correct Answer:** ツインルーム (twin room)

**Explanation:** ツインルーム means a room with two separate beds — always confirm room type using the polite よろしいでしょうか form.
