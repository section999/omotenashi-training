**汚れ** *(yogore)*

"汚れ" refers to dirt, stains, or grime. Staff identify and clean these areas thoroughly. Reporting unusual or difficult stains may be necessary. Proper attention improves guest satisfaction.

---

## Quiz

**A guest points out a stain. How do you respond professionally?**

- A. 汚れをすぐに清掃いたします (we will clean the stain immediately)
- B. 汚れ放置 (leaving the stain unattended)
- C. 汚れ自分で (clean the stain yourself)
- D. 汚れです (it is a stain)

**Correct Answer:** 汚れをすぐに清掃いたします (we will clean the stain immediately)

**Explanation:** 汚れをすぐに清掃いたします means "we will clean the stain immediately" — すぐに (immediately) combined with いたします shows urgency and professionalism.
