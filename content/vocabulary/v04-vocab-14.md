**掃除機** *(soujiki)*

"掃除機" is the vacuum cleaner. Staff use it during room cleaning to maintain hygiene. Guests may rarely request it for special situations. Proper handling ensures safety and effectiveness.

---

## Quiz

**清掃の際、____を使用して床をきれいにいたします。
(During cleaning, we use a vacuum cleaner to clean the floor.)**

- A. 掃除機 (vacuum cleaner)
- B. モップ (mop)
- C. 雑巾 (cleaning cloth / rag)
- D. ゴミ箱 (trash can)

**Correct Answer:** 掃除機 (vacuum cleaner)

**Explanation:** 掃除機 (vacuum cleaner) is the primary tool for carpeted floors — knowing cleaning tool vocabulary helps staff describe their work accurately.
