**交差点** *(kousaten)*

"交差点" is an intersection where streets meet, crucial for navigation. Staff should indicate signals, turns, and nearby landmarks. Clear instructions prevent guests from taking wrong paths. It also helps in giving safe walking directions outside the hotel.

*Example: 交差点を渡って左に曲がってください。* (Cross the intersection and turn left.)

---

## Quiz

**A guest asks the safest way to reach the station. You say:**

- A. 交差点で信号を確認してください (please check the traffic light at the intersection)
- B. 交差点を無視してください (please ignore the intersection)
- C. まっすぐ進むだけです (just go straight ahead)
- D. 曲がらないでください (please do not turn)

**Correct Answer:** 交差点で信号を確認してください (please check the traffic light at the intersection)

**Explanation:** 交差点で信号を確認してください — reminding guests to check the signal (信号) at intersections shows care for their safety beyond just navigation, which is true omotenashi.
