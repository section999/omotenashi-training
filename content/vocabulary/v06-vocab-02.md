**トラブル** *(toraburu)*

"トラブル" refers to trouble or unexpected issues that may disrupt a guest's experience. It can include technical failures, noise issues, or service delays. Staff should remain calm, listen carefully, and provide immediate assistance. Quick resolution minimizes inconvenience and improves guest confidence.

---

## Quiz

**Guest reports an issue in the room. What do you say first?**

- A. トラブルについてお伺いいたします (I will ask about the issue)
- B. 知らない (don't know)
- C. 無理です (impossible / that's not possible)
- D. 自分で (by yourself / on your own)

**Correct Answer:** トラブルについてお伺いいたします (I will ask about the issue)

**Explanation:** トラブルについてお伺いいたします — お伺いいたします (I will listen / I will hear about it) is deeply humble and signals that the guest's concern is being taken seriously.
