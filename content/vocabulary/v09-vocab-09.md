**人数** *(ninzuu)*

"人数" refers to the total number of guests in the reservation. Staff use it to assign rooms, beds, and prepare meals. Correct information is crucial for safety, billing, and service planning. Guests often provide 人数 during booking or check-in.

*Example: 人数は大人2名、子供1名です。* (The number of people is 2 adults and 1 child.)

---

## Quiz

**部屋の予約では、____を必ず確認します。
(When booking a room, we always confirm the number of guests.)**

- A. 人数 (number of people)
- B. 日数 (number of days)
- C. 到着日 (arrival date)
- D. 予約日 (reservation date)

**Correct Answer:** 人数 (number of people)

**Explanation:** 人数 determines room capacity and billing — hotels have legal fire capacity limits per room, so confirming 人数 is not just a service step but also a safety obligation that staff must never skip.
