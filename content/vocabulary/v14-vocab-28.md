## 習慣 (しゅうかん · shuukan)

**Meaning:** custom / habit

習慣 refers to habitual behaviours and practices in society. Tourists should respect local customs to avoid misunderstandings. Customs may include greetings, dining etiquette, or daily routines. Awareness of 習慣 ensures culturally sensitive behaviour.

**Example:**

日本では靴を脱ぐ習慣があります。

*In Japan, there is a custom of removing shoes indoors.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** Social habits or traditional practices are called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 習慣

**Explanation:** 習慣 (shuukan) means custom or habit — the habitual behaviours and social practices observed within a culture.
