**メニュー** *(menyuu)*

"メニュー" is the list of food and beverages available in the restaurant. It may include descriptions, prices, and allergen information.

Staff should present it clearly and assist guests in understanding unfamiliar dishes.

---

## Quiz

**こちらが本日の____でございます。
(This is today's menu.)**

- A. メニュー (menu)
- B. 注文 (order)
- C. 提供 (provision)
- D. 会計 (billing / accounting)

**Correct Answer:** メニュー (menu)

**Explanation:** メニュー is the menu — こちらが本日の〜でございます is the standard phrase for presenting the menu at tableside with appropriate ceremony.
