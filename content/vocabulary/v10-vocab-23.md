**取消** *(torikeshi)*

"取消" refers to canceling a reservation, payment, or order. Staff must confirm details and apply cancellation policies. Clear instructions reduce confusion and disputes. Guests expect cancellation to be handled smoothly.

*Example: 予約を取消したいです。* (I want to cancel my reservation.)

---

## Quiz

**予約や注文を取りやめることは____です。
(Canceling a reservation or order is called cancellation.)**

- A. 取消 (cancellation)
- B. 返金 (refund)
- C. 変更 (change / modification)
- D. 精算 (settlement / final billing)

**Correct Answer:** 取消 (cancellation)

**Explanation:** 取消 triggers a chain of actions — confirming the 取消 activates the キャンセルポリシー, calculates any 返金 or キャンセル料, and frees the room in the system; doing steps out of order can result in double bookings or incorrect billing.
