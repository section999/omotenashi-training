## 乾燥 (かんそう · kansou)

**Meaning:** drying

乾燥 refers to removing moisture from washed items, typically using a dryer or hanging clothes to air dry. Proper 乾燥 prevents mold and unpleasant odors. Travelers often combine 洗濯 and 乾燥 for complete laundry care.

**Example:**

洗濯後、服を乾燥機で乾燥しました。

*After washing, I dried the clothes in the dryer.*

---

## Quiz

**Removing moisture from clothes is called:**

- A. 洗濯 (washing / laundry)
- B. 乾燥 (drying)
- C. 洗剤 (detergent)
- D. アイロン (iron)

**Correct Answer:** 乾燥 (drying)

**Explanation:** 乾燥 (kansou) means drying — removing moisture from washed items using a dryer or air drying. 洗濯 = washing, 洗剤 = detergent, アイロン = iron.
