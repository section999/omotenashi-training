## 病院 (びょういん · byouin)

**Meaning:** hospital

病院 is a medical facility where professional care is provided for illness or injury. Staff must know the nearest hospital and transportation options for guests. Quick access saves lives during emergencies. Clear directions and coordination with medical services are critical.

**Example:**

緊急の場合、病院まで送迎いたします。

*In emergencies, we can transport you to the hospital.*

---

## Quiz

**A guest needs urgent medical attention. Where should you take them?**

- A. 病院 (hospital)
- B. 消防車 (fire truck)
- C. 警報 (alarm)
- D. 安全 (safety / safe)

**Correct Answer:** 病院 (hospital)

**Explanation:** 病院 (byouin) means hospital — the medical facility for serious illness or injury. 消防車 = fire truck, 警報 = alarm, 安全 = safety.
