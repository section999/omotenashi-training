**お釣り** *(otsuri)*

"お釣り" is the amount returned when a guest overpays. Staff must count carefully and hand over politely. Accurate change prevents complaints and builds trust. Guests expect clarity and correctness.

*Example: 千円を出したら、お釣りは500円です。* (You paid 1,000 yen; your change is 500 yen.)

---

## Quiz

**Guest pays 10,000 yen for a 9,500 yen bill. What do they receive?**

- A. お釣り (change / money returned)
- B. 領収書 (receipt)
- C. 合計 (total)
- D. 精算 (settlement / final billing)

**Correct Answer:** お釣り (change / money returned)

**Explanation:** お釣りは500円でございます (your change is 500 yen) — always state the amount aloud while handing it over; counting change visibly on the counter (お客様の前で数える) is standard practice in Japanese service to confirm accuracy to both parties.
