**清掃済み** *(seisou zumi)*

"清掃済み" means the room has been cleaned. It indicates that cleaning is finished and the room is ready for guest use. Staff may place a card or sign to mark the room as ready.

---

## Quiz

**Which sentence correctly says "This room has been cleaned"?**

- A. この部屋は清掃済みでございます (this room has been cleaned)
- B. この部屋は清掃中です (this room is being cleaned)
- C. この部屋は汚れています (this room is dirty)
- D. この部屋は使用中です (this room is occupied)

**Correct Answer:** この部屋は清掃済みでございます (this room has been cleaned)

**Explanation:** 清掃済み means cleaning is complete — paired with でございます it confirms to guests or supervisors that the room is ready.
