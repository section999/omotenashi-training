**接続** *(setsuzoku)*

"接続" means connection, especially for internet or devices. Guests may need help connecting to Wi-Fi. Staff should guide step-by-step. Proper support improves convenience.

---

## Quiz

**Which phrase correctly offers to guide a guest through the connection process?**

- A. 接続方法をご案内いたします (I will guide you on how to connect)
- B. 接続できない (cannot connect)
- C. 知らない (don't know)
- D. 自分で (by yourself / on your own)

**Correct Answer:** 接続方法をご案内いたします (I will guide you on how to connect)

**Explanation:** 接続方法をご案内いたします — ご案内いたします (I will guide you) is the humble form for directing or explaining; 方法 (method) narrows the offer specifically to the how-to process.
