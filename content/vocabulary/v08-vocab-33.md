**フロアマップ** *(furoamappu)*

"フロアマップ" is a map showing the layout of floors. Staff provide it to help guests locate rooms, amenities, or exits. It reduces confusion in multi-floor buildings. Using visual aids enhances understanding.

*Example: こちらがフロアマップでございます。* (Here is the floor map.)

---

## Quiz

**エレベーターの横に____がございます。
(There is a floor map next to the elevator.)**

- A. フロアマップ (floor map)
- B. 案内図 (guide map)
- C. 案内板 (information board)
- D. 地図 (map)

**Correct Answer:** フロアマップ (floor map)

**Explanation:** フロアマップ (floor map) is placed at high-traffic points like elevators or stairwells — proactively offering こちらがフロアマップでございます when a guest looks uncertain is a simple but powerful act of kikubari.
