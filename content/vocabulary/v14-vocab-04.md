## ビザ (びざ · biza)

**Meaning:** visa

ビザ is an official document allowing entry to a foreign country. Different countries have different visa requirements and durations. Tourists, students, and workers must obtain appropriate visas. Checking visas ensures legal travel and avoids penalties.

**Example:**

入国前にビザの取得が必要です。

*You need to obtain a visa before entering.*

## Quiz

**Question:** An official document that allows entry into a country is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** ビザ

**Explanation:** ビザ (biza) means visa — the official document granting permission to enter a foreign country for a specific period.
