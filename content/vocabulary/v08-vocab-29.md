**上** *(ue)*

"上" indicates a higher position, such as floors or shelves. Staff combine it with elevators, stairs, or floors. Guests use it to locate rooms or amenities above. Accurate directions enhance guest satisfaction.

*Example: 会議室は二階の上です。* (The conference room is upstairs on the second floor.)

---

## Quiz

**倉庫は3階の____にございます。
(The storage room is upstairs on the 3rd floor.)**

- A. 上 (up / above)
- B. 下 (down / below)
- C. 奥 (back / deeper in)
- D. 外 (outside)

**Correct Answer:** 上 (up / above)

**Explanation:** 上 (up / above) — when combined with 階 (floor), 上 clarifies vertical position; for guests in corridors or shops, 上の棚 (upper shelf) or 上の階 (floor above) are practical examples of daily use.
