## 時刻表 (じこくひょう · jikokuhyou)

**Meaning:** timetable

時刻表 lists the schedule of transportation services. Tourists use timetables to plan trips efficiently. Accurate knowledge of schedules prevents missed connections. Timetables are available digitally or physically at stations.

**Example:**

電車の時刻表を確認しました。

*I checked the train timetable.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** A schedule showing departure and arrival times is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 時刻表

**Explanation:** 時刻表 (jikokuhyou) means timetable — a schedule listing departure and arrival times for trains, buses, and other transport.
