**修理依頼** *(shuuri irai)*

"修理依頼" means a request for repair when something is broken or not functioning properly. Staff must report it to maintenance promptly. Clear communication ensures faster resolution. Tracking requests helps maintain service quality.

---

## Quiz

**Guest says: "The TV is not working." Best response?**

- A. 修理依頼を承ります (we will accept your repair request)
- B. 直せません (cannot fix)
- C. 知らない (don't know)
- D. 自分で (by yourself / on your own)

**Correct Answer:** 修理依頼を承ります (we will accept your repair request)

**Explanation:** 修理依頼を承ります — 承ります (I receive/accept your request) is the formal humble acceptance phrase; it commits to action without overpromising a timeline.
