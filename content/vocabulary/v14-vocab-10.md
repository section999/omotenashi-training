## 観光地 (かんこうち · kankouchi)

**Meaning:** tourist spot

観光地 is a place of interest for visitors, such as landmarks, parks, or cultural sites. Tourist spots attract people and generate local revenue. Clear signs, guides, and accessibility improve the tourist experience. Planning visits helps avoid crowded times.

**Example:**

東京には多くの有名な観光地があります。

*Tokyo has many famous tourist spots.*

**Question:** A place visited by tourists is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 観光地

**Explanation:** 観光地 (kankouchi) means tourist spot — a location of interest such as a landmark, park, or cultural site that attracts visitors.
