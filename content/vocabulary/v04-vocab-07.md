**タオル交換** *(taoru koukan)*

"タオル交換" means towel replacement. Guests may request fresh towels daily or multiple times during the stay. Staff should respond promptly and politely to maintain comfort and hygiene.

---

## Quiz

**Which phrase correctly offers towel exchange service?**

- A. タオル交換はご希望のときに承ります (towel changes are available upon request)
- B. タオル交換ありません (no towel change)
- C. タオル交換自分で (change towels yourself)
- D. タオル交換しない (will not change towels)

**Correct Answer:** タオル交換はご希望のときに承ります (towel changes are available upon request)

**Explanation:** タオル交換はご希望のときに承ります means "we will handle towel changes whenever you wish" — 承ります is the polite humble form of receiving a request.
