**歯ブラシ** *(haburashi)*

"歯ブラシ" is a toothbrush provided in rooms. Staff may supply it upon check-in or upon request. Ensuring hygiene and timely replacement is important for guest satisfaction.

---

## Quiz

**必要であれば、歯ブラシを____いたします。
(If necessary, we will provide a toothbrush.)**

- A. お持ち (bringing (polite))
- B. お掃除 (cleaning (polite))
- C. お取り (taking (polite))
- D. お借り (borrowing (polite))

**Correct Answer:** お持ち (bringing (polite))

**Explanation:** お持ちいたします means "I will bring it" — the お持ち stem with いたします forms the humble request-fulfillment phrase used across hotel service.
