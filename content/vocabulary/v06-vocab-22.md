**交換希望** *(koukan kibou)*

"交換希望" means a request for replacement, such as changing items or rooms. Staff should confirm reasons and respond appropriately. Proper handling improves satisfaction. Flexibility is important.

---

## Quiz

**お客様はタオルの____でございます。
(The guest has a replacement request for a towel.)**

- A. 交換希望 (exchange requested)
- B. 修理依頼 (repair request)
- C. 忘れ物 (lost and found / forgotten item)
- D. 紛失 (lost item)

**Correct Answer:** 交換希望 (exchange requested)

**Explanation:** 交換希望 (replacement request) — distinguishing between 交換希望 (guest wants a swap) and 修理依頼 (needs repair) ensures the correct team or action is triggered without unnecessary back-and-forth.
