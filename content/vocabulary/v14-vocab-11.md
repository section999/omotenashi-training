## 名所 (めいしょ · meisho)

**Meaning:** famous place

名所 refers to well-known locations, often historical or cultural. Famous places attract both domestic and international tourists. Knowledge of 名所 enhances sightseeing experiences. Local guides often highlight these places for educational purposes.

**Example:**

京都の金閣寺は人気の名所です。

*Kinkaku-ji in Kyoto is a famous place.*

---

## Quiz

**A well-known, historically or culturally notable place is called:**

- A. 観光地 (tourist destination)
- B. 名所 (sightseeing spot / landmark)
- C. おすすめ (recommendation)
- D. 地図アプリ (map app)

**Correct Answer:** 名所 (sightseeing spot / landmark)

**Explanation:** 名所 (meisho) means famous place — a well-known location of historical or cultural significance. 観光地 = tourist spot (broader), おすすめ = recommendation, 地図アプリ = map app.
