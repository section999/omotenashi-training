**こんばんは** *(konbanwa)*

This phrase means "good evening" and is used in the evening or nighttime. It maintains a polite tone suitable for customer service situations. Hotel staff often use this when greeting guests returning at night. It helps create a warm and respectful atmosphere.

---

## Quiz

**____。お疲れ様でございます。
(Good evening. Thank you for your hard work.)**

- A. こんにちは (hello / good afternoon)
- B. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- C. こんばんは (good evening)
- D. どうぞ (please / go ahead)

**Correct Answer:** こんばんは (good evening)

**Explanation:** こんばんは is the polite evening greeting, appropriate when welcoming guests in the evening or at night.
