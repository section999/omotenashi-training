**右** *(migi)*

"右" means "right" and is used for directions in hallways, streets, or inside buildings. Staff should clearly distinguish it from left to prevent guest confusion. It is commonly combined with landmarks or distance. Proper use ensures safe and efficient navigation.

*Example: 次の角を右に曲がってください。* (Please turn right at the next corner.)

---

## Quiz

**この道をまっすぐ行って、次の角を____に曲がってください。
(Go straight and turn right at the next corner.)**

- A. 右 (right)
- B. 左 (left)
- C. 奥 (back / deeper in)
- D. 手前 (in front / near side)

**Correct Answer:** 右 (right)

**Explanation:** 右 means right — when giving directional guidance, combining 右 with a landmark (次の角, this corner) makes the instruction concrete and reduces the chance of misunderstanding.
