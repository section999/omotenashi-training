**コーヒー** *(koohii)*

"コーヒー" is a common beverage served after meals or during breakfast. Hotels may offer different types such as espresso or drip coffee.

Staff should ask preferences like hot or iced.

---

## Quiz

**食後に____はいかがでしょうか。
(How about coffee after your meal?)**

- A. コーヒー (coffee)
- B. お水 (water)
- C. 紅茶 (black tea)
- D. メニュー (menu)

**Correct Answer:** コーヒー (coffee)

**Explanation:** 〜はいかがでしょうか is the polite way to offer something — it is softer and more elegant than asking directly, ideal for upselling beverages after a meal.
