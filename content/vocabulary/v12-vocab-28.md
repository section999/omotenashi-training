## 緊急対応 (きんきゅうたいおう · kinkyuu taiou)

**Meaning:** emergency response

緊急対応 refers to the actions taken to handle emergencies efficiently, such as fires, medical incidents, or natural disasters. Staff must be trained to react quickly, communicate clearly, and follow protocols. Coordination with emergency services is essential. Effective response reduces injury and damage.

**Example:**

火災発生時は緊急対応マニュアルに従ってください。

*Follow the emergency response manual in case of fire.*

## Quiz

**What term describes staff actions taken during urgent emergencies?**

- A. 緊急対応 (emergency response)
- B. 注意 (caution / attention)
- C. 点検中 (under inspection)
- D. 応急処置 (first aid)

**Correct Answer:** 緊急対応 (emergency response)

**Explanation:** 緊急対応 (kinkyuu taiou) means emergency response — coordinated actions taken to handle a crisis. 注意 = caution, 点検中 = under inspection, 応急処置 = first aid.
