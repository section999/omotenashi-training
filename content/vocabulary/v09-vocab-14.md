**割引** *(waribiki)*

"割引" is a price reduction applied for early bookings, promotions, or loyalty programs. Staff apply it carefully to ensure accurate billing. Discounts attract more guests and improve satisfaction. Clear explanation avoids confusion or disputes.

*Example: 早割で10％割引になります。* (You get a 10% discount with early booking.)

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks about early booking price reduction. Which term applies?**

- A. 割引 (discount)
- B. 追加料金 (additional charge)
- C. 特典 (benefit / privilege)
- D. プラン (plan)

**Correct Answer:** 割引 (discount)

**Explanation:** 割引 (discount) — when applying a 割引, always state the original rate and the discounted rate separately (通常〇円のところ、〇円割引で〇円でございます) so guests clearly understand what they are saving and feel the benefit.
