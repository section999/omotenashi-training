**シーツ交換** *(shiitsu koukan)*

"シーツ交換" refers to changing bed sheets. Hotels perform this regularly or upon guest request. Proper handling ensures hygiene and prevents discomfort. Staff should confirm if guests want extra or immediate changes.

---

## Quiz

**本日、ベッドの____を行いました。
(Today, we performed the sheet change for your bed.)**

- A. シーツ交換 (sheet change)
- B. タオル交換 (towel change)
- C. 補充 (replenishment / refill)
- D. 点検 (inspection)

**Correct Answer:** シーツ交換 (sheet change)

**Explanation:** シーツ交換 specifically means changing bed sheets — knowing the difference from タオル交換 (towel change) avoids guest confusion.
