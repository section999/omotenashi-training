## 注意 (ちゅうい · chuui)

**Meaning:** caution

注意 indicates that extra care or alertness is required to prevent accidents. Staff often combine verbal warnings and signage to communicate hazards. Awareness reduces the likelihood of injury. Proper use of caution signs ensures guests understand potential risks.

**Example:**

床が濡れていますので、注意してください。

*The floor is wet, so please be careful.*

---

## Quiz

**Which word is used to alert someone to potential risk?**

- A. 注意 (caution / attention)
- B. 危険 (danger)
- C. 安全 (safety / safe)
- D. 応急処置 (first aid)

**Correct Answer:** 注意 (caution / attention)

**Explanation:** 注意 (chuui) means caution — a warning to take care. 危険 = danger (stronger level), 安全 = safety, 応急処置 = first aid.
