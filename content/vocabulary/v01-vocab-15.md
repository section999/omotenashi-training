**お手伝いしましょうか** *(otetsudai shimashou ka)*

This means "may I help you?" It is used to offer assistance to guests. It is polite and shows attentiveness and care. This phrase is essential in hospitality to improve customer satisfaction.

---

## Quiz

**お荷物を____？
(May I help with your luggage?)**

- A. 恐れ入ります (I appreciate / excuse me (polite))
- B. お手伝いしましょうか (may I help you?)
- C. ごゆっくりどうぞ (please take your time)
- D. かしこまりました (certainly / understood (very formal))

**Correct Answer:** お手伝いしましょうか (may I help you?)

**Explanation:** お手伝いしましょうか offers assistance politely — it shows proactive care and attentiveness toward the guest.
