**シャンプー** *(shanpuu)*

"シャンプー" is shampoo provided in guest bathrooms. Staff must ensure sufficient supply and check for guest needs. Proper handling ensures guest comfort and cleanliness.

---

## Quiz

**お部屋のバスルームには____をご用意しております。
(Shampoo is provided in your room's bathroom.)**

- A. シャンプー (shampoo)
- B. 石鹸 (soap)
- C. タオル (towel)
- D. ゴミ箱 (trash can)

**Correct Answer:** シャンプー (shampoo)

**Explanation:** シャンプー is a standard bathroom amenity — ご用意しております (we have prepared) is the polite way to inform guests what is available.
