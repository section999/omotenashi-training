**失礼いたします** *(shitsurei itashimasu)*

This phrase means "excuse me" in a very polite way. It is used before entering a room, interrupting someone, or leaving a conversation. In hotels, staff use it to maintain professionalism. It shows respect for the other person's space and time.

---

## Quiz

**お部屋に入ります。____。
(I will enter the room. Excuse me.)**

- A. 少々お待ちください (please wait a moment)
- B. どうぞ (please / go ahead)
- C. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- D. 失礼いたします (excuse me)

**Correct Answer:** 失礼いたします (excuse me)

**Explanation:** 失礼いたします is used when entering a space or interrupting — it acknowledges the intrusion politely.
