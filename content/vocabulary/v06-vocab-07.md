**紛失** *(funshitsu)*

"紛失" means loss of an item, usually reported by the guest. Staff should assist in searching and confirm details. Proper reporting procedures are necessary. Clear communication helps manage expectations.

---

## Quiz

**Guest says: "I lost my key." Best response?**

- A. 紛失について確認いたします (we will confirm about the lost item)
- B. 無理です (impossible / that's not possible)
- C. 知らない (don't know)
- D. 自分で (by yourself / on your own)

**Correct Answer:** 紛失について確認いたします (we will confirm about the lost item)

**Explanation:** 紛失について確認いたします — 確認いたします (I will check/verify) signals immediate action; responding calmly reassures the guest and begins the recovery process.
