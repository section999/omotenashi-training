**日本語対応** *(nihongo taiou)*

"日本語対応" indicates staff can assist in Japanese. Important for domestic guests or when verifying documentation. Ensures clear and accurate communication.

*Example: 日本語対応可能です。* (We can assist you in Japanese.)

---

## Quiz

**日本語で対応できることは____です。
(Being able to assist in Japanese is called Japanese language support.)**

- A. 日本語対応 (Japanese support)
- B. 英語対応 (English support)
- C. 外国語 (foreign language)
- D. 通訳 (interpretation)

**Correct Answer:** 日本語対応 (Japanese support)

**Explanation:** 日本語対応 is the default expectation in Japan — however, specifying 日本語対応 is useful when advertising to international operators or on multilingual websites, signaling that Japanese-speaking guests will receive full service rather than being redirected to a translator.
