**音声案内** *(onsei annai)*

"音声案内" provides automated instructions over the phone. Staff must guide guests to follow prompts accurately. It helps in routing calls and providing information efficiently. Clear instructions prevent mistakes or confusion.

*Example: 音声案内に従って番号を押してください。* (Please follow the voice guidance to press the numbers.)

---

## Quiz

**What guides the caller automatically by voice?**

- A. 音声案内 (audio guidance)
- B. 留守番電話 (voicemail / answering machine)
- C. 担当者 (person in charge)
- D. 内線 (internal / extension line)

**Correct Answer:** 音声案内 (audio guidance)

**Explanation:** 音声案内 is the automated phone menu — when a guest seems confused by it, offer to guide them through (音声案内で1番を押してください、その後に2番をお押しください); stepping the guest through the IVR system personally is a small but memorable act of service.
