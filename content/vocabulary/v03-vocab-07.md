**禁煙** *(kin'en)*

"禁煙" means non-smoking. Hotels clearly label non-smoking rooms for guest comfort. Staff must confirm this preference during booking or check-in.

---

## Quiz

**ご希望は____でございますか？
(Is your preference non-smoking?)**

- A. 禁煙 (non-smoking)
- B. 喫煙 (smoking)
- C. 空室 (vacancy / available room)
- D. 満室 (fully booked / no vacancies)

**Correct Answer:** 禁煙 (non-smoking)

**Explanation:** 禁煙 means no smoking — always confirm smoking preferences early to ensure the correct room is assigned.
