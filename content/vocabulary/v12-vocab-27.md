## 安全確認 (あんぜんかくにん · anzen kakunin)

**Meaning:** safety check

安全確認 refers to verifying that equipment, systems, or areas are safe for use. Staff must inspect and confirm conditions before allowing guests to enter or operate facilities. Regular safety checks prevent accidents and ensure compliance. Documentation is often required for accountability.

**Example:**

エレベーター使用前に安全確認を行いました。

*We conducted a safety check before using the elevator.*
