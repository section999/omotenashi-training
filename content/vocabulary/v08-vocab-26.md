**秒** *(byou)*

"秒" refers to seconds, often used in precise timing, such as elevator doors or train schedules. Staff should use it in combination with minutes for clarity. It ensures guests are aware of exact waiting or travel times.

*Example: エレベーターのドアは5秒で閉まります。* (The elevator doors close in 5 seconds.)

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "How fast do the doors close?"**

- A. 5秒で閉まります (it closes in 5 seconds)
- B. 5分で閉まります (it closes in 5 minutes)
- C. 1時間です (it is one hour)
- D. 開いたままです (remains open)

**Correct Answer:** 5秒で閉まります (it closes in 5 seconds)

**Explanation:** 5秒で閉まります — 秒 (seconds) applies to very short intervals like elevator doors; knowing this precision shows guests you are aware of their safety and helps prevent them from rushing or being caught.
