**パスポート** *(passport)*

A passport is required for identity verification, especially for foreign guests. It is checked during check-in as part of legal requirements. Staff must request it politely. Handling personal documents carefully is very important.

---

## Quiz

**パスポートを____してもよろしいでしょうか。
(May I see your passport?)**

- A. 拝見 (to humbly look at / see)
- B. 確認 (confirmation)
- C. 見て (please look)
- D. 検査 (inspection / examination)

**Correct Answer:** 拝見 (to humbly look at / see)

**Explanation:** 拝見 (haiken) is the humble form of "to see/look at" — the correct polite verb for viewing a guest's documents.
