**清掃** *(seisō)*

"清掃" means cleaning. Staff use it to describe daily room cleaning or additional housekeeping. Guests may request special cleaning times. Clear explanation ensures expectations are aligned.

---

## Quiz

**清掃は午前10時までに____いたします。
(Cleaning will be done by 10 a.m.)**

- A. 完了 (completion / done)
- B. 開始 (start)
- C. 中止 (cancellation / stop)
- D. 延期 (postponement)

**Correct Answer:** 完了 (completion / done)

**Explanation:** 完了いたします means "will be completed" — informing guests of the cleaning schedule helps them plan their day accordingly.
