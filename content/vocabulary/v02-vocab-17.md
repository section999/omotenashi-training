**現金** *(genkin)*

"現金" means cash and is one of the basic payment methods. Hotels usually confirm how the guest would like to pay. It is important to ask politely rather than assume. This helps avoid confusion at checkout.

---

## Quiz

**Choose the most natural and polite sentence:**

- A. 現金？ (cash?)
- B. 現金ですか？ (will it be cash?)
- C. お支払いは現金でよろしいでしょうか (will cash payment be fine?)
- D. 現金する？ (paying by cash?)

**Correct Answer:** お支払いは現金でよろしいでしょうか (will cash payment be fine?)

**Explanation:** お支払いは現金でよろしいでしょうか uses the honorific お支払い and the polite confirmation form よろしいでしょうか.
