**寒い** *(samui)*

"寒い" means cold and indicates discomfort due to low temperature. Staff should check heating systems or provide blankets. Prompt action ensures guest comfort. Proper response shows attentiveness.

---

## Quiz

**寒い場合は、____を調整いたします。
(If it is cold, we will adjust the air conditioning.)**

- A. 空調 (air conditioning / HVAC)
- B. 照明 (lighting)
- C. テレビ (TV / television)
- D. シャワー (shower)

**Correct Answer:** 空調 (air conditioning / HVAC)

**Explanation:** 空調を調整いたします — 調整いたします (we will adjust) covers both heating and cooling; mentioning 空調 specifically reassures the guest that the cause of their discomfort will be addressed.
