**変更** *(henkō)*

"変更" means change or modification. It is used when adjusting reservation details such as dates or room types. Hotels try to accommodate changes when possible. Staff should respond politely and confirm availability.

---

## Quiz

**ご予約の変更は____でございます。
(Changes to your reservation are possible.)**

- A. 可能 (possible)
- B. 不可 (not possible)
- C. ご確認 (confirmation (polite))
- D. 延泊 (extended stay)

**Correct Answer:** 可能 (possible)

**Explanation:** 可能 (kanō) means "possible" — ご予約の変更は可能でございます is the polite and clear way to confirm that a modification can be made.
