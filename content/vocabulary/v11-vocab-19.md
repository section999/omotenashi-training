**確認いたします** *(kakunin itashimasu)*

"確認いたします" is used to politely assure a guest that the staff will verify information. It conveys professionalism and attentiveness. Staff must follow up promptly after checking. Clear communication prevents misunderstandings.

*Example: 担当者に確認いたします。* (I will check with the person in charge.)

---

## Quiz

**Which phrase means "I will check"?**

- A. 確認いたします (I will confirm)
- B. お繋ぎします (I will connect you)
- C. 少々お待ちください (please wait a moment)
- D. 折り返し (call back / return call)

**Correct Answer:** 確認いたします (I will confirm)

**Explanation:** 確認いたします is a promise to act — always follow through; if the verification takes time, return to the caller with an update (確認に少々時間をいただいております) rather than leaving them waiting in silence, which erodes trust.
