**かしこまりました** *(kashikomarimashita)*

This is a very polite way to say "certainly" or "understood." It is used when responding to a customer's request. Compared to "wakarimashita," it is more formal and appropriate in service industries. It shows respect and readiness to assist.

---

## Quiz

**はい、____。すぐにご用意いたします。
(Certainly. I will prepare it right away.)**

- A. ありがとうございます (thank you)
- B. 失礼いたします (excuse me)
- C. 申し訳ございません (I am very sorry)
- D. かしこまりました (certainly / understood (very formal))

**Correct Answer:** かしこまりました (certainly / understood (very formal))

**Explanation:** かしこまりました is the most formal way to say "certainly" — the gold standard response to any guest request.
