## 交通機関 (こうつうきかん · koutsuu kikan)

**Meaning:** transportation

交通機関 refers to systems used for moving people, such as trains, buses, or taxis. Understanding transportation options is essential for efficient travel. Schedules, fares, and connections affect tourist planning. Public transportation knowledge improves safety and convenience.

**Example:**

日本の交通機関はとても便利です。

*Transportation in Japan is very convenient.*

## Quiz

**Systems used for moving people from place to place are called:**

- A. 道案内 (directions)
- B. 交通機関 (public transportation)
- C. 地図アプリ (map app)
- D. 切符 (ticket)

**Correct Answer:** 交通機関 (public transportation)

**Explanation:** 交通機関 (koutsuu kikan) means transportation — the collective systems for moving people, including trains, buses, and taxis. 道案内 = directions, 地図アプリ = map app, 切符 = ticket.
