## 警報 (けいほう · keihou)

**Meaning:** alarm

警報 is a signal alerting people to imminent danger such as fire, earthquake, or gas leak. It can be auditory, visual, or both. Staff must respond immediately upon hearing a warning. Regular testing ensures alarms function properly during emergencies.

**Example:**

火災警報が鳴ったらすぐに避難してください。

*When the fire alarm sounds, evacuate immediately.*

## Quiz

**Which of the following indicates a signal for danger?**

- A. 警報 (alarm)
- B. 安全 (safety / safe)
- C. 事故 (accident)
- D. 救助 (rescue)

**Correct Answer:** 警報 (alarm)

**Explanation:** 警報 (keihou) means alarm or warning signal — an auditory or visual alert to danger. 安全 = safety, 事故 = accident, 救助 = rescue.
