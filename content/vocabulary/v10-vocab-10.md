**領収書発行** *(ryoushuusho hakkou)*

"領収書発行" is issuing a receipt as proof of payment. Staff must ensure the name, amount, and date are correct. Receipts are crucial for reimbursement or business records. Timely issuance improves guest trust.

*Example: 領収書を発行いたしますか。* (Would you like a receipt issued?)

---

## Quiz

**Providing official proof of payment is called?**

- A. 領収書発行 (receipt issuance)
- B. 内訳 (breakdown)
- C. 合計 (total)
- D. 精算 (settlement / final billing)

**Correct Answer:** 領収書発行 (receipt issuance)

**Explanation:** 領収書発行 — always ask 宛名はいかがいたしましょうか (what name shall I put on the receipt?) before printing; a receipt with the wrong addressee cannot be used for business reimbursement and will require a time-consuming reissue.
