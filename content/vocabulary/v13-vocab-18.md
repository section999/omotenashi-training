## 連絡 (れんらく · renraku)

**Meaning:** contact / communication

連絡 is the act of communicating information to colleagues or management. It can be via phone, email, or in person. Effective contact ensures workflow continuity and reduces misunderstandings. Prompt responses are important in a professional environment.

**Example:**

急用があるので、上司に連絡しました。

*I contacted my boss because of an urgent matter.*
