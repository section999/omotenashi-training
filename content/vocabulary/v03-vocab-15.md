**シャワー** *(shawā)*

"シャワー" refers to a shower facility in the room. Staff may explain its use, especially for foreign guests. It is essential for comfort.

---

## Quiz

**お部屋の____は温度調節が可能でございます。
(The shower in your room can adjust temperature.)**

- A. シャワー (shower)
- B. エアコン (air conditioning)
- C. テレビ (TV / television)
- D. 冷蔵庫 (refrigerator)

**Correct Answer:** シャワー (shower)

**Explanation:** シャワーは温度調節が可能でございます — informing guests about room features politely prevents confusion and enhances comfort.
