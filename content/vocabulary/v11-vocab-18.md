**接続不良** *(setsuzoku furyou)*

"接続不良" indicates a weak or failed connection. Staff must troubleshoot and resolve promptly. Guests expect stable calls, especially for important matters. Proper handling minimizes frustration.

*Example: 接続不良で通話が途切れました。* (The call was interrupted due to poor connection.)

---

## Quiz

**通話が途切れる状態は____です。
(The state where a call keeps breaking up is poor connection.)**

- A. 接続不良 (poor connection)
- B. 切断 (disconnection)
- C. 保留 (hold / on hold)
- D. 転送 (transfer / forwarding)

**Correct Answer:** 接続不良 (poor connection)

**Explanation:** 接続不良 vs 切断 — 接続不良 is intermittent (断続的に途切れる) while 切断 is a complete break; when reporting to maintenance, specifying which helps them diagnose faster and reduces the guest's total time without a working line.
