**承知いたしました** *(shouchi itashimashita)*

This is a very formal way to say "understood" or "certainly." It is more polite and respectful than "wakarimashita." It is often used when responding to customer requests or instructions. This phrase shows professionalism and careful attention to the guest's needs.

---

## Quiz

**ご要望、____。
(I understand your request.)**

- A. かしこまりました (certainly / understood (very formal))
- B. 承知いたしました (certainly / understood)
- C. 申し訳ございません (I am very sorry)
- D. ありがとうございます (thank you)

**Correct Answer:** 承知いたしました (certainly / understood)

**Explanation:** 承知いたしました is a very formal acknowledgement — more polished than wakarimashita, used for important guest requests.
