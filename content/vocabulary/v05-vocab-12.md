**おかわり** *(okawari)*

"おかわり" means a refill or second serving. It is commonly used for drinks, rice, or buffet items.

Staff should offer refills proactively when appropriate.

---

## Quiz

**お飲み物の____はいかがですか。
(Would you like a refill for your drink?)**

- A. おかわり (refill / second helping)
- B. 注文 (order)
- C. 提供 (provision)
- D. 水 (water)

**Correct Answer:** おかわり (refill / second helping)

**Explanation:** おかわり means a refill — proactively offering おかわり before the guest needs to ask is a core expression of kikubari (attentive anticipation) in F&B service.
