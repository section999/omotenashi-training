**毛布** *(mōfu)*

"毛布" means blanket. Some guests request extra blankets, especially in cold seasons. Staff should confirm politely and provide promptly.

---

## Quiz

**追加の____をお持ちいたしましょうか？
(Shall I bring you an extra blanket?)**

- A. 毛布 (blanket)
- B. 枕 (pillow)
- C. シーツ (bed sheets)
- D. タオル (towel)

**Correct Answer:** 毛布 (blanket)

**Explanation:** 毛布 means blanket — お持ちいたしましょうか is the polite offer to bring something, showing proactive care.
