## 報告 (ほうこく · houkoku)

**Meaning:** report

報告 refers to providing information on completed tasks, incidents, or status updates. Timely reporting ensures management is informed for decision-making. Reports maintain transparency and accountability. Employees must report accurately and professionally.

**Example:**

業務の進捗を上司に報告しました。

*I reported the progress of work to my boss.*

---

## Quiz

**Providing information on tasks or incidents is called:**

- A. 連絡 (contact / communication)
- B. 報告 (report)
- C. 会議 (meeting / conference)
- D. 指導 (guidance / instruction)

**Correct Answer:** 報告 (report)

**Explanation:** 報告 (houkoku) means report — sharing information on completed tasks or incidents. 連絡 = contact/communication, 会議 = meeting, 指導 = guidance.
