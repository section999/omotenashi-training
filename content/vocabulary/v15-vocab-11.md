## 発送 (はっそう · hassou)

**Meaning:** shipping

発送 refers to sending items to another location, domestically or internationally. Hotels often assist guests with 発送 of souvenirs or parcels. Proper packaging and labelling are important to ensure safe arrival.

**Example:**

荷物を宅配便で発送しました。

*I shipped my package via courier.*

---

## Quiz

**Sending items to another location is called:**

- A. 荷物 (luggage / baggage)
- B. 発送 (shipping / sending)
- C. 受取 (receipt / pickup)
- D. 配達 (delivery)

**Correct Answer:** 発送 (shipping / sending)

**Explanation:** 発送 (hassou) means shipping — sending items domestically or internationally. 荷物 = luggage, 受取 = pickup/receipt, 配達 = delivery.
