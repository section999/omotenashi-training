**テレビ** *(terebi)*

"テレビ" means television. Staff may explain how to operate it, channel lists, or on-demand services. Proper explanation enhances the guest experience. Polite guidance is expected, especially for foreign visitors.

---

## Quiz

**お部屋の____はリモコンで操作可能でございます。
(The TV in your room can be operated with the remote.)**

- A. テレビ (TV / television)
- B. エアコン (air conditioning)
- C. 冷蔵庫 (refrigerator)
- D. シャワー (shower)

**Correct Answer:** テレビ (TV / television)

**Explanation:** テレビはリモコンで操作可能でございます — clear and polite instruction about room equipment is a key part of check-in service.
