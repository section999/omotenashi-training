## 作業 (さぎょう · sagyou)

**Meaning:** task / work

作業 refers to specific pieces of work or tasks performed during duty. Tasks can be part of larger projects or daily operations. Proper planning and execution ensure high-quality output. Recording completed tasks improves accountability.

**Example:**

倉庫での作業は安全に行ってください。

*Please perform tasks in the warehouse safely.*

---

## Quiz

**A specific piece of work or duty is called:**

- A. 作業 (work / task)
- B. 業務 (work / operations)
- C. 勤務 (work / duty)
- D. 指導 (guidance / instruction)

**Correct Answer:** 作業 (work / task)

**Explanation:** 作業 (sagyou) means task or work — a specific piece of work. 業務 = all assigned duties (broader), 勤務 = working duty/period, 指導 = guidance.
