## 会議 (かいぎ · kaigi)

**Meaning:** meeting

会議 is a formal gathering where employees discuss plans, review performance, and make decisions. Meetings are essential for communication, problem-solving, and aligning team goals. Proper preparation and participation ensure productive meetings. Documentation of meetings supports accountability.

**Example:**

明日の会議は午前10時から始まります。

*Tomorrow's meeting starts at 10 AM.*

---

## Quiz

**A formal gathering to discuss work matters is called:**

- A. 打ち合わせ (meeting)
- B. 会議 (meeting / conference)
- C. 報告 (report)
- D. 連絡 (contact / communication)

**Correct Answer:** 会議 (meeting / conference)

**Explanation:** 会議 (kaigi) means meeting — a formal gathering for discussion and decision-making. 打ち合わせ = briefing (informal), 報告 = report, 連絡 = contact.
