## 研修 (けんしゅう · kenshuu)

**Meaning:** training

研修 refers to structured programmes designed to improve employees' skills and knowledge. Training ensures staff understand procedures, safety rules, and company standards. Effective training boosts productivity and reduces errors. Continuous learning helps career growth and organisational efficiency.

**Example:**

新入社員は入社後に1週間の研修があります。

*New employees have a one-week training after joining.*

**Explanation:** 研修 (kenshuu) means training — structured programmes to improve employee skills, knowledge, and adherence to company standards.
