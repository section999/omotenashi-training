**避難経路** *(hinan keiro)*

"避難経路" shows the path to evacuate safely. Staff explain it during check-in or tours. It is critical for fire safety and disaster preparedness. Clear marking and instruction prevent confusion.

*Example: 避難経路は廊下の右側です。* (The evacuation route is along the right side of the corridor.)

---

## Quiz

**火災の場合は____に従ってください。
(In case of fire, please follow the evacuation route.)**

- A. 避難経路 (evacuation route)
- B. 案内図 (guide map)
- C. フロアマップ (floor map)
- D. 案内板 (information board)

**Correct Answer:** 避難経路 (evacuation route)

**Explanation:** 避難経路 (evacuation route) — knowing this word is essential not just for guests but for all hotel staff; 避難経路に従う (follow the evacuation route) is the core instruction in any fire or disaster drill.
