**ドア** *(doa)*

"ドア" refers to the door of the guest room. Staff may explain security features, automatic locks, or emergency exit instructions. Clear instructions reduce confusion and improve safety.

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "How do I lock the door?" Which is the correct response?**

- A. ドアは内側から鍵を回してください (please lock the door from the inside)
- B. ドア開ける (open the door)
- C. ドア閉める (close the door)
- D. ドアどうぞ (please go through the door)

**Correct Answer:** ドアは内側から鍵を回してください (please lock the door from the inside)

**Explanation:** ドアは内側から鍵を回してください clearly explains the action — security instructions must always be clear and complete.
