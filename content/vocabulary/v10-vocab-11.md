**明細書** *(meisaisho)*

"明細書" is a detailed financial statement for services used. Staff provide this to help guests or companies verify charges. It lists amounts, dates, and service details. Statements prevent disputes and aid accounting.

*Example: ご希望であれば明細書をお渡しします。* (We can provide a statement if you wish.)

---

## Quiz

**料金の詳細が書かれた書類は____です。
(The document with detailed charge information is an itemized statement.)**

- A. 明細書 (itemized statement)
- B. 請求書 (invoice / bill)
- C. 領収書 (receipt)
- D. 伝票 (bill slip)

**Correct Answer:** 明細書 (itemized statement)

**Explanation:** 明細書 differs from 領収書 — a 領収書 confirms payment was made, while a 明細書 shows what was charged and why; corporate guests often need both: the 明細書 to verify charges and the 領収書 to prove payment to their accounts department.
