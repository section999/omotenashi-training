## 受取 (うけとり · uketori)

**Meaning:** pickup / receipt

受取 is the act of receiving goods, parcels, or laundry. Ensures correct handling and delivery confirmation. Hotels often manage 受取 for packages or laundry. Verification using tracking numbers or claim tickets is standard.

**Example:**

宅配便の荷物を受取に行きました。

*I went to pick up my courier package.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** The act of receiving items from a service is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 受取

**Explanation:** 受取 (uketori) means pickup or receipt — the act of receiving goods, parcels, or laundry with verification.
