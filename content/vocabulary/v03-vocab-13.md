**タオル** *(taoru)*

"タオル" refers to a towel, typically for hands or face. Staff should provide towels and replenish them as needed.

---

## Quiz

**こちらがご用意した____でございます。
(This is the towel we have prepared for you.)**

- A. タオル (towel)
- B. シーツ (bed sheets)
- C. 毛布 (blanket)
- D. 枕 (pillow)

**Correct Answer:** タオル (towel)

**Explanation:** タオル is the word for towel — こちらがご用意した〜でございます is the polite way to present any prepared item to a guest.
