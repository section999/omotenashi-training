**確認します** *(kakunin shimasu)*

"確認します" means "I will check." It is used when staff need to verify information or conditions. It reassures guests that action is being taken. Follow-up is important.

---

## Quiz

**すぐに状況を____。
(I will immediately check the situation.)**

- A. 確認します (I will confirm)
- B. 対応します (will respond)
- C. 案内します (I will guide you)
- D. 提供します (will provide)

**Correct Answer:** 確認します (I will confirm)

**Explanation:** すぐに確認します — すぐに (immediately) paired with 確認します reassures the guest that verification is starting now, not later; it buys time professionally while action is being taken.
