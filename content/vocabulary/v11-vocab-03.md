**外線** *(gaisen)*

"外線" refers to calls made outside the hotel or organization. Staff need to access outside lines for guest calls or business purposes. Proper handling ensures security and accurate billing. Guests rely on it to reach external contacts efficiently.

*Example: 外線は0を押してから番号を入力してください。* (Press 0 before dialing an outside line.)

---

## Quiz

**Which refers to calling outside the building?**

- A. 外線 (outside / external line)
- B. 内線 (internal / extension line)
- C. 電話 (telephone)
- D. 回線 (line / circuit)

**Correct Answer:** 外線 (outside / external line)

**Explanation:** 外線 requires pressing 0 (or another prefix) first — always guide guests clearly: 外線をおかけになる場合は、まず0をお押しください (to make an outside call, please press 0 first); skipping this step is one of the most common guest frustrations with hotel phones.
