**下** *(shita)*

"下" means down or below, used for floors, basements, or lower levels. Staff provide directions to restaurants, parking, or rooms below. Clear instructions prevent guests from entering wrong levels.

*Example: 駐車場は地下1階の下にございます。* (The parking lot is below basement level 1.)

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "Where is the parking?"**

- A. 下です (it is down / below)
- B. 上です (it is up / above)
- C. 奥です (it is at the back)
- D. 手前です (it is in front)

**Correct Answer:** 下です (it is down / below)

**Explanation:** 下 (below / down) — for guests unfamiliar with building layouts, 下 combined with a floor reference (地下1階、地下) and an access method (エレベーターで) gives a complete and safe instruction.
