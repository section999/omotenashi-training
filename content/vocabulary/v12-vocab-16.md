## 医者 (いしゃ · isha)

**Meaning:** doctor

医者 refers to a licensed professional who diagnoses and treats illnesses or injuries. Staff may need to contact a doctor for guest emergencies. Proper identification of symptoms ensures the correct specialist is called. Cooperation with medical professionals ensures guest safety and comfort.

**Example:**

病気の場合は医者を手配いたします。

*We will arrange a doctor if you are ill.*

---

## Quiz

**Who should you call when someone is seriously ill?**

- A. 医者 (doctor)
- B. 消防車 (fire truck)
- C. 警察 (police)
- D. 救助 (rescue)

**Correct Answer:** 医者 (doctor)

**Explanation:** 医者 (isha) means doctor — the medical professional to call for illness or injury. 消防車 = fire truck, 警察 = police, 救助 = rescue.
