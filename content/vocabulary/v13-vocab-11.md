## 残業 (ざんぎょう · zangyou)

**Meaning:** overtime

残業 refers to work done beyond regular working hours. Overtime is often necessary to meet deadlines or handle urgent tasks. Proper management ensures employees are compensated and prevents burnout. Monitoring overtime is essential for workplace health and legal compliance.

**Example:**

今日は残業で夜9時まで働きました。

*I worked overtime until 9 PM today.*

---

## Quiz

**Work done beyond regular hours is called:**

- A. 勤務 (work / duty)
- B. 残業 (overtime)
- C. 出勤 (going to work)
- D. 休憩 (break)

**Correct Answer:** 残業 (overtime)

**Explanation:** 残業 (zangyou) means overtime — work performed beyond the standard working hours. 勤務 = duty, 出勤 = going to work, 休憩 = break.
