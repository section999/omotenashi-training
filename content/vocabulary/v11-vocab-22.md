**お問い合わせ** *(otoiawase)*

"お問い合わせ" is used for questions or requests from guests. Staff should respond clearly and politely. It includes calls, emails, or messages. Efficient handling improves guest trust and satisfaction.

*Example: お問い合わせありがとうございます。* (Thank you for your inquiry.)

---

## Quiz

**質問や依頼のことを日本語で____と言います。
(Questions or requests in Japanese are called inquiries.)**

- A. お問い合わせ (inquiry)
- B. ご用件 (your inquiry / business)
- C. ご連絡 (contact / notification (polite))
- D. 伝言 (message / verbal message)

**Correct Answer:** お問い合わせ (inquiry)

**Explanation:** お問い合わせありがとうございます is the standard phone opening line — it immediately signals professionalism and gratitude, setting a warm tone; staff who skip it and go straight to 何ですか sound abrupt and unwelcoming on the phone.
