## 事故 (じこ · jiko)

**Meaning:** accident

事故 refers to an unplanned event resulting in injury, damage, or disruption. Staff must report accidents promptly and assist affected guests. Preventive measures and adherence to safety rules reduce accident occurrence. Documentation ensures accountability and future safety improvements.

**Example:**

ホテル内で事故が発生した場合、フロントにご報告ください。

*Report to the front desk if an accident occurs in the hotel.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** An unintended harmful event is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 事故

**Explanation:** 事故 (jiko) means accident — an unplanned event causing injury, damage, or disruption that must be reported promptly.
