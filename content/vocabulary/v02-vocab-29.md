**受付** *(uketsuke)*

"受付" refers to reception or registration. It can mean both the place and the process. It is often used in formal contexts. It overlaps with front desk functions.

---

## Quiz

**Which is the most polite way to direct a guest to reception?**

- A. 受付こちら (reception is here)
- B. 受付はこちらでございます (the reception is here)
- C. 受付ある (there is a reception)
- D. 受付行く (go to reception)

**Correct Answer:** 受付はこちらでございます (the reception is here)

**Explanation:** 受付はこちらでございます uses the complete polite structure — はこちらでございます is the standard directional phrase in hospitality.
