## 上司 (じょうし · joushi)

**Meaning:** boss / supervisor

上司 is a person who supervises employees and oversees their work. A good supervisor provides guidance, feedback, and ensures tasks are completed according to standards. Effective communication with a supervisor is crucial for workplace harmony. Respecting hierarchical roles is important in Japanese work culture.

**Example:**

上司に報告書を提出しました。

*I submitted the report to my boss.*

## Quiz

**Who supervises employees and gives instructions at work?**

- A. 同僚 (colleague)
- B. 上司 (supervisor / manager)
- C. スタッフ (staff)
- D. 従業員 (employee)

**Correct Answer:** 上司 (supervisor / manager)

**Explanation:** 上司 (joushi) means boss or supervisor — the person who oversees and guides employee performance. 同僚 = colleague, スタッフ = staff, 従業員 = employee.
