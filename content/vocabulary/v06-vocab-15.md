**Wi-Fi** *(waifai)*

"Wi-Fi" is a wireless internet service provided in hotels. Guests often rely on it for communication and work. Staff should explain access clearly. Stable connection is important for satisfaction.

---

## Quiz

**Guest asks: "Do you have Wi-Fi?" Best response?**

- A. Wi-Fiをご利用いただけます (Wi-Fi is available)
- B. ありません (there is not / we don't have)
- C. 知らない (don't know)
- D. 無理 (impossible / not feasible)

**Correct Answer:** Wi-Fiをご利用いただけます (Wi-Fi is available)

**Explanation:** Wi-Fiをご利用いただけます — ご利用いただけます (you are able to use) is the polite way to confirm service availability; it positions access as a privilege prepared for the guest.
