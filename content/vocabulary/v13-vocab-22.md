## 担当 (たんとう · tantou)

**Meaning:** person in charge

担当 is the individual responsible for a particular task or area. Being in charge includes decision-making, coordination, and accountability. Clear assignment prevents confusion and overlaps. Employees should report to their designated担当 for guidance.

**Example:**

彼は経理担当です。

*He is in charge of accounting.*

## Quiz

**Fill in the blank: ___**

**Question:** The person responsible for a specific task is called ____ in Japanese.

**Answer:** 担当

**Explanation:** 担当 (tantou) means person in charge — the individual responsible for a specific task, area, or decision-making function.
