**転送** *(tensou)*

"転送" means redirecting a call to another department or staff. Staff must ensure the correct destination. Guests rely on smooth transfers to avoid repeated explanations. Accurate handling improves service quality.

*Example: 担当部署に転送いたします。* (I will transfer you to the department in charge.)

---

## Quiz

**Which term means "call transfer"?**

- A. 転送 (transfer / forwarding)
- B. 保留 (hold / on hold)
- C. 切断 (disconnection)
- D. 内線 (internal / extension line)

**Correct Answer:** 転送 (transfer / forwarding)

**Explanation:** 転送 best practice is to brief the receiving party first — whenever possible, do a warm transfer (担当者に状況をお伝えしてからお繋ぎします) so the guest does not have to repeat themselves; cold transfers where the caller must re-explain are one of the top complaints in hotel phone service.
