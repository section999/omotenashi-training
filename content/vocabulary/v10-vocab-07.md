**合計** *(goukei)*

"合計" refers to the sum of all charges, including services, taxes, and additional fees. Staff must display this clearly to avoid misunderstandings. Guests rely on this to confirm their payment amount. Correct calculation enhances trust and transparency.

*Example: 合計金額は15,000円です。* (The total amount is 15,000 yen.)

---

## Quiz

**全ての料金を足した金額は____です。
(The sum of all charges is called the total.)**

- A. 合計 (total)
- B. 内訳 (breakdown)
- C. 精算 (settlement / final billing)
- D. 領収書 (receipt)

**Correct Answer:** 合計 (total)

**Explanation:** 合計 is the number the guest sees last before paying — saying 合計〇〇円でございます (the total is〜yen) clearly and slowly, while showing the screen or printout, prevents mishearing and avoids the need to reprocess the transaction.
