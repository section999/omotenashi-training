## 救助 (きゅうじょ · kyuujo)

**Meaning:** rescue

救助 refers to the act of saving people from danger or life-threatening situations. Staff may assist in evacuations, fires, or natural disasters. Coordination with emergency services ensures efficiency. Rapid and calm response reduces risk to life.

**Example:**

山で遭難した人を救助しました。

*We rescued a person stranded in the mountains.*

---

## Quiz

**What is the term for helping people in dangerous situations?**

- A. 救助 (rescue)
- B. 注意 (caution / attention)
- C. 応急処置 (first aid)
- D. 危険 (danger)

**Correct Answer:** 救助 (rescue)

**Explanation:** 救助 (kyuujo) means rescue — saving people from danger. 注意 = caution, 応急処置 = first aid, 危険 = danger.
