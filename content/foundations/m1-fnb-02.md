title: Handling Dietary Requirements
---

Dietary requirements — allergies, intolerances, religious restrictions, personal preferences — must be handled with **absolute care, complete discretion, and genuine warmth**. The guest should never feel that their requirement is an inconvenience. The ideal response communicates that it has been anticipated, planned for, and handled without any need for concern.

The moment a dietary requirement is disclosed — whether at booking, check-in, or at the table — it must be **communicated to the kitchen immediately**. Do not wait until the order is placed. Do not pass a note and assume it will be read. Confirm with the kitchen directly, confirm back to the guest what has been communicated, and ensure the information follows through every course.

## Quiz

**How should dietary needs be handled in luxury hospitality?**

- A. Noted but not prioritised
- B. Handled discreetly with creative alternatives that feel like upgrades
- C. Left to the kitchen to manage alone
- D. Only addressed if the guest reminds staff

**Correct Answer:** Handled discreetly with creative alternatives that feel like upgrades

**Explanation:** Dietary management must be proactive, discreet, and handled with genuine care for the guest's wellbeing.
